Discipline: Quality Management
This discipline includes the processes required to ensure that the project will satisfy the needs for which it was undertaken. It addresses quality from the perspective of both the management of the project and the products and services that are to be produced.
Relationships
Tasks
  • Assess lessons learned and collect reusable assets
  • Capture lessons learned and debrief staff
  • Close-out project
  • Complete project management system implementation
  • Define project management records and project procedures
  • Discuss and agree on corrective actions (IMSP421)
  • Identify deliverables quality requirements
  • Participate in review (IMSP421)
  • Prepare for review (IMSP421)
  • Update work plans and Action Log (IMSP421)
