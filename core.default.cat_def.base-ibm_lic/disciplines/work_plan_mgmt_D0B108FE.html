Discipline: Work Plan Management
This discipline includes the processes that are used to plan all the technical and project management work and the costs of the project over time.
Relationships
Tasks
  • Analyze request for compliance with Agreements (IMSP460)
  • Assign work and update plans (IMSP460)
  • Assign work packages to team members
  • Breakdown project budget into financial measurement baseline
  • Consolidate costs and prepare budget elements
  • Define deliverables components and conduct method adoption workshop
  • Develop startup plan and orient startup staff
  • Finalize POU planned schedule and cost estimates
  • Identify additional cost elements
  • Organize Work Breakdown Structure and determine team structure
  • Reconcile Operational Schedules & Project Management Schedule baseline
  • Reconcile POUs work plans and confirm the overall project schedule
  • Reconcile work plans and confirm overall project plans (next phase)
  • Refine Deliverable Definitions and phase WBS
  • Refine work plans and schedules to an operational level (IMSP391)
  • Review phase estimates and planned resources
