Discipline: Event Management
This discipline includes the processes necessary to collect project issues and monitor their resolution, analyze symptoms that are causing concern about a particular project situation, handle compliance incidents, and handle actions.
Relationships
Tasks
  • Analyze situation root causes and determine actions (IMSP525)
  • Calculate and record total damage (IMSP615)
  • Determine resolution actions (IMSP620)
  • Determine workaround solution (IMSP615)
  • Identify situational factors (IMSP421)
  • Investigate issue and plan for situation analysis (IMSP525)
  • Monitor progress of actions (IMSP605)
  • Monitor progress of issue resolution (IMSP620)
  • Perform initial analysis, accept or escalate (IMSP620)
  • Record and evaluate noncompliance situation (IMSP615)
  • Review Action Log, cancel or launch pending actions (IMSP605)
  • Review effectiveness of actions (IMSP605)
  • Select and implement health improvement actions (IMSP421)
