Discipline: Risk Management
This discinpline includes those processes which identify, analyze, and respond to risk throughout the life of a project.
Relationships
Tasks
  • Assess effectiveness of risk mitigation plan (IMSP421)
  • Document risks associated with the approach (IMSP395)
  • Document risks attached to solution (IMSP395)
  • Evaluate damage and loss to the project (IMSP600)
  • Identify contingency plan and assess workaround actions (P600)
  • Identify new risks and revise risk priorities (IMSP421)
  • Launch risk control actions and update Risk Management Plan (IMSP421)
  • Monitor progress and effectiveness of contingency plan (IMSP600)
  • Prepare initial Risk Management Plan
