Discipline: Change Management
This discipline includes the processes required to control all the unforeseen changes that inevitably arise during the course of a project and may jeopardize cost, revenue, quality, or deadlines. Therefore, the management of change is a critical factor in the success of a project.
Relationships
Tasks
  • Determine impact and potential solutions
  • Obtain formal agreement and schedule Change Orders (IMSP610)
  • Perform initial assessment, reject or accept for analysis (IMSP610)
