Discipline: Human Resource Management
This discipline includes the processes required to coordinate the human resources on a project. Such processes include those needed to plan, obtain, orient, assign, and release staff over the life of the project.
Relationships
Tasks
  • Allocate office and material
  • Anticipate staff release plan
  • Develop orientation material and Team Charter
  • Evaluate and confirm staff
  • Orient staff and assign work packages
  • Reconcile plans versus actual staffing
  • Release staff
  • Validate successful assimilation (internal staff)
