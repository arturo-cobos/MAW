Discipline: Deliverables Management
The scope of this discipline includes managing the life cycle of deliverables from definition through acceptance.
Relationships
Tasks
  • Acceptance or rejection and notify supplier (IMSP540 and IMSP711)
  • Acknowledge receipt of deliverable (IMSP540 and IMSP711)
  • Analyze and respond to sponsor findings (IMSP540)
  • Archive deliverable and return sponsor components (IMSP540)
  • Assess conformance to sponsor acceptance criteria (IMSP540)
  • Assess progress of deliverable completion (IMSP540)
  • Assess project readiness to validate deliverable (IMSP540)
  • Assess readiness of sponsor to receive deliverable (IMSP540)
  • Assess supplier readiness to deliver (IMSP540 and IMSP711)
  • Clarify Deliverable Definitions and quality characteristics
  • Ensure sponsor acceptance activities begin on schedule (IMSP540)
  • Formally document and plan dispositions (IMSP540)
  • Formally notify sponsor of delivery (IMSP540)
  • Initiate and oversee acceptance work (IMSP540 and IMSP711)
  • Negotiate dispositions agreeable to the sponsor (IMSP540)
  • Re-release and obtain formal sponsor acceptance (IMSP540)
  • Understand disagreement on the nature of findings (IMSP540)
