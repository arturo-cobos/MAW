Domain: Enterprise
The Enterprise sub-domain work products provide a framework for both business and IT, which guide investment and design decisions. They specify processes, standards, interfaces, and common services for the deployment and management of IT assets in support of business objectives.

Main Description
These work products are part of the Enterprise Architecture Framework that provides linkage among a business strategy, an IT strategy, and the implementation of IT solutions.  Enterprise Architecture work products address four constituent parts: Business, Data, Application, and Technical Architectures.
