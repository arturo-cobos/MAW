Roadmap: How to Adopt the Understanding the Business Strategy practice
Main Description

Getting Started

Understanding the business strategy is important while outlining and refining the business solution. The activities include understanding business strategy and refining the business objectives. The objective is to be able to link the goals of the current work effort to the business strategy in order to establish the value to be achieved. The tasks involved can generally be performed by whoever is responsible for defining requirements, however complex situations may benefit from involvement of a strategy consultant who is versed in the particular industry in question.
