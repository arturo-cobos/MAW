Task: Assess Customer Wants and Needs
Determine if the solution under consideration will be acceptable from the point of view of the ultimate customer. Note that this customer may be the customer of the solution's sponsor.
Disciplines: Business Requirements
Purpose
Validation of the requirements of the customer set of artifacts exposes the relationship between the client and his customers. This is necessary if the project is to enable the client to deliver more value to his customers, to improve customer satisfaction, to increase share of customer, and to grow revenue and profits.

    • Customer Wants and Needs
      Main Description

      Validate the information in the Customer Wants and Needs work product including consideration of the following:

      • How satisfied the client's customers are with their performance versus the competition
      • What the client's current customers want, need and expect from them.
      • What the client's potential customers want and need to do business with them.
      • The relative importance of each of the client's customer requirements.
      • How groups of buyers (current and potential customers) with similar needs form market segments.
      • How to identify the best market segments to target for entry, exit or investment.
