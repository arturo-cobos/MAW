Task: Validate Business Issues And Goals
Understand the client's motivations and their strategic and tactical responses to current perceived business issues
Disciplines: Business Requirements
Purpose

The reasons for performing this task include:

  • To validate all downstream work products in terms of the reasons that motivate the work
  • To measure the success of the project by evaluating system requirements in terms of their support for the goals of the business
  • To ensure there is agreement among all parties to the engagement (developer, managers and users) about:
    • the purpose of this project in terms of opportunities for the business
    • the motivations that are driving process changes, role definition, and solution initiatives
  • To help manage expectations by zeroing in on goals that this engagement can address.

    Steps
    Review the motivations for the sponsoring organization to undertake this engagement
    Examine the issues facing the business, and the intended strategic and tactical responses
    Confirm the goals, objectives, and measurements
    Create a textual, template-based treatment of problems facing the business and the goals, objectives, and measurements that address them
