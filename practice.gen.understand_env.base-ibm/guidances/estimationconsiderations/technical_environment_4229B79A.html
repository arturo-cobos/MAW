Estimation Considerations: Technical Environment
This guideline provides guidance on how to estimate the time it will take to create the Technical Environment artifact.

Main Description

The time taken to create the Technical Environment artifact could be up to three weeks elapsed depending on a number of factors such the complexity of the enterprise’s technical environment, documents available, organizational complexity, etc.

The amount of time required to create the Technical Environment artifact will vary with the complexity of the enterprise’s environment, documents available, and organizational complexity. For example, with a team of four people, 80 relevant documents, and possibly 15-20 interviews to conduct, an elapsed period of not less than three weeks would be appropriate.

For guidance:

  • Assume interviews conducted in pairs, and allow 3 hours to prepare, conduct, and document each one.
  • If any workshops are necessary, assume half a day for each, with two project people, and a further two hours to document the findings.
  • Allow 2-3 days to prepare the Technical Environment report itself and a further week elapsed time to get the report reviewed and updated to address review comments.
