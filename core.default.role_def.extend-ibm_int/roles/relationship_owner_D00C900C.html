Main Description

The Relationship Owner is responsible for leading the IBM team in activities which build and support our overall relationship with the client and which create and maintain a single account vision and strategy for growth.  These activities for engaging the client develop in the CVM Understand the Client’s Business and Needs Phase and are continuously monitored and strengthened during the engagement lifecycle with the client.
Additionally, the Relationship Owner is responsibility for leading client engagement activities that confirm the value that the client is experiencing in transactions with IBM. These activities occur during the CVM Confirm Client Value and Experience Phase of the engagement lifecycle.   The focus in these activities, whether with an individual client contact or generally with the client team, is on improving our long term relationship, influence and position with the client.
