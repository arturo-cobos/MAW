Role: Opportunity Identifier
This role is responsible for leading the client engagement which result in the noticing and identifying of new opportunities for IBM to add value in a client account.
Role Sets: Adjunct, Marketing
Relationships
Main Description

The Opportunity Identifier [OI]  role is responsible for leading the client engagement activities in the CVM Understand the Client’s Business and Needs Phase which result in the noticing and identifying of new opportunities in a client account.   The OI is responsible for identifying new opportunity areas to help the client achieve success and improved shareholder value.  The identification of new opportunities enables IBM to grow our involvement and relationship collateral with the account, as well as revenue generated from the account.

Staffing
Skills
  • Apply industry and business acumen
  • Build relationship with the client
  • Execute the IBM Sales Call Model (questioning techniques)
  • Confirm the client’s compelling reason to act
  • Identify the client’s Buying Behavior and key expectations
  • Validate an opportunity
  • Apply IBM solution knowledge and expertise
Key Considerations
May be performed as a primary or secondary role.
