Task: Define Usability Design and Evaluation Plan
Gain agreement between the client and the project team on the relative importance of usability for this system in general and with regard to other priorities
Disciplines: Technical Design, User Experience
Purpose
It provides direct user evaluation of solution design and development to ensure usability requirements are met. Furthermore, it provides a basis to estimate the impact of usability issues on the project cost, duration, required skills, and resources.
