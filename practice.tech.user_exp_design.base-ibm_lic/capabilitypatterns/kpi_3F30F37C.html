Work Product Descriptor (Artifact): Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Key Performance Indicators are identified metrics used to determine and evaluate performance. KPI's provide a means for assessing a client's performance relative to its industry peers.

Main Description
A measure of performance specifically related to a Critical Success Factor or Business Goal.  It is one of a limited number of measures used by management to steer and improve operations. This term is also used in the context of the Balanced ScoreCard.
