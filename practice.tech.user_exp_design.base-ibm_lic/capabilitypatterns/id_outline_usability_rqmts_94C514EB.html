Main Description
Identify and validate the key the usability requirements needed to ensure the users will accept the system. These requirements should be documented as key attributes required in terms of usability by conducting requirements gathering sessions, surveys and observations. The capabilities of the existing solution should also be kept in mind when determining what is required to promote end user acceptance of the new solution. At this stage of the process, these requirements do not need to be defined at a testable or measurable level, but they may be if the information is known. All requirements should be justified in terms of the strategic needs of the business and the overall goals defined for the project.
