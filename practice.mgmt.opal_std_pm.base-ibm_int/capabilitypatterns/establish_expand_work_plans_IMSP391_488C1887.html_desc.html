Activity: Establish or expand work plans (IMSP391)
This activity will confirm the validity of the planning prerequisites, and expand the project's plans and schedules to bring them to an operational level of detail.
DescriptionWork Breakdown StructureTeam AllocationWork Product Usage
Purpose
To bring the project's plans and schedules to a level of detail appropriate to allocate work packages to team members, assess the estimates, and track progress.
Relationships
Parent Activities
  • common_activities
Description

This activity includes the following:

  • Confirm the validity of the planning prerequisites
  • Refine work plans and schedules to an operational level
  • Update other project plans
  • Expand delivery process plans
  • Describe how to monitor costs against budget
  • Validate technical solution
