Activity: Receive deliverable (IMSP540 and IMSP711)
This activity will verify that the deliverable is ready to be delivered from a supplier and will oversee the acceptance of the deliverable to the project, including determining if the deliverable meet the acceptance criteria, and formally notifying the supplier of the acceptance or rejection.
DescriptionWork Breakdown StructureTeam AllocationWork Product Usage
Purpose
To ensure the successful receipt and formal acceptance of a deliverable from a supplier, with the delivery occurring on time and with the expected content.
Relationships
Parent Activities
  • common_activities
Description

This activity is based on the WWPMM work pattern HD1: Prepare to receive deliverable.

To make sure the delivery will occur on time and with the expected content, the progress of deliverable completion must be monitored closely. Work may be reassigned, if necessary. Sponsor readiness to receive the deliverable must also be assessed to ensure it will not negatively impact the project plans.

Formal deliverable reviews are usually performed before delivery to ensure that no open items remain and that it is ready to be delivered. This is normally defined in the business process of the delivery organization and is required by the international standards for quality assurance. If necessary, assure that the any rework needed is compliant with the time constraints.

This activity includes the following:

  • Prepare to receive deliverable
    • Assess supplier readiness to deliver
    • Assess project readiness to validate deliverable
  • Accept deliverables
    • Acknowledge receipt of deliverable
    • Initiate and oversee acceptance work
    • Determine acceptance or rejection and formally notify supplier
