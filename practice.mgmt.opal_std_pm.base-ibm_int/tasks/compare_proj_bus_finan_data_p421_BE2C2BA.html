Task: Compare project and business financial data (IMSP421)
This task compares project and business system financial data, in order to be able to correct discrepancies, and update forecasts for future tracking periods.
Purpose
To have the financial information to be able to correct discrepancies and assess forecasts for future tracking periods. To periodically compare project related financial data from business systems, and project internal costs and expense accounting, in order to reconcile the financial data, correct discrepancies, and assess forecasts for future tracking periods.  Also take actions to resolve financial discrepancies and maintain forecasts in line with the project financial measurement baseline (FMB), update the Financial Plan, and create issues or actions as necessary.

        Main Description

        Reference IGSDF - Using GBS CRM on Engagements for links to your local GBS CRM Playbook for specific guidance on CRM Step 8. Execute Project for tools for business unit financial and reporting schedule, requirements and tools.

        The key outputs of this task are the Financial Plan (which may be part of the Project Management System Summary); the Issue Document and/or Issue Log, as needed; and the Action Control Document and/or Action Log, as needed.
        This task includes the following:

        • Compare the project and business system financial data
        • Reconcile the business system financial data with the financial plan and the expenditure log
        • Document the discrepancies so they can be tracked for resolution
        Steps
        Conduct project financial processing

        Claim & Expense Management

        These tasks should be performed on a weekly basis to ensure accurate costs and revenue for both time and expenses.

        1. Verify/approve correct coding of labor
        2. Verify/approve correct coding of expenses
        3. Verify amounts remaining in bill codes
        4. Reswizzle hours
        5. Submit travel corrections, unbilled, Relocation Expense Account and international travel to financial tool
        Monthly Invoicing

        IBM invoicing generally occurs monthly, but can occur on a different schedule as required by the client SOW in the Agreement.   It is imperative to ensure accurate and timely client invoicing and receipt of payment.

        1. Review the billing report and invoices from the financial tool
        2. Prepare manual invoices/adjustments to invoices by working with appropriate financial contacts
        3. Review / approve invoice
        4. Invoice sent / delivered to client
        5. Resolve billing disputes
        6. Work with appropriate financial contacts to collect monies due and cash application
