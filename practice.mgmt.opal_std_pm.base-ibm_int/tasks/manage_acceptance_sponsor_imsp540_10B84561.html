Task: Manage acceptance by sponsor (IMSP540)
This task will monitor the sponsor's acceptance of deliverables, and will analyze and respond to any findings by the sponsor, such as controversial defects.
Purpose
To ensure the formal successful acceptance of a deliverable, including addressing any of the sponsor's findings.

        Main Description

        This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern HD4: Manage acceptance.

        This task is launched each time a deliverable has been released to the sponsor and the sponsor's acceptance must be obtained. It is important to monitor the sponsor's (or his representative's) validation of the deliverable to be sure that progress is being made and that any rework of deliverables can be done promptly. This will facilitate obtaining the sponsor's acceptance as soon as possible.

        Ensure sponsor acceptance activities begin as scheduled and committed in the Agreement. Ensure that all sponsor findings are being researched and resolved. Determine if corrective actions or issues should be raised.

        The key outputs of this task are the Delivery Control Documentation (to the sponsor), the Findings Log (replies and decisions), and the Operational Schedule.   Small or less complex project may choose to use the Event Log to record Findings and associated Issues.

        The task includes the following:

        • Monitor sponsor's validation process to ensure acceptance on time
        • Analyze and respond to sponsor findings
        • Re-release and obtain formal sponsor acceptance
        • Archive deliverable and return sponsor components
        • Initiate invoicing for delivery acceptance (if delivery is a payment milestone)
