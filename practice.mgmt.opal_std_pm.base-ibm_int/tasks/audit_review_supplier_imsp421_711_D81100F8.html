Task: Audit or review supplier (IMSP421 and IMSP711)
This task conducts an examination of certain aspects of supplier's activities to assess the quality of their work and the progress they are making against the plan.
Purpose
To assess the quality and progress of a supplier's work in order to ensure the health of the project.

      Main Description

      This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern M4: Audit or review subcontractor.

      This task is launched at a previously planned point in time (such as the beginning of a phase), or when there is a reason to be concerned about a supplier.
      In accordance with the supplier Agreement or in an effort to prevent a concern about the supplier from developing into a more serious situation, a decision is made to review their activities. The review may focus on their compliance with policies, procedures, and standards defined in the supplier's Project Management System Summary, Project Quality Plan and Project Procedures Description. It may also focus on the health of the supplier's project both from a technical and management perspective.

      The audit or review may be performed by project management or by a third party appointed by project management.

      The key outputs of this task are the Quality Review Documentation, and the Action Control Document and/or Action Log, as needed.

      This task includes the following:

      • Plan and inform suppliers of audit or review
      • Perform review and agree on corrective actions
      • Check effectiveness of corrective actions
