Task: Manage progress and effectiveness of actions (IMSP605)
This task will monitor the resolution and effectiveness of actions taken.
Purpose
To periodically review pending actions and monitor their progress, including adjusting the Operational Schedule for actions involving a significant workload.  Also, review the effectiveness of the actions as appropriate before closing them.
Relationships

      Outputs

        Main Description

        This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern HE2: Handle actions and is launched either daily or, at the least, at short time intervals in order to launch, review, revise or close actions. It is part of the Handling exceptions activities.

        The key outputs of this task are the Action Log or the PM Event Log.

        The task includes the following:

        • Review Action Log, cancel or launch pending actions
        • Monitor progress of actions and ensure that actions are progressing as expected
        • Review results of actions and ensure they have been effective
