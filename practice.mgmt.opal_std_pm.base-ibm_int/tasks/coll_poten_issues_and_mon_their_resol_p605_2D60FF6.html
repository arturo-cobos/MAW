Task: Collect potential issues and monitor their resolution (IMSP605)
This task will resolve exceptions, such as actions, issues, situation/root cause analyses and compliance incidents that will occur during the life of the project. By definition, these activities respond to unplanned project events and therefore cannot be scheduled in advance.
Purpose
To resolve the different types of exceptions or other unplanned events that may occur during the life of a project and address these various types of events in order to keep the project under control.

      Main Description

      Handle all types of exception events that occur during the life of a project. Address these various types of events:

      • Handle issues
        • Identify and record new issues, analyze them, determine how to resolve them and monitor their resolution.
        • Ensure that each pending issue has an appropriate action to resolve the issue.
        • Monitor progress of issue resolution and ensure that issues are being resolved in a timely manner.
        • Monitor the progress and effectiveness of contingency plans.
      • React to risk occurrence
        • Evaluate the impact of a risk occurrence and understand the impact to the project.
        • Identify how to react to a risk occurrence, either using existing contingency plan (if valid) or other actions such as using risk reserves.
        • Monitor the progress and effectiveness of contingency plans.
      • Handle compliance incident
        • Identify compliance incidents in which the sponsor or a supplier is not meeting an obligation under the agreement
        • Record and evaluate the details associated with a compliance incident and notify the sponsor or supplier.
        • Determine appropriate actions that will allow work to continue until the compliance incident is resolved.  Address the impact to work plans.
      • Handle controversial findings/defect
        • Understand disagreement on the nature of the findings
        • Negotiate dispositions agreeable to the sponsor
        • Formally document and plan dispositions
      • Analyze situation root causes
        • Analyze underlying causes of situations that are causing concern, to resolve them and prevent their recurrence

      The key outputs of this task are the Action Log, Issue Log (which can be documented in the Event Log for smaller projects), and appropriate documents depending on the type of issue being processed (Compliance Incident document, Change Request, Situation Analysis Documentation or Root Cause Analysis Record), plus Agreement amendments or updates of Operational Schedules when necessary.
