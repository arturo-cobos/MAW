Task: Schedule and conduct meeting (IMSP421)
This task will plan and conduct effective verbal exchanges of project related information, disseminate information internally, gather information from team members, collect new issues, and determine appropriate actions.
Purpose
To ensure effective communication across the project team, gathering and dispersing information, so the team is informed, appropriate actions can be taken as needed and accurate project status can be reported.

      Main Description

      The key outputs of this task are Meeting Documentation; Action Control Document and Action Control Log, or PM Event Log, if necessary.

      This task includes the following:

      • Schedule meeting, prepare and distribute agenda
      • Conduct meeting and distribute meeting notes
