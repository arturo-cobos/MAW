Artifact: Independent Review Report (ART 0540)
This artifact documents the result of formally reviewing a Project estimate.
Purpose

This artifact is itself validating and verifying that the estimate has been created to the best of our ability and is fit for purpose, that is, is sufficiently detailed and accurate for further estimating work to be performed, following the estimating process.  Ultimately this estimate is used as the basis for the final estimate to be priced.

It is used to indicate whether the next stage of the governance process can be performed

Description
Brief Outline

This artifact records:

  • Project name, review date and Estimation Report under review
  • The responsible parties for the estimating activities
  • Confirmation of the completion of estimation activities, and associated follow-on actions as necessary
  • Any deviations from the estimating procedure or quality
  • A hardcopy signature by the responsible nominee to indicate the approval status of the estimate

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not having this work product are:

  • Lack of clarity about where an estimate currently sits within the estimating process
  • Absence of documented estimating responsibilities that may lead to reduced estimate accuracy and missed estimate deadlines
  • Absence of documented estimating activity that may lead to reduced estimate accuracy
  • Lack of a documented baseline against which the estimate can be developed further
  • Lack of a complete set of Estimating Governance reports against which other estimates may be compared for estimating improvement purposes
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is used in documenting the results of reviewing the estimate during the Proposed or Indicative stage in its progress. It is especially highly recommended for Proposed estimates.  Reasons for not using this artifact should be documented and approved by management.
