Artifact: Confirm Project Data is Appropriate Review Report (ART 0539)
This artifact documents the result of formally reviewing a Project’s final metrics – actual effort, cost and schedule – for completeness, accuracy and quality.
Purpose
This artifact is itself validating and verifying that the estimate and actual project results (effort, cost and schedule figures for the entire project and for the estimation factors) have been collected to the best of our ability and are fit for purpose, that is, are complete, accurate and of adequate quality to properly reflect what actually happened during the project.

Description
Brief Outline

This artifact records:

  • Project name, Estimation Report, Estimating Considerations Checklist, Estimation Factors Report, all the Evidence of Governance Reports under review, and the review date
  • The responsible parties for the estimating activities 
  • Confirmation of the completion of estimation activities, and associated follow-on actions as necessary
  • Any deviations from the estimating procedure or quality
  • A hardcopy signature by the Independent Subject Matter Expert (Estimating) to indicate the approval status of the estimate

Key Considerations
This review is important in ensuring that the right data is collected from finishing a project, in order that the results achieved may be compared to future projects that require estimating, to improve the estimate.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not having this work product are:

  • Lack of clarity about where an estimate currently sits within the estimating process
  • Incomplete or inaccurate estimation and actual project result records
  • Lack of a complete set of Estimating Governance reports against which other estimates may be compared for estimating improvement purposes
  • Lack of ability to identify when generally accepted best practices are not being followed, and the reasons for not following them
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is highly recommended for Closed estimates. Reasons for not using this artifact should be documented and approved by management.
