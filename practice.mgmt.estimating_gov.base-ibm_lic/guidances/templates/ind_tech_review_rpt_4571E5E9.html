Main Description

Read the instructions on the first worksheet and fill in the second worksheet with reference to the instructions. 

This involves filling in the project name, filename of the associated estimate (documented in the Estimation Report) and the date of the review.  The names of those involved in the estimate are then typed over their associated roles, and this populates the roles in the rest of the worksheet with the names.

There is a checklist for the reviewer to complete, with space for notes, and guidance on who should have done what, and there is a final approval section, with or without conditions.  The worksheet is then printed and the approver signs the report which is then filed.

In accordance with the Estimating Process, the Estimation Report and its associated files are baselined if the estimate is approved at the Independent Technical Review.
