Main Description

Read the instructions on the first worksheet and fill in the second worksheet with reference to the instructions. 

Completion of the second worksheet involves filling in the project name, filename of the associated estimate (the estimate is documented in the Estimation Report), the date of the review and the names associated with the estimating roles.

There is a checklist for the reviewer to complete, with space for notes, and guidance on who should have performed which action.  This is followed by a final approval section, with or without conditions.  The worksheet is then printed and the approver signs the report which is then filed.
