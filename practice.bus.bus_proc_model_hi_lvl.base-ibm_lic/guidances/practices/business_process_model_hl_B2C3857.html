Practice: Business Process Modeling (High Level)
This practice provides a process centric approach to business design and subsequent solution design. This practice analyzes business processes and sub-processes to a level of detail where candidate services, service dependencies and opportunities for service flows and composition can be identified.

Purpose

To provide a way to perform a quick pass of Process Modeling and Decomposition such that we can understand the breadth and depth of the scope of the business design and subsequent solution definition in terms of business processes. 

Main Description
This practice provides high level pass of Process Modeling and Decomposition to start to identify the business processes, to develop current state and future state process maps, and to decompose the processes to identify candidate services based on the future state business processes.
