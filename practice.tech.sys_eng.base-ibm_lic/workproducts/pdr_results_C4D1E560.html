Artifact: Preliminary Design Review Results (ENG 422)
The Preliminary Design Review Results work product documents the results of the Preliminary Design Review (PDR), which is used to baseline the system design and requirements at the component level. The PDR is held to develop a component baseline.
Domains: Quality Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose
  • To quantitatively assess the Preliminary Design Review (PDR)
  • To provide a framework for identifying, managing, and reporting defects identified in the review
  • To provide process evidence that the review has been conducted and communicate results

Impact of not having

Preliminary Design Review (PDR) cannot be conducted.  Design baseline cannot be defined and agreed to by both the customer and the project team.  No evidence of process conformance. Results of the Preliminary Design Review (defects, issues, actions, and changes) are not recorded.

Reasons for not needing

Must have the work product if the review is held; otherwise, the work product is not necessary.
