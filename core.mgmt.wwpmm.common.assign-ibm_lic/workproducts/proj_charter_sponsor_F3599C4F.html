Main Description
The Project Charter formalizes the request from a sponsor for devising a project to respond to a business need.
 
The Project Charter is usually a short document that refers to some other more detailed documents such as a "new offering request" or a "request for proposal", and supporting statements of requirements. It may also be used to support a request for additional scope within a program.
 
Notice that the Project Charter is a special work product that is created by the sponsor, and may have various formats or names: from a verbal business need to a detailed Request for Proposal.
