Main Description

A preliminary Communication Management Plan is built during project Defining and Planning activities. The plan is then expanded at the start of execution and maintained or adjusted throughout the life of the project. The Communications Management Plan defines the regular meetings, reporting, and other communications that take place during the project. It identifies the type of information, the medium for communicating, and the audience to whom it is directed. The Communications Managment Plan may be incorporated into the Project Management System Summary (PMSS) or referenced there and maintained as a separate stand-alone document.
