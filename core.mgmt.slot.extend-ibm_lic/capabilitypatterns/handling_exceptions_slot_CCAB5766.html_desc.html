Capability Pattern: Handling Exceptions
The activities in this group are intended to resolve exceptions that will occur during the life of the project. By definition, these activities respond to unplanned project events and therefore cannot be scheduled in advance.
DescriptionWork Breakdown StructureTeam AllocationWork Product Usage
Purpose

To resolve the different types of exceptions or other unplanned events that may occur during the life of a project and address these various types of events in order to keep the project under control.

Description

Collect project issues and monitor their resolution, analyze symptoms that are causing concern about a particular project situation, handle compliance incidents, manage the completion of action, respond to risk occurrences and manage unforseen changes.

The key outputs of this activity group are the Issue Documents, Risk Occurrence Document, Compliance Incident Document, Change Request, Action Control Document and their related logs plus any necessary updates to project schedules and plans.
