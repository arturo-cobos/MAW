Task Descriptor: Train the UAT Testers
Delivery sufficient training to the UAT testers so that they are able to properly conduct testing.

Main Description

Conduct training for the UAT testers, using material either developed specifically for this project, or 'vanilla' training materials associated with a solution package. The training for these testers should be conducted as close to the actual UAT activity as possible in order to enhance their ability to retain the information.

The output of this task, Education and Training Evaluation, can be used to capture feedback on the training delivery experience by the UAT testers for potential improvement to the training materials. If these materials are not within the scope of work of the project, then this output may not be necessary and a simple log of training delivery would suffice to record the activity.
