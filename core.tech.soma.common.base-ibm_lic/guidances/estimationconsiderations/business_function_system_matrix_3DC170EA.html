Estimation Considerations: Business Function / System Matrix
Estimation guidance for Business Function / System Matrix development based on a low-high range of hours for key activities.

Main Description

For each business application to be analyzed, assume the following allocation of hours:

Low High
Prepare for interview or work sessions 1 4
Interviews or facilitated work sessions 7 24
Documenting 2 8
Validation and final revisions 2 4
Total 12 40


The duration and the amount of effort required to develop the Functionality Matrix will vary depending on:

  • Complexity of system being analyzed
  • Scope of analysis (does the entire system need to be analyzed, or a subset of its functions)
  • Quality and completeness of the input work products (inputs should be available to define the business processes and business functional areas that are in-scope for the analysis activity, along with functional and technical documentation for the system that is being analyzed).
  • Experience of team (in technique and industry, and considering the capabilities and knowledge of customer SMEs that will attend work sessions or interviews)

Where all the necessary input is available, (particularly the activity and information models and the usage matrix), then the development of the model will require approximately 5-15 days effort from the consultant responsible for the work product.
