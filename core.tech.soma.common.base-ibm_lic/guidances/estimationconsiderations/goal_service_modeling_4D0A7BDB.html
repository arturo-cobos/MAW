Estimation Considerations: Goal Service Modeling
Low-high range estimation guidance for Goal Service Modeling based on the number of business domains and other factors to be analyzed.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Goal Service Model
Main Description

Plan for one iteration of Goal-Service Modeling for each business domain that falls within the scope of the project. The level of effort for each iteration of the Goal-Service Model can be expected to fall in the range of 12 to 40 hours. This will complete the Goal-Service Model to the first elaboration.

For each business domain, in order to complete the Goal-Service Model to the first level of elaboration, estimate:


Low High
Prepare for interview or work session 2 6
Interviews or facilitated work sessions 4 16
Goal Service Model refactoring and refinement 2 6
Validation and final revisions 4 12
Total 12 40


Note: the second elaboration of the Goal-Service Model may lead to minor updates to the Goal-Service Model as a result of the validation and completion of the mapping of goals to services. This can potentially add a small number of additional goals or sub-goals to address gaps found during the mapping process. Effort for the second elaboration is considered to be part of adding Service Exposure Decisions to the Sernive Model artifact.

The duration and the amount of effort required to develop the Goal-Service Model will vary depending on: Level of effort is dependant on the following factors:

  • Complexity of goals for the business domain 
  • Number of functional areas within the business domain
  • Quality and completeness of the input work products (i.e. list and description of high level goals)
  • Experience of team (in technique and industry)
