      Main Description
      After carrying out a fit-gap analysis, document the results, apply final refinements to issues and recommendations, review with stakeholders as necessary, and make sure they are "customer-ready".
      Steps
      Analyze the results of the fit gap analysis done during leverage existing assets
      Rationalize whether there is a cost benefit for building a new asset
      Rationalize whether there is a cost benefit for building a new asset for future reuse; there may not be a business case for a reuse case, and a one off may be sufficient.
      Provide a justification for why the asset should be built or not
      Recommend a business case be constructed for funding purposes
