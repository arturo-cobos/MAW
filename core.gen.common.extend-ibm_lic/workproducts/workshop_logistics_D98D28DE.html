Artifact: Workshop Logistics (ART 0609)
The complete set of arrangements required to conduct a workshop - location, date and time, participants, objectives and agenda, travel and lodging information (if required).
Domains: Management
Work Product Kinds: Meeting, Plan
Purpose

The purpose of this artifact is to ensure that all of the arrangements needed to get the optimal participation of client staff in a workshop have been made.

Description
Main Description

The Workshop Logistics consists of establishing the content and approach most appropriate for the purposes of the particular workshop addressed. The types of workshops that may be addressed will vary greatly over the breadth of project types and client requirements.

Brief Outline

When this artifact has been completed, the following types of information should have been developed for the workshop in question:

  • The format of the information/topics that will be discussed during the workshop, including order of execution and timeframes
  • How topics will be delivered and the method in which team members should participate (i.e., anonymous voting, discussion, verbal voting, etc.)
  • Any scoring or voting criteria that will be used and the scale of that criteria (i.e., numerical scoring from 1 to 10 with 1 being worst and 10 being best)
  • Roles and responsibilities of participants, and estimated time frames. Planned time frames should be reviewed with session participants and monitored, to facilitate gaining group consensus with these time frames, and/or making the appropriate adjustment to schedule and commitment.
  • An example of the results of the workshop and how they will be used in the remainder of the project to ensure the relevancy of the effort and establish priority next steps.
