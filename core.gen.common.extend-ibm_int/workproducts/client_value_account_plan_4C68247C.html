Artifact: Client Value Account Plan (ART 0614)
This artifact documents a single account vision, centered on the client's initiatives, issues, strategies, and priorities, constructed by the extended team (S&D, Brands and Services) supporting the account.
Domains: Sales
Work Product Kinds: Plan
Purpose

It is used internally to guide the IBM team to think strategically about the client's business, the new opportunity areas, and team plan to grow the account and the overall relationship.

The client validated business initiatives, which form the foundation for account planning, influence the direction and outcome of planning. As a result, resources are more knowledgeable of the client’s business and agenda and the planned actions are more tuned to what the client values and needs.

This plan also provides a reference for each team member to understand their role and contributions within the overall approach and strategy.

Key Considerations

Focused on the client’s domestic and international (if applicable) priorities, this artifact is jointly constructed by account team members from IBM Sales and Distribution (S&D), the IBM Brands, IBM Global Financing, and IBM Global Services. Extended team members for international accounts may include remote client team members representing the largest revenue producing countries or those with the greatest potential for growth.

Building this plan is an extended team activity (Sales, GBS, GTS and Brands) in which one account planning method is used and one account plan per account is developed for overall team execution.

A light version of this artifact can be produced for any team that wants a simplified process.
