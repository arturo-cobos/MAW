Artifact: Value Proposition (ART 0615)
This artifact consolidates and summarizes the business value the client can expect from implementing Team IBM’s recommended solution.
Domains: Sales
Work Product Kinds: Plan
Purpose

It facilitates and confirms the client's and IBM’s understanding of the complete business case by guiding a focused conversation about the business value of the solution from the perspective of key client stakeholders who either benefit or must adopt the solution.

This artifact structures a concise means of describing the business value to the client of IBM’s solution, and reflects client-specific categories of investment and results. The Win Theme(s) highlighted will be crucial to your proposal strategy and creation.  In use with the client, it arms your client’s Decision Leader with a compelling justification for taking action and buying the proposed solution, by enabling the client to track actual vs. expected results to assess the value realized and return on investment

Value propositions based on IBM unique value acknowledged by the client help us win against competition.

Description
Main Description

Describes the solution and quantifies the business value acknowledged by the client for the IBM solution being proposed. Focused on building the client's commitment to implement the solution(s), this artifact highlights and differentiates how our solution addresses the client's stated problem and value drivers to achieve the desired business capabilities and benefit. Update this work product throughout the CVM phases to capture the client’s evolving acknowledgement and expectation of value from the emerging solution.

Key Considerations

A strong value proposition is needed to impact all client buying decision occasions but the Buying Behavior will influence the detail of the content included in of the value proposition.

The Justification template portrays the client’s investment and results over time; the Value Capture template portrays actual versus expected results.  Use the client’s financial measures to present the investment and results information in both the Business Justification and Value Capture sections.
