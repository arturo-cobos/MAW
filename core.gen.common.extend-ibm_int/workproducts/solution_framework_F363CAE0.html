Artifact: Solution Framework (ART 0613)
This artifact provides a structure for developing a comprehensive solution with the client and Team IBM. It aligns the sales and delivery teams to define, propose and deliver what the client needs, values and expects.
Domains: Sales
Work Product Kinds: Requirement
Purpose

Its purpose is to enable the team to discuss and develop a solution mapped to the client's business capabilities and preferences. It provides a comprehensive view of all aspects of the solution and the range of options to consider, emphasizing IBM services for strategy, business design, and solution deployment, and tests client receptivity to IBM solution components before developing a detailed solution recommendation.

It also provides insight into resources needed as part of the transition team for the solution.

Key Considerations
  • Discuss the total solution with the client, even if the client is not acquiring all solution components from IBM - in order to provide context for integration into their IT environment.
  • Use this artifact to better understand a competitor’s solution so that you may better describe IBM’s unique business value.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without this artifact:

  • it may be difficult to have a focused conversation with the client about the proposed solution components in order to gain their acceptance
  • needed resources may not be identified
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is always needed. The degree of formality in the documentation is based on the needs of the opportunity.
