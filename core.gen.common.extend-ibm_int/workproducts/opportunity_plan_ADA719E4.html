Artifact: Opportunity Plan (ART 0612)
This artifact is a multifunction planning resource that documents critical facts about an opportunity.
Domains: Sales
Work Product Kinds: Plan
Purpose

It enables the client relationship team to assess the opportunity, identify gaps in the sales strategy, and develop actions to close the gaps and execute the sales strategy.

It  aligns the IBM sales and delivery teams to define, propose, and deliver what the client needs, values, and expects based on insight gathered about the client opportunity using the Difference Makers.

This artifact, when well maintained and accessible to everyone on the team, helps our team act as one team, with one set of messages, one focus, one coordinated set of strategic and tactical approaches â€“ so that we function and support them as one coordinated team focused on the client's agenda.

It enables the team to remain updated on progress made and new actions necessary differentiate ourselves from the competition.

Description
Main DescriptionThe Opportunity Plan is the planning tool used by all teams to create and deliver value expected by the client in solving a business problem.  The Opportunity Plan is created in CVM Phase UNDERSTAND following the Account Planning for opportunities that are identified. It includes the Buying Behavior Planner, Opportunity Assessment and Opportunity Win Plan (action plan.) The Opportunity Plan uses the CVM Difference Makers to help the IBM Team create client value in each phase of CVM. The Opportunity Plan is verified and updated throughout the life-cycle of the opportunity. Use it internally with Team IBM and business partners.
Brief Outline
  • Opportunity Information - pertinent information about the client and the specific opportunity
  • Buying Behavior Planner - buying behavior profile most appropriate for this client, based on their value drivers and unique expectations that will affect their buying decision and satisfaction with the vendor and solution selected.
  • Opportunity Assessment - evaluation of client value being created and delivered during the end-to-end client experience
  • Opportunity Action Plan (Win Plan) - description of the opportunity strategy and team actions to win the deal and delivery the value expected by the client.

Key Considerations

Each client relationship team participant should prepare to share their insight about the opportunity and their areas of expertise with other members of the team in order to optimize the team's response to the client expectations of value.

This artifact is intended to be used internally, not with the client.

Update the opportunity assessment and plan early and often throughout  the opportunity lifecycle to:

  • Document critical facts about the opportunity
  • Assess the opportunity and find gaps in the sales strategy
  • Identify actions to close these gaps and execute the sales strategy
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without this artifact, a clean handoff between sales and delivery may not take place. Without up to date information about the opportunity documented and available to every memeber of the IBM team, we may send inconsistent messages to the client.

Reasons for not needingThere is never a reason not to produce this artifact. Each section of the plan can be elaborated as necessary according to the needs of the team.
Representation OptionsThis artifact is captured in a document. The template can be generated from CRM Siebel, updated outside of that tool, and then attached to the account.
