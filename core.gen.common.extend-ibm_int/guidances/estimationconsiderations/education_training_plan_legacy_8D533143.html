Main Description

Education and training is delivered just in time.  The number of classrooms, instructors, end users, and a variety of other constraints must be taken into consideration when determining how many weeks prior to change implementation training must occur.  For estimation purposes, training is typically delivered 4-6 weeks prior to cut-over.

The initial development plan will typically take 5-10 workdays to produce for an average sized implementation.  Approximately 1-2 days will be required per month to update the development plan with the latest information.
