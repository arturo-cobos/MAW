Main Description

The final Deployment plan will probably take two to three weeks to assemble. The actual effort involved may be from 20 to 40 hours depending on the size of the implementation project and the complexity of the deployment. There will be considerable coordination between the IBM Project Manager, the Client Project Manager, the System Architect, and the IT Infrastructure people on the best method to proceed.   

Multi-site deployments will be the most complex requiring the coordination of the following items on a location basis:

  • The sequence of sites for deployment.
  • The scheduling of client personnel, facilities and instructors for site training.
  • Legacy system integration and removal planning.
  • Hardware, site-specific software, and support personnel considerations.
