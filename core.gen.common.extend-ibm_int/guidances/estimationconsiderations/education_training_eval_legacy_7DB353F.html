Main Description
Training is delivered just in time. The number of classrooms, instructors, end users, and a variety of other constraints must be taken into consideration when determining how many weeks prior to "Go Live" training must occur. For estimation purposes, training is typically delivered 4-6 weeks prior to implementation.
