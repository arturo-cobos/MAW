Main Description

The complexity of this work product will depend on the scope of the effort. Complicating factors include (but are not limited to) the amount of content, content stability, access to SMEs, number of changes that have been requested by the client, and expected size of the learning solution. Time required to complete, review, and approve Learning Solution Design will depend on the presence of all these factors.   

Range of effort:   

Plan on at least two days for planning and consulting with Development Team and Support Team members, and at least one day to capture the Learning Solution Design. This estimate may increase given the size and complexity of the solution, as well as the number of approval steps needed.
