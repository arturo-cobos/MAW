Task: Provide Warranty Support
The team monitors the system, tracks defects, and works with the maintenance team to resolve any identified defects.
Purpose
The project team typically provides some level of warranty support, as the application is passed to the maintenance team. 
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
  • Application Architect
  • Application Developer
  • Business Analyst
  • Stakeholder
  • Test Specialist
Outputs
  • Test Log
    Main Description

    This task is further defined by the contract or governance process in place, but for the defined period, the project team, or a small part of the team typically remains partially engaged to support questions and answers of the maintenance team, assists them in categorizing any issues that are identified, and may assist them in resolving any issues categorized as post-production defects.

    This information may be summarized in the Project Completion Report.
