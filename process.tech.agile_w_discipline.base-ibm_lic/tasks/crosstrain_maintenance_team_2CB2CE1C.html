Task: Crosstrain the Maintenance Team
The team works with the Maintenance team to finalize the turn over of knowledge and project documentation.
Purpose
Cross training with the maintenance or sustain teams allows the trained team members the ability to do the same work as the development team. This is good for the managers and the client. It is also good for the teams because it will improve new skills and can present new challenges to maintenance team members.
