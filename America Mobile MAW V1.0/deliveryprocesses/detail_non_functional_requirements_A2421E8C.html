Usage Guidance
Any Non-functional Requirements identified during Solution Development, as specified in the TeamSD method, should be carried forward as an input to this task. Also, any requirements that may be specified in the Requirements Matrix developed during TeamSD should also be examined for applicability to the NFRs in this engagement.
Key Considerations
At this point in the life cycle we are only concerned with identifying those non-functional requirements which would be exceptional and require special attention during the architecture outline activity.
