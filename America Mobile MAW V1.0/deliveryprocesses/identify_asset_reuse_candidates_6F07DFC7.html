Task Descriptor: Identify Asset Reuse Candidates (Asset Production)
Identify potential assets that may be generated from this project
Based on Method Task: Identify Asset Reuse Candidates (Asset Production)

  • None
Outputs
  • Candidate Asset List
Steps
Understand what potential assets may be required or useful in the future
  • Understand the requirements of the business environment for which assets are being considered.
  • Understand the need for component assets which function in a particular operational environment.
  • Understand the broad range of functionality required of reusable assets.
Identify project assets which match these characteristics
List these candidates on the Asset Harvesting List of the Candidate Asset List
Review candidates on the Asset Harvesting List
Determine if any should now be deleted from the list, or accepted as assets to be harvested from the project
