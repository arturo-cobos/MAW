Tailor service litmus tests and service exposure scopes to support client/project needs and environment.

Steps
Adapt the questions and scoring table for universal SLTs
Update the SLT questions for the universal SLTs to meet client environment and terminology. Update the scoring table and guidance around scoring level to meet client’s environment.
Add additional project/client specific SLT
Add project/client specific SLT, define the corresponding questions, define the scoring table for newly added SLTs, and add guidance around scoring level for those questions in the scoring table.
Adapt the service exposure scope to client’s environment
Update the service exposure scope pre-defined in SOMA and associated definition to fit into client’s environment.
