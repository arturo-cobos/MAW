Activity: Capture Existing Environment
Canvass the client environment to discover details of the current technical environment, standards, and any other constraints that may be important for the solution to consider when making requirements and design decisions.
DescriptionWork Breakdown StructureTeam AllocationWork Product Usage
Purpose
The purpose of this activity is to gain an understanding of the client's business and organization in preparation for recommending a solution.
Relationships
Parent Activities
  • Solution Outline
Description

Obtain or validate information about the client environment. This information may have already been given to the project team during Solution Design activities, or it may have been withheld until such time as the project team is engaged with the client. In either case, as this information is of critical importance in making decisions about the solution requirements and constraints, an effort should be made to ensure it is as accurate and complete as possible.
