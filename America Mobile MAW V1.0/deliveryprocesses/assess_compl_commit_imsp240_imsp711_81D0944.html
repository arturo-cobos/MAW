Task Descriptor: Assess completion of mutual commitments (IMSP240 and IMSP711)
Outlines the assessment performed to evaluate the completion of all commitments by the project stakeholders.

Steps
Assess completion of mutual commitments
Assess completion of mutual commitments. This includes but is not limited to:
  • Reviewing the agreement (i.e. SOW) to identify any areas where terms may not have been met
  • Checking the extent to which stakeholder commitments have been met
  • Addressing any concerns and ensuring that any outstanding items are completed
  • Reviewing the agreement (i.e. SOW) with the sponsor to ensure that it is agreed that all of the delivery organization's commitments have been met
  • Recovering any delivery organization property from the sponsor or the supplier
  • As agreed with the sponsor, carefully returning or destroying any of the sponsor's confidential material and sensitive information
  • Ensuring that ongoing support needs are addressed, including any contractual addenda or agreements that are required
Conduct closeout assessment of client satisfaction

Conduct closeout assessment of client satisfaction on expectations established throughout the project lifecycle.

This includes: 

  • Conducting the Conditions of Satisfaction closeout review
  • Understanding how / if GBS has met client expectations
  • Informing the client of potential upcoming IBM Client Value Assessment - Transaction Survey process
