Artifact: Bill of Materials
This artifact lists the constituent parts of a given version of a product or system, and where the physical parts may be found.
Purpose

This work product is used for planning the packaging of a release, to ensure that someone is responsible for creating all the required items, and as a final checklist to confirm that everything has been packaged and delivered.
