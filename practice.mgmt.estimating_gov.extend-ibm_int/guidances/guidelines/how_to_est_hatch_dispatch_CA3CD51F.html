Guideline: How to do an estimate: from hatch to dispatch
This guideline has been created to explain the four states of the estimating lifecycle and the steps required to estimate size, effort, schedule, and cost of a project.

Main Description

The techniques outlined in this document are, of necessity, generic in nature and cannot cover all eventualities. Use your experience and available information to follow them as closely as possible.

Deviations (and reasons) from the procedures outlined here should be documented in the Project Control Book at the Governance Points, to provide clarity for subsequent readers. 

The Governance Points are review steps at which:

  1. Responsibilities for these reviews are defined
  2. The estimate is reviewed
  3. The estimate is approved, or if not, remedial action is identified
  4. Once approved, the estimate and the review results are recorded in the Project Control Book.
