Main Description

One of the critical success factors for a project is assigning responsibilities and identifying who needs to be involved / consulted. Many a projects sometimes struggle to define this.

The most frequently used technique, that is of immense help in this area is the RACI (Responsible, Assigned, Consulted and Informed) matrix. It is a powerful yet simple project or process management technique for clarifying the way cross-functional team members collaborate and work together and it is one of the most common representations of the Responsibility Assignment Matrix (RAM).
