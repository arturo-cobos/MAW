Artifact: Meeting Documentation (ENG 334)
Resources used to conduct and record meetings, including agendas and minutes.
Domains: Project Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose

The purpose of Meeting Documentation is:

  • To provide the invitees with timely information about the purpose, time and location of the meeting
  • To provide the attendees with all information to prepare for the meeting efficiently
  • To keep a written record of the proceedings of the meeting
  • To disseminate information about the result of the meeting to the identified parties.

Description
Main Description

Meeting Documentation gathers all the information that is useful to:

  • Organize and prepare a meeting
  • Record and agree about the results of the meeting
Brief Outline

The Meeting Documentation is composed of:

  • Meeting agenda
  • Meeting minutes

Key Considerations

The purpose of the meeting is decided, and the practical aspects of it, including the agenda, are organized.

Meeting minutes are written and attendees given the opportunity to validate their accuracy in recording the results of the meeting.

Meeting Documentation that contains only a Meeting agenda component does not need to be validated. Meeting Documentation that contains a Meeting minutes component is validated by distribution to meeting attendees. If corrections are required, changes are made and redistributed to the original list.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not using Meeting Documentation are:

  • Objectives of the meeting are unclear for the attendees
  • Unnecessary time is spent to obtain the practical information about the meeting
  • Lack of efficiency in preparing for the meeting
  • The results of the meeting are unclear or nonexistent for the participants or unknown to people who did not attend the meeting
  • Greater risk that concurrent or successive meetings will generate inconsistent decisions and actions due to the lack of documentation.
Reasons for not needing

When the purpose of the meeting is to deal with a single item such as issues or to decide some actions, the result of the meeting can be documented with the Action Control Document, Issue Document, Change Request, etc.

Kickoff meetings or brief “news flash” meetings that address a single topic may not require meeting minutes.
