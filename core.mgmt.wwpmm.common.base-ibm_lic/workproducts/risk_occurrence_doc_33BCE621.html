Artifact: Risk Occurrence Document (ENG 353)
A document which records the information regarding the risk that has occurred including the description, the reaction description, the related actions, and history.
Domains: Project Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose

The purpose of a Risk Occurrence Document is:

  • to keep track of an event occurrence that adversely affects the project and that was anticipated as a risk
  • to follow-up the actions that are planned and implemented to respond to this risk occurrence.

Key Considerations
When an identified risk occurs, the Risk Management Plan defines the planned contingency actions for dealing with risk occurrence. These are reassessed and if still appropriate the plan is implemented. If more effective strategies are identified, actions to implement these are launched.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not using the Risk Occurrence Document are:

  • Risk contingency and reserve plans may not be implemented.
  • Actions may not be tracked and assessed for effectiveness
Reasons for not needing

The Risk Occurrence Document may not be required on projects for which few risks have been identified. In a simplified PM System, the Issue document may be used instead of the Risk Occurrence Document.
