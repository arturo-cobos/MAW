Artifact: Correspondence Log (ENG 401)
A tracking list of the individual correspondences with identifying information such as sender, recipient, date, and category.
Domains: Project Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose
The purpose of the Correspondence Log is to make sure that certain key communications and transmissions are clearly recognized and documented for possible action (for example, a formal complaint) and future reference.

Description
Main Description

The Correspondence Log is established to track and maintain key written communications and transmissions on the project.

It contains information about which stakeholder sent the communication, the date, description, category of the correspondence, and where it is archived.

Notation

The Correspondence Log is established at project level. It contains one component.

Key Considerations

The Correspondence Log is created at the beginning of Starting and maintained throughout the duration of the project. Its use is reviewed during Starting and as each new stakeholder joins the project.

The top level project manager should review and approve the Correspondence Log.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Lack of a Correspondence Log can inhibit a team from adequately and easily identifying key communications and formal correspondences through lack of traceability.

Inability to verify a date, or proof of the correspondence could lead to more serious consequences.

Reasons for not needingA Correspondence Log may not be required for small projects where team communications occur on a frequent basis.
