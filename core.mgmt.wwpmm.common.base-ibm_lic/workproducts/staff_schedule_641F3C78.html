Purpose

The purpose of the Staff Schedule is to provide the project manager with the up to date availability information needed when assigning individuals to project activities.

Summary information from the Staff Schedule is used to update the Human Resource Plan.

Description
Main Description

The Staff Schedule contains detailed information about the availability of the members of the project team.

Brief Outline

The Staff Schedule contains two components for each individual.

  • Identification and overall availability, which shows the period for which each individual is available for work on the project.
  • Unavailability period(s), which contains information about the periods, within the overall availability period, when the individual is planned to be unavailable for reasons such as vacation and training.

Key Considerations

The Staff Schedule is created as the project the team is built up during Starting and is updated as the planned availability of individuals changes during the course of the project.

The project manager verifies that:

  • The Staff Schedule is always complete and up to date.
  • The Operational schedule remains consistent with the Staff Schedule.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Lack of a Staff Schedule could result in:

  • Inefficient allocation of the work, leading to the overloading or underloading of individuals.
  • Difficulties in managing the release of individuals from the project by the date planned.
  • Failure to meet the individuals’ project related training needs.
Reasons for not needingThe Staff Schedule is not needed for a small project with few participants and short duration.
