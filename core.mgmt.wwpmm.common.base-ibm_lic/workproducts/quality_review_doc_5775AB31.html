Purpose

The purpose of the Quality Review Documentation is:

  • To create the conditions that allows the quality review to have effective results to improve the project course
  • To provide all the involved persons with timely information about the purpose of the review and its practical organization
  • To provide all the involved persons with the decision that come out from the review
  • To provide the evidence that the review has been conducted and that the recommendations have been taken into account.

Description
Main Description

The Quality Review Documentation collates all data that are necessary to prepare, perform a quality review, and follow up its results. It can also apply to management and business review.

The Quality Review Documentation is adapted to various types of quality reviews:

  • Health review documentation
  • Compliance review documentation
  • Deliverable review documentation
Brief Outline

The Quality Review Documentation contains these components:

  • Review definition
  • Review plan
  • Review report
  • Review follow-up

Key Considerations

The Quality Review Documentation plan is developed by the reviewer, or reviewing team, and then sent to the team that will be reviewed. The two groups agree to the proposed plan, adjusting as necessary, and then the plan is disseminated.

After the review, the report is developed by the review team and sent to the reviewed organization, who then plans the corrective actions and reports these plans back to the reviewer. The reviewer accepts the plan or asks for modifications. The reviewer might also specify a follow-up review date, at which time the effectiveness of the actions will be verified.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not using the Quality Review Documentation are:

  • The review cannot be scheduled appropriately
  • The conclusions and recommendations from the review are unclear
  • There is no common understanding of the corrective actions
  • The effectiveness of the corrective actions is not obtained and not checked
Reasons for not needingQuality Review Documentation is necessary to ensure efficiency of the quality reviews.
