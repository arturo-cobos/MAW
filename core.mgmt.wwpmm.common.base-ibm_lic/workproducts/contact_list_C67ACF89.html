Purpose
The purpose of the Contact List is to provide easily accessible, up to date contact information for everyone involved in the project.

Key Considerations

The Contact List is created at least by Starting and then updated regularly to reflect all the changes as people come and go during the course of the project.

Everyone concerned has easy access to an up to date copy of the Contact List.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without a Contact List, people waste time and effort simply finding out how to get in touch with each other. This is especially the case on large projects, on projects located across several sites, and on projects where the staff turnover is high.

Reasons for not needingA Contact List is not needed on small projects where the whole team is working together in one location.
