Artifact: Compliance Incident Document (ENG 321)
A document which records the information about an event in which the sponsor or a supplier is not meeting an obligation under the agreement. It contains the initial description, progress of resolution, and final assessment of damage.
Purpose

The purpose of the Compliance Incident Document is to:

  • Record a compliance incident and its impact on team progress
  • Record negotiations with the sponsor or supplier involved regarding financial responsibility
  • Record negotiations with the sponsor or supplier involved regarding the resolution actions
  • Record the resolution that was determined to allow the project to return to normal and avoid recurrence
  • Help assess resolution progress, particularly when the compliance incident triggered more than one resolution process or action
  • Help assess project status
  • Help defend the delivery organization in case of a legal action regarding the project.

Description
Main Description

A compliance incident is an event in which the sponsor or a supplier is not meeting an obligation under the Agreement, excluding defects.

The Compliance Incident Document records the information necessary to manage the complete life-cycle of a compliance incident: initial description, progress of resolution, and final assessment of damage.

Brief Outline

The Compliance Incident Document contains these components:

  • Compliance incident summary
  • Compliance incident description
  • Compliance incident resolution
  • Compliance incident history

Key Considerations

The Compliance Incident Document is recorded in detail and the sponsor or supplier involved is notified.

Workaround actions that allow the team to continue work are determined and put in place.

Resolution actions to fix the problem and to prevent recurrence are discussed with the sponsor or supplier involved. Mostly, those actions are to be undertaken by the sponsor or supplier involved in order for him to meet his obligations.

References to any written documentation related to the incident itself, discussions with the sponsor or supplier involved, resolution, and workaround actions that have been taken are recorded.

As soon as possible, damage is calculated and negotiation with the sponsor or supplier is conducted until an agreement is reached.

Several compliance incidents can be gathered in only one negotiation. The summary report can be built based on all the Compliance Incident Documents that are available.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

When compliance incidents are not recorded and tracked, the following could occur:

  • When discussing financial responsibilities related to the sponsor or a supplier not having met their obligations, factual data about the compliance incident and its impact will be missing.
  • Factual data will also be missing in case of a legal action regarding the project.
Reasons for not needingOn a small project, the Compliance Incident Log or even the Event Log might be used instead of Compliance Incident Documents.
