Main Description

The Issue Log is the list of issues that have occurred during the life of the project and that required recording.

The Issue Log contains these components:

  • List of issues
  • Issue statistics

Key Considerations

Issues that require recording are listed in the Issue Log, unless they can be recorded strictly with their corresponding actions in Action Control Documents.  Or, as is sometimes the case on small projects, issues might simply be recorded in meeting minutes or in status reports.

The Issue Log is then updated when issues change status.

The log is reviewed periodically to monitor resolution of pending issues, and also to analyze trends in issue occurrence and resolution.

The Issue Log must be up-to-date and consistent with its corresponding Issue Documents, if any were created.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without an Issue Log:

  • there is no visibility on the number of open issues and their status.
Reasons for not needing

The Issue Log is not needed on simple projects where the issues can be recorded with the actions or simply listed in meeting minutes and status reports.
