Artifact: Risk Definition (ENG 351)
Documents the risk description and risk history of events for each risk identified within the project.
Purpose

The purpose of a Risk Definition is to provide the project stakeholders with complete and accurate information about the risks facing the project.

Description
Main Description

The Risk Definition provides a description of each anticipated risk associated with the project.

A risk is a future event which may adversely affect the project.

Brief Outline

The Risk Definition contains the following components:

  • Risk identification
  • Risk description
  • Risk analysis/revision
  • Risk management plan summary
  • Risk reassessment history

Key Considerations
WWPMM embraces the concept of continuous risk management as defined by the Software Engineering Institute (SEI). The goal of risk management is to identify project risks and develop strategies which eliminate or significantly reduce the probability and impact of the risk occurrence.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not using the Risk Definition are:

  • The project manager is unprepared to react to adverse events which could have been anticipated.
  • The project plans are liable to change without warning when an undocumented risk occurs.
Reasons for not needing

The Risk Definition is required as a basis for the Risk Management Plan.
