The Expenditure Log work product records the actual expenditure of the project from purchase orders, invoices and expense sheets.

Key Considerations

Project management collects purchase orders, invoices and expense sheets and validates them for integration into business systems.

Validation of cost-related data is performed when a purchase order, invoice or expense sheet is received.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without this work product, project expenditures are not known or understood, which means that incorrect, inappropriate, or unauthorized charges are not prevented.

Reasons for not needingThis work product may not be needed when the project is small (less than five persons), has only labor from regular employees, no travel, and no hardware or software products.
