Task: Establish Development Facility
Setup of facilities required to support the development environment.
Disciplines: Environment
Purpose
The purpose of this task is to build the physical environment where people, machines, and software will work to build the proposed system.

      Main Description
      Establishment of the development facility is heavily dependent on available resources and organizational needs. However, it involves everything from providing the building and office space for development to occur, to acquiring the furniture, phones, lighting, office supplies, etc.
