Role Descriptor: Quality Analyst
This role is responsible for tracking and reporting on internal workmanship and external customer defects. Monitor quality and provide feedback on quality to operators, team leads, and managers.

Main Description

This role works with manufacturing technicians to analyze defect data in order to identify root causes and implement action plans to prevent future defects and to produce 'best in class' quality work
