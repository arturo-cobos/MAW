Getting Started

Test planning must start as early as possible in the project life cycle to ensure that testing requirements and dependencies are identified early and influenced, if necessary. The activities in test planning involve confirming the test strategy and developing a Master Test Plan and other detailed test plans for all levels of testing. The high-level Test Strategy drives the Master Test Plan, which gives the general direction for testing at the project level and is committed to by all groups.

In addition, detailed test plans, are also documented and interlocked through the Master Test Plan for each level of testing.

Test management processes are implemented through the related tasks to assure and demonstrate successful control and completion of testing activities.
