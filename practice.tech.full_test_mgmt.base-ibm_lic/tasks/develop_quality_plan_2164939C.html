Task: Develop Quality Plan
Create the quality plan for the project considering the deliverables specified in the statement of work and anticipated work products to be produced.
Disciplines: Test Management
Purpose

To ensure that:

  • The quality plan addresses only the appropriate areas.
  • That the quality factors which will define the quality of a system are identified and quantified.

Focusing upon these factors during development will ensure that the quality required of the system is achieved within the constraints imposed by the project budget.

      Main Description

      This task describes the level of quality the project needs to achieve.

      1. Define the scope, major quality objectives and evaluation criteria essential to the project.
      2. Outline a strategy for ensuring that the project meets the quality objectives.
      3. Identify risk areas.  
      4. Identify the metrics to be used to measure quality.
      5. Develop methods for tracking results against the plan.
      6. Integrate all the sections completed into the Quality Plan, and schedule review and revisions.
      Steps
      Review the project scope and deliverables
      Determine if project deliverables are to be excluded from scope of quality plan
      Also determine whether the quality aspects of those deliverables are to be excluded from the scope of the quality plan. Determine the reasons for this.
      Agree the quality scope
      Define the quality objectives
      Use JAD (Joint Application Development) or other brainstorming techniques to perform this.
      Define factors within the project which will measure the success of the project
      Consider the following project and service delivery factors: Capability, Reliability, Usability, Performance, Operability, Maintainability, Installability.
      Define and agree a measurable objective for each factor
      Verify that the objectives are SMART
      SMART stands for Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Realistic and Timely.
      Obtain customer approval to the quality scope and objectives
