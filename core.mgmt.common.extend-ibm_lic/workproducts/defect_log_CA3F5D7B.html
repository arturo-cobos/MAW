Artifact: Defect Log (APP 414)
This artifact lists defects discovered during a project. It assists in the management of the defect and provides a mechanism for tracking the defect to its resolution
Domains: Quality Management
Work Product Kinds: Inventory
Purpose

Its purpose is to:

  • Describe defects found in work products
  • Ensure that an audit trail of the defect is maintained
  • Assist in performing root cause analysis of defects
  • Assist in the resolution of defects with minimal impact to the project

Description
Brief Outline

This artifact generally contains:

  • Project information
  • Defect details, such as:
    • Severity
    • Status
    • Defect type and related information
    • Resolution information
    • Originator information
Selected RepresentationProcess Asset Library users may use the Defect Management Documentation template which consolidates the Defect Log and Defect Analysis Document templates.

Key Considerations
The artifact is verified and validated by the Originator, Project Manager, Resolution Owner, and any other relevant stakeholders of the project.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

The consequences of not using this work product include:

  • Chances of defect appearing again in the next stage is high 
  • Difficulty in analyzing the defect and identifying its priority 
  • Difficulty in recommending an appropriate fix
Reasons for not needingThis artifact may not be necessary if an automated tool or process is used to record the defect management details.
