Artifact: Configuration Audit Findings (ART 0668)
This artifact identifies a baseline, any missing required artifacts, and incompletely tested or failed requirements.
Domains: Quality Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose

This artifact is used to record the findings of baseline audits, including documenting and tracking audit failures to closure, these items include the following:

  • the performance of the developed software conforms to its requirements
  • the required work products are physically present

RolesResponsible: Modified By:

Description
Brief Outline

The following topics need to addressed:

  • Introduction 
    • Project identification
    • Date of Audit
    • Person performing the audits and other participants
    • Audit effort hours
  • The Intent of the Audit
  • Audit steps
  • Overall Assessment (including summarized strengths and weaknesses)
  • Physical Configuration Audit
  • Baseline Identification
  • Missing Work products
  • Functional Configuration Audit
  • Untested Requirements
  • Failed Requirements
  • Open Change Requests
  • Corrective Actions
    • Action
    • Actionee
    • Follow-up Date

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not using this work product are:

  • Data about the audit failures and their resolution might be lost, leading to incomplete or mismatched resolution information.
  • Status of the corrective actions might not be clear, leading to a resolution approach that is poorly designed and that results in duplication of effort and wasted time.
  • If the audit results need to be escalated to the next level of management, the information related to the transfer and follow-up might be difficult to obtain.
  • Progress of the resolution might be difficult to follow-up on.
