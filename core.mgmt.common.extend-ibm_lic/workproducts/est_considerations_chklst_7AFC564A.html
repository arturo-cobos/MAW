Description
Main Description

This artifact provides a list of items that should be considered in developing estimates on projects to ensure that sufficient time and budget is allocated to perform the work. 

Brief Outline

The checklist questions are broadly classified under the following categories:

  • Considerations for Estimate Development
  • Considerations for Estimating Size
  • Considerations for Estimating Effort
  • Considerations for Estimating Schedule
  • Considerations for Estimating Cost
  • Final Considerations
  • Miscellaneous Project Management Considerations

Key Considerations
Before estimating, the estimator should attempt to identify actual results from similar past projects. If any are found, the estimator should include them as part of the considerations to perform or validate the estimate.
Tailoring
Impact of not havingConsequences of not having this work product are:
  • Lack of guidance while creating or reviewing the estimate, and chances are that some critical considerations are missed out when performing the estimates.
  • No formal record of the review results based on the checklist.
Reasons for not needingThis checklist helps ensure that all considerations have been made when performing the estimate, so that sufficient time is allocated to perform the work. Depending on the size, complexity, or type of project or task, the team member may make detailed considerations in some areas, or skip over others as not being applicable to the project or task being estimated.
