Artifact: Project Suitability Assessment (ART 0642)
Assessment of whether the project in question has appropriate characteristics for the selected development approach.
Domains: Project Management
Work Product Kinds: Assessment
Purpose

Determination of fit between project characteristics and the selected development approach. A poor fit indicates that alternative development approaches should be considered or the requirements of the project modified to better fit the approach currently under consideration.

Relationships
RolesResponsible: Modified By:

Description
Main Description

Evaluate the characteristics of the project to determine if the selected development approach is feasible and minimizes risk. Factors to consider include:

  1. Quantity and complexity of requirements
  2. Maturity of technology envisioned
  3. Number of stakeholders and their expectations
  4. Time frame expected for completion
  5. Team size necessary to perform development as determined by time frames and architectural requirements
  6. Skill levels possessed by project team members
  7. Flexibility of project requirements, timelines, and sponsorship
Tailoring
Impact of not havingThe team may pursue a development approach ill fitted to the characteristics of the project, resulting in poor progress and failure to meet stakeholder expectations.
Reasons for not needingThe project characteristics are easily understood to match the development approach, i.e. we've done this many times using this approach with excellent results.
