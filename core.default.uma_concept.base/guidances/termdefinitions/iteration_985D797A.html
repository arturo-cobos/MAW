Term Definition: iteration
Main Description

A grouping of repeatable activities based on a set period of time that produces an expected set of results that has value.  These results may be further refined in successive iterations.

  • Sets detailed increment goals for the development of specific functionality. The iteration includes design, implementation, unit test, and assessment.
  • Ends with an assessment of how well the increment goals of the iteration were met and approval of the software produced by the iteration for integration into the build.
  • Within each build cycle may be multiple development teams working on parallel iterations.
