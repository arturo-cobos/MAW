      Main Description
      Gather and evaluate feedback from the sponsor and project team. Make suggestions for improvement. Collect reusable assets and forward them to the appropriate intellectual capital management focal point. Publish lessons learned in the appropriate repository.

      Analyze the completed survey to determine areas of satisfaction and dissatisfaction. Take action to address any specific areas of dissatisfaction.
