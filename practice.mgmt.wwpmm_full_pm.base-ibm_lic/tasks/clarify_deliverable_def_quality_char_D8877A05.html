Task: Clarify Deliverable Definitions and quality characteristics
Disciplines: Deliverables Management
Purpose
To identify and document the deliverables and their quality characteristics for each project organizational unit (POU).
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Project Definition
  • Work Product List
    Outputs
    • Deliverable Definition
      Main Description
      Review the part of the project scope that is assigned to the project organizational unit (POU) and identify the work products that the POU plans to deliver to either the sponsor or another POU.

      Clarify the acceptance or completion criteria for these work products, and determine the quality characteristics and quality criteria that will contribute in meeting these acceptance criteria. Document these in the Deliverable Definition(s).
