Task: Prepare and review proposed Agreement
Disciplines: Sponsor Agreement Management
Purpose
To develop a proposal or proposed Agreement according to the delivery organizations guidelines and provide support for any negotiations with the sponsor and the delivery organization regarding this Agreement.

      Main Description
      Develop a proposal - or proposed Agreement - by integrating material from the Project Charter, the plans that have been developed or outlined so far, and other documents or standard paragraphs, into the Agreement framework provided by the delivery organizationâ€™s standard template for the type of project concerned.

      Note that, particularly in commercial contexts, preparing a proposal is a nontrivial and time-consuming task. However, most delivery organizations have developed templates and detailed guidance, and have put in place specialized teams to simplify the writing and to assure the quality of the document. But in any case the relevant inputs must be brought to such specialized teams, and the resulting draft proposal must be reviewed from complementary points of view to ensure that all necessary aspects are addressed and are not open to misinterpretation.

      Finally, follow up and provide support to the negotiation and approval of the Agreement by the project sponsor, include key customer satisfaction items and issues in the Project Definition so that they can be transferred to the team in charge of performing project execution, and package all records and documentation relevant for that team.
