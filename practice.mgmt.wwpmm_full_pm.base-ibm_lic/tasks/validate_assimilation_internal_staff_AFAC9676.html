Task: Validate successful assimilation (internal staff)
Disciplines: Human Resource Management
Purpose
To ensure that team members are assimilating into the project successfully.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Individual Status Report
    Outputs
    • Corrective actions
      Main Description
      Solicit comments from new team members to determine how well they feel with the piece of work they have begun, and examine early Individual Status Reports to ensure that team members are getting up to speed.

      Rapidly take steps to help any the team members catch up, as appropriate.
