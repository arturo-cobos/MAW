Task: Present report to delivery organization management and escalate issues
Disciplines: Communications Management
Purpose
To convey status report information to the management team of the delivery organization.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Project Status Report
    Outputs
    • Project Status Report
      Main Description
      Present or communicate the report to the management of the delivery organization and obtain feedback. Emphasis should be placed on risk and cost control for the project.

      Review the contents of the management summary with the senior management of the delivery organization per the Communications Management Plan.

      Negotiate actions requested by management.
