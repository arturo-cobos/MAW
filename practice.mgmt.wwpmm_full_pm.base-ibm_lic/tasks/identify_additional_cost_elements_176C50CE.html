Task: Identify additional cost elements
Disciplines: Work Plan Management
Purpose
To identify any additional costs associated with things like risk mitigation activities, or unexpected requirements of the technical environment, or overhead costs incurred by meetings or regulations, or costs associated with difficult quality criteria.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager

    Outputs

    • Human Resource Plan
    • Outline of the Communications Management Plan
    • Outline of the Project Procedures Description
    • Outline of the Project Quality Plan
    • Outline of the Technical Environment Plan
    • Project Decision Structure
    • Project Management Schedule
      Main Description
      Complete the Deliverable Definitions, including the acceptance processes and criteria that will need to be agreed to with the project sponsor.

      Complete the requirements for the Project Decision Structure, that will be responsible for making specific decisions about the direction, management, and results of the project. This structure usually includes management representatives of both the sponsor and the delivery organization, and is key to the project success.

      Ensure that the project Work Breakdown Structure (WBS), Project Management Schedule, and Human Resource Plan, properly take into account any additional dependency, activity, and workload resulting from the initial Risk Management Plan (from previous task) and from the complementary plans that are considered above.

      Consider the level of detail to which the Technical Environment Plan, Communications Management Plan, and Project Quality Plan, and others, should be brought to avoid any surprise with unforeseen workload and costs when performing project execution, and outline these plans accordingly.
