Task: Organize Work Breakdown Structure and determine team structure
Disciplines: Work Plan Management
Purpose
To organize the activities for the project organizational unit (POU) and understand the team structure needed to support them.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)
    Outputs
    • Human Resource Plan
    • Organizational Breakdown Structure (OBS)
    • Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)
      Main Description
      Determine the nature and amount of resources that will be necessary to undertake the project organizational unit activities and, referring to relevant team structure models, design the team structure that best fits the activities to be performed. Document in the Human Resource Plan and in the team structure of the Organizational Breakdown Structure (OBS) for the project organizational unit (POU).

      At the same time, the activities that were identified in the initial project organizational unit Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) are bundled into groups and structured so that responsibilities can be clearly assigned to components of the team structure.

      Lastly, determine the activities and resources to manage the POU work as well as those for providing support when appropriate. Document in the WBS for the POU being considered.
