Task: Compare productivity measurement with estimating basis
Disciplines: Tracking and Control
Purpose
To compare estimates with current effort data (actual and total estimates to complete) and identify and assess any variances.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Operational Schedule
    Outputs
    • Operational Schedule
      Main Description
      Analyze the effort data (actual and total estimates to complete) for each activity type and compare it with the current estimating basis.. The current estimating basis may be derived from the original estimates created during Shaping or might have been created when previously assessing the estimating basis and making adjustments due to previously detected variances.

      Identify the variance for each type of activity.

      Assess if any other factors have affected the productivity before changing the estimating basis.
