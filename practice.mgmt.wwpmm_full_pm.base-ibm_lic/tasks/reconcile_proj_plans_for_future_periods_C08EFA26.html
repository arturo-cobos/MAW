Task: Reconcile project plans for future periods
Disciplines: Tracking and Control
Purpose
To review or adjust project plans in response to deviations that have been identified. Identify issues or changes, as necessary.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Milestone List
  • Operational Schedule
  • Project Management Schedule
    Outputs
    • Milestone List
    • Operational Schedule
    • Project Management Schedule
      Main Description
      Review and adjust project plans for future periods to ensure the best use will be made of resources.

      Raise an issue if the resolution of a deviation cannot be done at once.

      Launch a Change Request if modifications to the project baselines and commitments are necessary.
