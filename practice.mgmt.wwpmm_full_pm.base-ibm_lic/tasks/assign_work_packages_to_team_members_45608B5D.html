Task: Assign work packages to team members
Disciplines: Work Plan Management
Purpose
To bundle work units and assign work packages to the appropriate resources, based upon the availability and expertise of team members.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Human Resource Plan
  • Project Management Schedule
  • Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)
    Outputs
    • Operational Schedule
      Main Description
      Bundle the work units into work packages, compare the skills and time frame required to produce the work packages with the profiles and availability of the project team members, and then plan the appropriate assignments.

      Adjust as necessary the detailed effort estimates within the constraints of the baseline to take into account the availability and capability of these team members, and elaborate the teams Operational Schedules for the coming tracking periods.

      Note that this task may iterate with the next one, until the Operational Schedules, staffing plans (Staff Schedules), and project schedule baseline can be reconciled.
