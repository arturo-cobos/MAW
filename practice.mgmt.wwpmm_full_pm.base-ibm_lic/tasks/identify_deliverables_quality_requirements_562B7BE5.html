Task: Identify deliverables quality requirements
Disciplines: Quality Management
Purpose
To ensure that all quality-related requirements are understood and that the associated quality activities are accounted for in the project plans.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Agreement
  • Deliverable Definition
  • Outline of the Project Quality Plan
    Outputs
    • Project Quality Plan
      Main Description
      Review the sponsor Agreement, Deliverable Definitions, Product Breakdown Structure, and the outline of Project Quality Plan that were produced when shaping the project.

      Complete the identification and description of the quality characteristics, quality criteria and quality contributors which should ensure meeting the deliverables acceptance criteria.

      Get appropriate inputs from technical team members, assess that the estimates for quality related activities are reliable and that they are correctly reflected in the project Work Breakdown Structure and Project Management Schedule, and finalize the Project Quality Plan.

      When the project organization contains several organizational units (POU) or subcontractors, ensure consistency of the overall project plan with the local quality plans that may exist at the POU or subcontractor level.
