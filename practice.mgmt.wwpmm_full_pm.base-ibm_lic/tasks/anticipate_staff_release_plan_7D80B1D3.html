Task: Anticipate staff release plan
Disciplines: Human Resource Management
Purpose
To plan for the release of staff.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Human Resource Plan
  • Operational Schedule
  • Project Management Schedule
    Outputs
    • Operational Schedule
    • Staff Schedule
      Main Description
      Determine a reasonable sequence in which people should leave and adjust plans accordingly. Adjust the Operational Schedule to reflect the work to be completed as well as necessary staff reassignments. Adjust the Staff Schedule.
