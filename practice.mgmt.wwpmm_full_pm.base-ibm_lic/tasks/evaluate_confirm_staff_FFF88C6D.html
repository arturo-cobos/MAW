Task: Evaluate and confirm staff
Disciplines: Human Resource Management
Purpose
To evaluate potential resources, select specific candidates, and document any specific staffing needs in the Staff Schedule and Staff List.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Human Resource Plan
    Outputs
    • Staff List
    • Staff Schedule
      Main Description
      Review the staffing requirements from the Human Resource Plan. Evaluate candidates using résumés and information acquired from prior managers, colleagues, and interviews.

      Obtain firm commitments from the sourcing organizations on the starting date, cost, and availability of the selected candidates, document the Staff Schedule and Staff List accordingly, and determine any education plan that should be required to adapt the candidates skills.
