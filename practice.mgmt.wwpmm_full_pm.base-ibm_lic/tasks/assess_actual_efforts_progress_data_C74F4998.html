Task: Assess actual efforts and progress data
Disciplines: Tracking and Control
Purpose
To collect actual effort and progress data, compare it to planned estimates for the work accomplished, and identify and resolve any variances between the plans and actuals.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Individual Status Report
  • Operational Schedule
  • Project Management Schedule
  • Subcontractor Status Report
  • Sub-Project Team Status Report
    Outputs
    • Operational Schedule
      Main Description
      Collect team members and subcontractor progress data, check completeness, correctness, and reliability of the data, compare with the effort and duration in the Operational and project schedules to assess project progress and estimates to complete.

      Identify effort and schedule variances. Also identify deviations involving risk identification and handling. Assess the status of subcontractor progress against subcontractor plans.

      Resolve variances within the scope of each project organizational unit, if at all possible, with focus on the project schedule critical path.

      This is a bottom-up task, where progress on low level items in the project Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) are rolled up to higher levels of the WBS.
