Task: Compare project and business financial data
Disciplines: Tracking and Control
Purpose
To gather and organize financial data from both internal project sources and external business systems, reconcile the financial data, and identify any discrepancies which require action.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Business Systems Project Data
  • Expenditure Log
  • Financial Plan
    Outputs
    • Financial Plan
      Main Description
      Collect purchase orders, invoices and expense sheets and validate them for integration into business systems and the project internal accounting.

      Ensure that all cost-related data have been collected and that the data is complete and up-to date.

      Early in the month, check that any corrections requested the previous month to the business data have been made, and compare business and project data.

      Identify any discrepancies and analyze causes.

      Reconcile business with project financial data and improve forecasts.
