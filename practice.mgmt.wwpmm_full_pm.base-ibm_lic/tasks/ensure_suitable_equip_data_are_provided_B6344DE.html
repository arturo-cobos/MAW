Task: Ensure suitable equipment and data are provided
Disciplines: Supplier Management
Purpose
To ensure that a subcontractor has suitable facilities and materials to begin work.

      Main Description
      Ensure that the facilities, the information technology, communications material, security rights, etc., are provided to the subcontractor in compliance with the supplier Agreement. Document any assets provided to the subcontractor in the project Asset Inventory accordingly.
