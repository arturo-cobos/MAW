Task: Determine impact and potential solutions
Disciplines: Change Management
Purpose
To assess the impact of a requested change upon the project, including detailed costs and benefits/risks, and seek approval of the Change Request.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Operational Schedule
  • Project Management Schedule
    Outputs
    • Change Order
    • Issue Document
      Main Description
      Review the rationale behind the change request and develop alternatives as appropriate to the proposed change. Identify the impact of the potential alternatives on all the aspects of the project which should be reworked or revised, such as deliverables, project plans, procedures, and sponsor and supplier agreement. Evaluate the time scales to get agreement on the change and then implement it, the related costs, and the potential benefits and risks for the project.

      Provide the change control board, or a higher level of management approval, with the detailed information they need to decide whether or not to implement the proposed change.
