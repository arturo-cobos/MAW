Role: Business Operations Professional (042009)
This role is responsible for operations management. This could include revenue; profit performance; development and management of business processes including quality assurance, business controls and procedures; customer satisfaction deployment and ratings; and plan administration.
Role Sets: Administrator, Business
Relationships
Main Description

This role develops and administers unit revenue, cost, and expense budgets, and manage each to optimization. They are measured on revenue, profit, customer satisfaction and satisfactory audits.

  • Apply Knowledge of Human Resource (HR) Programs

  • Perform Customer Environment Assessment
