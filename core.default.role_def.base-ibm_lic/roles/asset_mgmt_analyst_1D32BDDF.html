Role: Asset Management Analyst (044057)
This role provides primary responsibility to ensure high quality and timeliness of input into the asset, i.e. hardware and/or software, tracking database(s); provides process documentation and training activities as necessary; and ensures audit-readiness of all processes, tasks, and activities.
