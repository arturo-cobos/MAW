Role: Reviewer
This role reviews material and provides opinions or comments.
Role Sets: Adjunct, Any
Relationships
Additionally Performs
  • Conduct internal management review (IMSP391)
  • Launch project, understand scope, risk, build startup plan (P391)
  • Refine or prepare initial risk management plan (IMSP391)
Modifies
  • Final PMR Report and Rating
    Main Description

    This role performs a review of material and forms opinions on it with respect to the needs of the project.  This role provides detailed comments and reasons for the opinions to the project team.  The person filling this role requires another set of skills and responsibilities that are defined by the project at hand.

    Staffing
    Skills

    The skills this role needs are supplied by other roles on the project team.
