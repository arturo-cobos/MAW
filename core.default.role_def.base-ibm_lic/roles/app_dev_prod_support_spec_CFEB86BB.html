Role: Application Development Products Support Specialist (045180)
IT Specialists in this job role will have expertise in one or more application development products. Examples include mainstream application development frameworks, such as those from IBM, Microsoft, and Sun.
Role Sets: Specialist, Technology

    Main Description

    Typical examples of the deliverables are building proof-of-concept solutions with application development technology, architecting application development software solutions to address client requirements, and conducting solution assurance reviews to ensure proposed solutions meet client requirements.  IT Specialists who apply their technical skills to support the operation and maintenance of vendor products, services, and solutions.  Individuals who are part of Support typically have responsibility for sizing, troubleshooting, and critical customer situations.
