Role: Opportunity Owner
The opportunity owner is responsible for validating an opportunity.
Role Sets: Adjunct, Marketing

  • Define target solution (IMSP395)
  • Document risks attached to solution (IMSP395)
  • Evaluate options and discuss project objectives (IMSP395)
  • Understand objectives and plan defining activities (IMSP395)
Modifies
  • Agreement
  • Project Start (Project Launch) Letter
    Main Description
    This role is responsible for assessing the initial opportunity, confirming the client's business needs, and validating the opportunity with the client. This includes clarifying  the business needs and initial requirements during the assessment of the initial opportunity and before developing the win strategy and themes, savings opportunities and value proposition.
