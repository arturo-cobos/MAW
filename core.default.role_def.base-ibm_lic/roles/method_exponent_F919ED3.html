Role: Method Exponent
This role is key to deploying the Methods at both Solution Design and Solution Delivery.
Role Sets: Consultant, Method
Relationships
Modifies
  • Project Defined Process
  • Risk Definition
  • Team Briefed
  • Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)
  • Work Product List
    Main Description

    This role deploys and tailors methods during solution design and solution delivery. This includes:

    • Assisting the project team in adoption of method and techniques as well as setting the example by contributing directly to the creation of work products
    • Assisting both the engagement management and the project team as one of its regular members
    • Fostering consistency
    • Sharing best practices among projects
    • Feeding back experience to improve to the method architecture and tooling)
    Staffing
    Skills
    • Apply Project Management Methodologies
    • Develop Risk Management Plan
    • Manage Client Relationships
    • Perform Customer Consulting
    • Perform Customer Relationship Management 
    Key Considerations

    Depending on the size, risk level and complexity of the project, the experience/skills of the project team, the involvement of this role can go up to full time.  For a project team of 10 or more it is expected that this will be a full time role, doing regular project work and fully billed to the project. In this way, they are recognized as a legitimate team member.
