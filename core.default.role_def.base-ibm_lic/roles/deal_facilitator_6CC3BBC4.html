Role: Deal Facilitator (042392)
This role assists bid teams on engagements across all IBM brands and lines of business in developing and delivering high quality and responsive proposals, solutions, and contracts.
Role Sets: Consultant, Marketing
Relationships
Main Description

Deal Facilitators provide leadership, essential knowledge, support, and teaming to help sellers and opportunity owners through the bid and proposal management process using Value Driven Proposal techniques and knowledge of IBM and its organization and resources.

  • Perform e-business Solution Delivery
  • Perform Project Execution, Control & Closure
