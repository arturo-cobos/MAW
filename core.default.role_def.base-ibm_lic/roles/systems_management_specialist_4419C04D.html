Role: Systems Management Specialist (040673)
IT Specialists in this job role will have expertise in managing and operation of IT hardware, software, communications, and/or application solutions, and the resources required to plan for, develop, deliver, and support properly engineered IT services and products to meet the needs of a business.
Role Sets: Operations, Specialist

    Main Description

    The scope of this job role includes preparation for new or changed services, management of the change process, and maintenance of regulatory, legal, and professional standards, management of performance of systems and services in relation to their contribution to business performance, and management of bought-in services including, for example, public network, virtual private network, and outsourced services. Typical examples of the deliverables are service-level reporting, risk, and contingency planning.  IT Specialists who apply their technical skills to support the operation and maintenance of vendor products, services, and solutions.  Individuals who are part of Support typically have responsibility for sizing, troubleshooting, and critical customer situations.
