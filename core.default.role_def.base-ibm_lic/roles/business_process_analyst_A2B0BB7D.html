Role: Business Process Analyst (043737)
The business process analyst leads and coordinates business use-case modeling by outlining and delimiting the organization being modeled for example, by establishing what business actors and business use cases exist and how they interact.
Role Sets: Analyst, Business
Relationships
Main Description

This job role includes:

  • Investigating complex business requirements;
  • Translating requirements to applications or processes proposals;
  • Assessing and documenting a road map to solution the requirement; 
  • Evaluating pros & cons, prioritize and recommend options; 
  • Distinguishing communication among audiences (i.e. higher & lower management, project participants, operation vs. staff functions etc..);
  • Providing a deep understanding of Business Controls, audit and risk aspects in business processes as well as applications;
  • Playing a vital role in project concept phase;
  • Addressing, coordinating and driving requirements capture and approval process;
  • Providing higher level support to operation and development teams;
  • Managing integration of acquisitions and alliances including any technical issues;
  • Understanding the IBM accounting strategy;
  • Planning and coordinating the re-engineering, integration, measurements/scorecards and control plans across accounting centers, regions, and geographies;
  • Identifying key skills and people required to execute the plans;
  • Identifying problems/exposures and recommend solutions to senior and executive management, providing, where possible, alternative solution approaches.
