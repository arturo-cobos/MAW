Role: Delivery Engagement Manager (042240)
This role is responsible for coordinating delivery services for an engagement. The services include technical solutions, resources, and other services the customer requires.
Role Sets: Management, Manager
Relationships
Main Description
This role must have a thorough understanding of the customer's business, goals, strategies, and industry trends and directions. They coordinate and contribute to efforts of large, multi-disciplinary teams, working to integrate products and services required to meet the customer's needs.
