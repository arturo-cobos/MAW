Task Descriptor: Establish Training Environment
This task may range from just reserving a classroom, to hosting the education materials so that students may take the required education.

Main Description
In this task the physical facilities required for training are identified and allocated to support the training schedule aligned with the rollout of the solution. These physical facilities include not just the classroom, with it's related capacity and presentation characteristics, but also other related infrastructure requirements such as computer(s), appropriate software, connections to hosting environments, etc. All these logistics have to be accomplished before formal training can begin.
