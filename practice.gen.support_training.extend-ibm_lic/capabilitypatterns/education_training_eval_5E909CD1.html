Key Considerations
  • Based on the course evaluations and informal feedback, the post production support strategy should be revised as needed.
  • If at all possible, have the client take full responsibility for scheduling the training.
  • If a client chooses to deliver training using their own instructors, refer to the Train the Trainer technique paper.
  • The training instance and data load must be carefully planned and managed.
  • It is recommend that a client representative be in all classrooms to address process related questions and to facilitate knowledge transfer to the client.
  • If a dress rehearsal is conducted, do not underestimate the preparation time needed. It will likely take a full-time coordinator several weeks with a great deal of involvement from the subject matter experts, security, and the training team.
  • To facilitate hands-on end user training, a training database may be created, populated, and maintained.
