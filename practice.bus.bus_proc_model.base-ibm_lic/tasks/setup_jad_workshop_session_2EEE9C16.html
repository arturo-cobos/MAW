      Main Description

      Process Modeling and Decomposition:

      The high level business process map is usually six to ten boxes which represent the high-level milestones in the current process. One can build this high-level map with a small team. It is often best to keep the working group small and have just a project lead who really knows the whole process or a majority of the process and facilitator. The project lead is a person who typically knows most about all the steps in the process and is usually a supervisor or manager. This person is the key point person for the team with the sponsor and will be the point person for any of the team members who want to raise issues outside of the team meeting situations. This person also helps coordinate the meetings. The facilitator manages the meetings, and needs both group process skills as well as knowledge of process improvement methodology. S/he encourages participation, and builds the diagrams with the team. Later this person will help with analysis and redesign of the process. In absence of an employee having the skills and knowledge to perform this role in the organization, typically an external consultant is hired to perform the role.

      Steps
      Select appropriate tools to capture business processes
      Identify workshop participants and assemble the team

      Identify the subject matter expert, facilitator/moderator, sponsor and other participants for the workshop and assemble the team. Moderator is responsible of managing the meeting and keeping the team on track. His/her other roles are:

      • Establish objective tone to the meeting.
      • Start and stop the meeting on time.
      • Establish and enforce the rules for the meeting.
      • Introduce the goals and agenda for the meeting.
      • Facilitate a process of decision and consensus making, but avoid participating in the content.
      • Make certain that all stakeholders participate and have their input heard.
      • Gather "Open Points" and follow up actions between sessions using meeting minutes.
      Setup the agenda
      Define the rules of execution of each workshop session
      Schedule JAD session/workshops
