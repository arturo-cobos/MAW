Task: Select Business Processes for Analysis
Select business processes for improvement based on the business goals established before.
Disciplines: Business Modeling
Purpose
To identify business processes requiring improvement.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Business Analyst
Additional Performers:
  • Stakeholder
InputsMandatory:
    • [Business Requirements]
      • Business Direction

      • Customer Wants and Needs
      • Industry Environment Analysis
      • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
      • Security and Privacy Policy
    		Optional:
    • Process Identification
      Outputs
      • Process Identification
        Main Description
        An organization has to select the business process to work on based on the business goals articulated before documenting the business process for improvement. Often this happens at a senior level where management prioritizes the work or information process they want to work on at this time, based on criteria they define. These criteria might be customer satisfaction, opportunities, competitive challenge and threats, or concern about defects or cycle time. Once a process has been decided it is helpful to articulate the business case â€“ in other words what is the business reason we are working on this now. The selection can be done by a work team who recognizes a need for process improvement and understands the business goals.
