Deliverable: Production Cutover Checklist (DCD 118)
This document will consist of the deployment criteria approved by the management, and the checklist of the deployment criteria that have been met and recommendations for additional actions required prior to the cut-over.
Purpose
The purpose of this document is to provide management with findings for whether the deployment criteria for the solution have been met, and recommendations for any additional actions that are required prior to the cut-over.

External Description

The objective of this document is to verify that management has reviewed the deployment criteria, including the cut-over date, and gives the approval for the cut-over.  This document is to be reviewed along with the deployment plans and deployment criteria are to be reviewed with management in order to obtain their approval for the cut-over.

Management is responsible for participating in the review of these items and making the final approval to cut-over to the production system based on the results of that review.

Deliverable Parts
  • Deployment Plan
  • Test Log
Packaging Instructions

The final document may be delivered in a reproducible format in addition to a final checklist that is also delivered in hard copy format printed in a single, bound document for final sign-off.

Inputs to this deliverable include the following:

Deployment Plan – provides details on what is need to cut-over to production and the exact process that will take place.  It is critical that agreement on the details of the deployment are reached prior to the actual cut-over.

Test Results – used to identify the whether all components of the solution have met the required criteria for the system to go live.
