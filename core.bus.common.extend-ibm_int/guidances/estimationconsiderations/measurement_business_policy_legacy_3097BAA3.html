Main Description

The organization-level policies typically take 4 – 6 labor hours each.  

The Measurement Implementation Plan typically take 8 – 10 labor hours and the Audit Considerations typically take 6 – 8 labor hours.

The basic estimating parameters for the measurement business policies are:

  • The number of measurements  (computed metrics) identified in the measurement model (either actual or high level planning estimate)
  • The number of unique primitive metrics identified in the measurement model
  • Assume a minimum 2-1 ratio of primitives to computed metrics – this would be a low end estimate

For estimating purposes, it could take up to two to six labor hours to work through the policy for each unique primitive to ensure completeness.  This includes defining the policies for each primitive and including them in the document.
