Main Description

Periodically, the Measurement Business Policy document should serve as an audit tool to determine if business processes are operating according to the policies established.  If they are not, corrective actions should be taken to either bring the business processes into conformance or to change the business policy.

Audits should be performed on key business processes during contract quality assurance reviews to ensure conformity to the established policies.
