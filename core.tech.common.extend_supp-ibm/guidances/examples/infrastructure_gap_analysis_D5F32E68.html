Sample Gap Analysis (Facts, Findings, Conclusions Group) (partial)

Conclusions

Less than 50% of ALL applications will be replaced by SAP R/3. We must implement and infrastructure that will be able to meet the service level requirements for both R/3 and non-R/3 applications. (A, B, D, E, F, H)

Findings
  1. There are applications that are in use at Company ABC that R/3 will not replace (i.e., MS Exchange, HR system, CAD, etc.)
  2. There are many 'departmental/location' specific applications in use across Company ABC.
  3. Company ABC has multiple databases and DBMS that will remain after SAP R/3 is implemented.
  4. Company ABC supports multiple versions of DBMSs (Oracle 7.1, 7.14)
  5. Company ABC plants have multiple data collection systems using specialized devices, some real time that provides source data for other systems.
  6. Company ABC utilized many interfaces between mfg. and business systems, mfg. and mfg systems, and business and business systems, some of which will remain after BMS/ERP package implementation.
