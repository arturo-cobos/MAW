Activity: Rollout Training
Conduct user training as needed to ensure the users needing to make use of the solution have the skills neccessary to do so.
Extends: Rollout Training
DescriptionWork Breakdown StructureTeam AllocationWork Product Usage
Purpose

To provide users of the solution release with the skills needed to use the solution. To provide support personnel with the knowledge required to support the routine operations of the solution.

Relationships
Parent Activities
  • Deploy
Description
Prepare the appropriate facilities for conducting the training of users for this release. Conduct pilot versions of the training as required and incorporate the lessons learned from these classes to make final adjustments to the training delivery. Deliver the training to users according the schedule determined previously.
