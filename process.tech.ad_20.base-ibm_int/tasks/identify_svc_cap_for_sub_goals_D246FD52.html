Task: Identify Candidate Services Based on Business Drivers
Identify services or business capabilities needed to fulfill business goals.
Disciplines: Business Modeling
Purpose
To ensure each business goal can be fulfilled by set of services or business capabilities.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • SOA Architect

    		Optional:
    • Service Model
      Outputs
      • Service Portfolio
        Main Description

        One of the many methods of service identification through business goal analysis is to identify the services that are required to fulfill each one key process indicators addressed by each goal. Goals are decomposed until an actionable service is identified that is required to fulfill the goal.

        During decomposition of goals into sub-goals and KPIs, the question will arise as to when we should stop this decomposition of goals. As already mentioned, goal decomposition can stop once sub-goals have been identified to the point at which services that can fulfill those sub-goals can be identified. If new services are identified, update the categorized service portfolio in the service model.

        For each sub-goal, identify the services needed to fulfill the sub-goal.
        Steps
        Identify services or business capabilities that satisfy sub-goals
        Update service portfolio
        Add services to the service portfolio in the Service Model work product
        Key Considerations

        Services can be identified by several means : through a decomposition of business processes, through existing asset analysis, variation-oriented analysis, analysis of information access requirements. Goal-service modeling is one of the principal techniques for service identification. It is recommended that a combination of techniques is used to identify services. The granularity of the services corresponds to the granularity of the goals associated with that service. The goal decomposition is continued until an actionable service is identified that can be used to fulfill that goal or subgoals. A key performance indicator can be used to measure the fulfillment of the goal as a result of the activation of the service.

        A goal may have multiple services associated with it that are collectively required to fulfill that goal.
