Guideline: DesignFlow Volumes - Guidance and Usage
This paper describes a technique for incorporating DesignFlow Volume constructs into work product based methodologies. It also provides recommended sections, notation, sample templates and specific considerations for developing DesignFlow Volumes.
Main Description
The DesignFlow methodology consolidates key operational process design work products in to the following four volumes:
 
These volumes are convenient packages of information that can be used by IBM consultants and client team members. The collection of engagement information in work products and volumes facilitates the analysis and communication of issues driving the project. The volumes are also typically used a final deliverables.
 
This technique paper is divided in the following sections:
  • Part 1 of the document focuses on mapping DesignFlow data frameworks to work products, an overall “Summary Chart” for the frameworks, and several framework selection scenarios.
  • Part 2 describes each data framework
  • Part 3 includes sample templates for DesignFlow Volume 1, Volume 2, and Volume 3 deliverables
