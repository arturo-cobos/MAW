Guideline: Assessing Knowledge Content
Main Description
The Knowledge Content Assessment is a process designed to help communities determine the types of content necessary for business success. The process looks at groups of people with common needs and attempts to identify and match information resources with their information needs and requirements.
 
This process worksheet is intended to be used to evaluate the knowledge content needs of a business area. In addition, this worksheet can be used to determine the gaps between these content needs and the currently available content. The results of this worksheet can be used to:
  • Provide a basis to selectively target certain projects and project outputs for proactive capture and knowledge engineering; and
  • Charter specific knowledge content development projects to fill needs that cannot be met simply through the post-processing of project outputs; and

Present a clear picture of all types of content important to the business area and requiring representation on the web site.
