Guideline: DesignFlow Process Redesign Workshop
Main Description
This paper describes a technique for conducting a process redesign workshop for any potential technology-enabled process.
 
The design workshop is the heart of a process redesign engagement. The objective of the process design workshop is to assist the client in identifying desired ‘To-Be’ process design points, which will address his business requirements. The design workshop is a joint effort with the client requiring full time commitment from all participants. This approach which combines the business knowledge and process expertise from the client with the leadership and technology expertise of our consultants has proven to be extremely effective in developing client solutions in days rather than weeks or months. It is imperative that before automating a process this workshop is run to ensure that we are not just automating a bad process, increasing the speed or consistency of a poor process.
 
The process redesign has five steps:
  • Preparation and Logistics
  • Focus Workshop 1: Understanding the Key Issues
  • Focus Workshop 2: Investigate Facts/Findings and Develop Root Cause Categories
  • Focus Workshop 3: New Process Requirements
  • Define Process Redesign Action Plan
