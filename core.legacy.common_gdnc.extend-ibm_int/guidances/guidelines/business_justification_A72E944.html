Guideline: Business Justification
Main Description

As part of an assessment or redesign engagement the client will frequently require a quantified analysis of the potential benefits of the proposed solution as part of the final report to enable them to make an informed business decision. This type of cost benefit analysis is focused on;

  1. The identification and quantification of all the benefits and cost related to the proposed solution alternatives
  2. Careful consideration of the benefits and costs by means of an economically oriented criteria or model
