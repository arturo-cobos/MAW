Check Items
Reflects the effectiveness of the testing activities

The Test Report should reflect the effectiveness of the activities proposed in the detailed test plans to meet the quality targets identified in the test strategy and master test plan.

Illustrates how the product has been tested

It should include enough information to illustrate how the product has been tested, including the rationale for understanding how conclusions and recommendations are reached.  Create a checklist from the Notation section to ensure all components are present.

Contains high-level descriptions of the test approach, methods or procedures

The report should contain enough high-level description of the test approach, methods, or procedures describing how the tests were conducted to provide context for the recommendations.  Further detail may be provided in appendices or referenced wherever it is stored and maintained.

Measures are traceable back to quantifiable requirements
Includeds recommendations for improvement based on test results

Recommendations for improving the product and the process should be provided based on the results of the tests.  The report should indicate the future (near-term and long-term) steps.
