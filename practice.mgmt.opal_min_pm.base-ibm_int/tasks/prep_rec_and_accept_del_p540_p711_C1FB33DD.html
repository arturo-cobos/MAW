Task: Prepare to receive and accept deliverable (IMSP540 and IMSP711)
This task will verify that the deliverable is ready to be delivered from a supplier and that the project is ready to receive them.
Purpose

To ensure the successful receipt of a deliverable, with the delivery occurring on time and with the expected content.

To ensure the formal successful acceptance of a deliverable from a supplier.

        Main Description

        This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern HD1: Prepare to receive delivery and HD2: Accept deliverable.

        This task will oversee the acceptance of a supplier's deliverables to the project, including acknowledging receipt of the deliverable, making a determination if the deliverable meets the acceptance criteria, and formally notifying the supplier of the acceptance or rejection.

        This task is launched when the project is approaching a due date for a supplier delivery. It is the time to make sure that everything will be ready to start acceptance work.

        This task can also be launched when the sponsor has committed to deliver products to the delivery organization and the project is approaching that delivery date.

        The key outputs of this task are the Issue Log or the Event Log.

        The task includes the following:

        • Assess supplier readiness to deliver
        • Assess project readiness to validate deliverable
        Steps
        Prepare to receive deliverable

        This step is based on the WWPMM work pattern HD1:  Prepare to receive deliverable.

        This step is launched when the project is approaching a due date for a supplier delivery. It is the time to make sure that everything will be ready to start acceptance work.

        This task can also be launched when the sponsor has committed to deliver products to the delivery organization and the project is approaching that delivery date.

        The key outputs of this task are the Issue Log or the Event Log.

        The task includes the following:

        • Assess supplier readiness to deliver
        • Assess project readiness to validate deliverable
        Accept deliverable

        This step is based on the WWPMM work pattern HD2: Accept deliverable.

        This step is launched when a subcontractor (or the sponsor) has just released a deliverable to the project (it may be a part of the final sponsor deliverable, a part of the technical environment, or of an intermediate work product). Acceptance work begins. Review the Agreement and Deliverable Definition to verify all clauses of the delivery and acknowledge receipt of the delivery. If delivery requirements are omitted, an issue is raised. Formal acknowledgment of delivery is recorded.

        Track progress of the acceptance work. This is done interactively by negotiating correction with the delivering group. Review the project team's validation findings on the supplier deliverable. If critical acceptance criteria are not being met or progress is slower than expected, corrective actions or issues might be needed. Findings are communicated to the supplier and prioritized. Corrective actions or issues should be raised if necessary.

        When the acceptance plan is complete and corrections verified, formally decide to accept or to reject the deliverable. Provide documentation of formal acceptance, acceptance with limitations, or rejection.

        The key outputs of this task are the Delivery Control Documentation or the Findings Log.  The Event Log can be used in place of the Findings Log and to track issues or actions. The Operational and Project Management Schedules are also adjusted, if necessary.

        The task includes the following:

        • Acknowledge receipt of deliverable
        • Initiate and oversee acceptance work
        • Determine acceptance or rejection and formally notify supplier
