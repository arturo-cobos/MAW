Task: Plan, verify and allocate office space and material (IMSP391)
This task will prepare for adding team members to the project by ensuring office space, material and the technical environment are available.
Purpose
To prepare for new team members to the project, facilitating their integration within the project team, and ensuring they have the resources to perform their work.

      Main Description

      This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern S6: Start staff.

      This task is part of the activity that should anticipate the date when team members are going to join the project for producing its deliverables, and should finish when it is clear that these team members are up to speed.

      Thus this task may be planned several times in the course of project execution.

      There are a number of practical and administrative steps that must be taken, regarding office space, allocation of material, appropriate access to the technical and PM environment, most of which are usually described in full detail in the managers general guidelines for the delivery organization.

      The focus in this task is on providing the new team members with the big picture of the project, the overview of the specific part of project execution they are going to be involved in, and on ensuring they are brought up to speed with the work they have to perform.

      The key outputs of this task are the Contact List which may be part of the Event Log, and the Asset Inventory.

      This task includes the following:

      • Verify and confirm the adequacy of physical office space and material to be provided
      • Ensure that the technical environment is available to any new project members, including subcontractor staff
      • Allocate the physical office space and material to the project staff
      • Document the assignment of any equipment to the project staff in the Asset Inventory
      • For the new staff, update and communicate the Contact List, which may be part of the Event Log
