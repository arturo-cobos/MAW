Task: Continually assess risk (IMSP421)
This task will assess the effectiveness of the risk mitigation actions that are implemented, identify new risks, revise risk priorities as needed, launch risk control actions when risks occur, and maintain the risk management plan.
Purpose
To ensure that risks and dependencies are monitored, assessed, and handled appropriately, to minimize adverse consequences and to keep the project under control.

        Main Description

        The key output of this task is the updated risk management plan which may be part of the project management system summary (PMSS).  Additional outputs may include the issue log, issue documents, action log, action control documents, risk occurrence documents, risk definition, or the event log, as needed.

        This task includes the following:

        • Assess effectiveness of the project risk management documentation and risk management plan by reviewing existing risks, dependencies and the results of any associated issues. External dependencies are added as risks
        • Identify new risks, assess impact and revise risk priorities
        • Launch risk control actions and update risk management plan (or PMSS)
