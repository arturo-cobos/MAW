Task: Update other project plans (IMSP391)
This task is designed to update other plans to ensure that they are complete and usable for execution of the project and are consistent with the operational schedule.
Purpose

        Main Description

        Update other plans to ensure that they are complete and usable for execution of the project and are consistent with the operational schedule.

        Project management plan to update which are not explicitly identified in other steps, include:

        • project quality plan
        • risk management plan
        • issue management plan
        • technical environment plan 
        • communications management plan 
        • stakeholders participation management plan
        • client data security & privacy plan
        • financial plan
        • estimation report
        • estimation checkpoint report
        • estimation factors report

        Note: In GBS PM Minimal (WWPMM) delivery process, all plans are documented in the Project Management System Summary (PMSS).

        The stakeholder participation management plan should be used in addition to the communications management plan to supplement information about stakeholders that is not captured in the communications management plan for complex projects or projects without strong support for organizational change.

        Ensure the Data Security & Privacy controls and activities are integration into your project management plans.

        In small organizations where control is centralized and there is strong support for organizational change, or the client is experienced with this type of change and has a defined strategy, the stakeholders' information, requirements, and their involvement can be documented in the communications management plan.
