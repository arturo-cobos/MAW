Task: Describe overall approach (IMSP395)
This task will document the overall approach, describing the major steps to deliver the target solution through the subsequent phases of the project, and will document the relevant risks for the overall approach.
Purpose
To further document the Project Definition, by providing the major steps to deliver the target solution, and to identify the risks so they can be understood and managed, enabling the project to reduce uncertainty, manage complexity and minimize the negative impact that the risks might have on the project.

        Main Description

        This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern D3: Describe overall approach.

        The target solution and the overall approach, or strategy, to achieve that solution may cover all of the sponsor's objectives, or it may cover a subset. For example, the sponsor may desire a low-cost solution with full functionality, while the target solution may trade off some complex functions to reduce cost.

        This task is primarily launched when, while defining the current project, it appears that only a subset of the target solution will be committed for this project and other portions will be completed in future projects.

        It is also worth launching this task when the whole target solution will be committed, but there is a need to reduce uncertainty and manage complexity in some aspects of the project by balancing development options such as building a prototype, piloting, or using an incremental approach in those aspects.

        The key outputs of this task are the Project Definition and the Viability Assessment. 

        This task includes the following:

        • Document approach and steps to deliver the target solution - Identify major work units, or candidates for sub projects, outcome and related decision points that cover, as necessary, designing, developing, and deploying the components of the target solution. Document them in the Project Definition.
        • Document risks associated with the approach - Identify, evaluate and document the residual risks associated with the proposed approach in the Viability Assessment (or optionally in the Risk Definition(s).)
