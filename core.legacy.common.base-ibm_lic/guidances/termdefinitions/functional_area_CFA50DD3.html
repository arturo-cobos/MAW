Term Definition: Functional Area (SOMA)
Main Description

A Functional Area in SOMA is used to denote logical grouping of business functionality in a particular Business Domain. It represents decomposition of a Business Domain.

For example: Claim Processing, Product Design, Logistics, Order Handling, Pharmacy Benefit Management
