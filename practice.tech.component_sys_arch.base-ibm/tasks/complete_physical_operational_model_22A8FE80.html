Task: Complete Physical Operational Model
This task completes the physical Operational Model.
Disciplines: Technical Architecture
Purpose
The purpose of this task is to create an initial draft of the final technology-dependent physical operational model at the detailed level.

    Main Description
    The focus of this task is on evaluating, selecting, configuring and validating physical products, which together satisfy the needs of the logical operational model.
    Steps
    Refine and complete the detailed physical node model
    Refine and complete the detailed physical specifications for nodes and connections
    Refine and complete the product selection:
    • for nodes
    • for technical components on nodes
    • for connections
    Refine and complete the Physical Operational Model
    Including completing the sizing of the nodes and connections
    Complete the configurations for nodes and connections
    Complete detailed designs where appropriate for nodes and connections
    Summarize the state of Physical Operational Model
    Key Considerations

    The Physical Operational Model is developed in parallel with the Logical Operational Model and is tightly coupled with both its development and also the development of the Component Model. Therefore decisions made when developing the Physical Operational Model may result in changes to both the Logical Operational Model and the Component Model.

    The state of the Physical Operational Model which needs to be reached at the end of this task is dependent on the context in which it is being developed, the level of confidence that needs to be achieved in the architecture and design, and the degree of accuracy of estimates both of product costs and resources to further develop the physical operational model.
