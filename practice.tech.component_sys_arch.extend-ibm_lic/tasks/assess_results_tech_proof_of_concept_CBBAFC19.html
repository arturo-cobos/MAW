Task: Assess the Results of the Technical Proof-of-Concept
Review the results of creating a technical prototype to determine if the unknowns have been properly addressed and the potential risks eliminated.
Purpose
To assess the results of prototyping in order to determine if additional work is required.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Technical Solution Architect
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Technical Proof of Concept
  • [Project Risk]
    • Risk Definition
    • Risk Occurrence Document
Optional:
  • None
Outputs
  • Viability Assessment
    Main Description
    Review the results of creating a technical prototype to determine if the unknowns have been properly addressed and the potential risks eliminated.  The findings and experience gained from building a Technical Proof of Concept not only benefit the current project, but potentially future projects facing similar situations. In addition to its primary goal of resolving key technical issues, the assessment may also point to other issues, or shed a light on complementary issues.
