Task Descriptor: Work with supplier (IMSP391 and IMSP711)
This task will prepare for suppliers to the project by working with them regarding plans, procedures and their readiness to meet expected quality and schedule obligations.

Main Description

The key output of this task is the Project Management Schedule.

This task includes the following:

  • Work with supplier
    • Verify readiness of the supplier regarding performing efficient work, and meeting expected quality and schedule obligations
    • Obtain supplier acceptance on statement of work and procedures
    • Ensure scope of the supplier's configuration management activities are included in the baseline audit for the project
    • Update the Project Management Schedule as needed
