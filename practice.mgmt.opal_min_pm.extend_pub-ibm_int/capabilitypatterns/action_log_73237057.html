Key Considerations

Once determined, actions are recorded in the Action Log, unless simply listed in meeting minutes or status reports, on small projects.

The Action Log is then updated when actions change status.

It is reviewed periodically to monitor execution of actions, and also to analyze trends in actions.

The Action Log must be up-to-date and the information recorded for each action must be consistent with the corresponding Action Control Documents, if any were created.
