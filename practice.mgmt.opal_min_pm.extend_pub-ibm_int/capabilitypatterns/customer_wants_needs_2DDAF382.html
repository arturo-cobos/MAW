Work Product Descriptor (Artifact): Customer Wants and Needs
Customer Wants and Needs form the functional value an offering provides to customers. They are requirements/desires, from the perspective of the customer, that define minimum expectations and/or ideal value at each interaction (touchpoint) between the business' customers and the business' products, services and/or processes.
