Role Descriptor: Sponsor
The senior executive who is the principal project stakeholder, and who has made the commitment and has the authority to resolve major project issues, approve project expenditures, plans and organization, and who is ultimately responsible for delivering the business objective to the enterprise.

Main Description

This role represents the principal project stakeholder in a project.  The role makes the resource commitment to the project and has the authority to resolve major issues, approve expenditures, plans and organization.  This role is ultimately responsible for delivering the project's business objectives to the enterprise.
