Activity: Assess estimating basis (IMSP421 and IMSP525)
This activity compares the estimating basis used to initially estimate the workload of the project or its phase, with the trends of the actual workload spent on work units that are completed or in progress. Adjustments are then made to assignments, estimates to complete the work, and project plans.
