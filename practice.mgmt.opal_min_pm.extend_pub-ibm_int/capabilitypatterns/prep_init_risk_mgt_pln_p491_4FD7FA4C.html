Task Descriptor: Prepare initial risk management plan (IMSP491)
This task will prepare for risk management, identify and analyze risks, plan risk responses, and will manage and document risks.

Main Description

This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern P4: Finalize sponsor agreement.

For solutions that do not require a Risk Management Review, the Risk Management Plan will need review by the Solution Manager or the Project Manager.

The key outputs of this task are the Risk Management Plan which may be part of the Project Management System Summary, and the Risk Definition which may be part of the Risk Management Plan.

This task includes the following:

  • Prepare for risk management
    • Determine potential sources of risks
    • Determine the risk classifications to be used for grouping risks based on shared characteristics which aid in evaluating impact and probability, and in consolidating risks for mitigation and contingency planning
    • Define qualification criteria for the purpose of identifying and analyzing potential risks
    • Establish a risk management strategy
  • Identify and analyze risks
    • Receive or identify, review, and approve or reject risk
    • Assess and classify risk, and create risk definition
    • Assign risk owner and ensure analysis completed
    • Obtain risk reviews and agreements as needed
    • Prioritize risks
    • Rank risks
    • Update risk definitions
  • Plan risk response
    • Analyze risks
    • Allocate risk reserves
    • Identify mitigation countermeasures and define mitigation plan
    • Define contingency plan
    • Create Issue management plan along side the Risk Management Plan, Plan and prepare for the WW QA Technical and Delivery Assessment (TDA), if applicable
