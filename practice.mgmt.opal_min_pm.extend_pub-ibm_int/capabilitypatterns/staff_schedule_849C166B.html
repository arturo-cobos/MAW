Description of when and which staff members or skills are or will be brought onto the project and released by the project.
Based on Method Work Product: Staff Schedule
Main Description

The Staff Schedule contains detailed information about the availability of the members of the project team.

Key Considerations

The Staff Schedule is created as the project the team is built up during Starting and is updated as the planned availability of individuals changes during the course of the project.

The project manager verifies that:

  • The Staff Schedule is always complete and up to date.
  • The Operational schedule remains consistent with the Staff Schedule.
