Role Descriptor: Project Quality Analyst
This role determines whether or not project quality compliance criteria are met.

Main Description

The role is responsible for performing internal process and product compliance reviews at intervals defined in project quality plans.

This role is responsible for tracking and reporting on:

  • Providing consultancy in the development and review of project plans, and the implementation of process asset library standards, policies and procedures 
  • Verifying adherence to process asset library standards, policies and procedures 
  • Verifying that all planned inspections and tests have taken place prior to release of project deliverables
  • Identifying, reporting and tracking any project non-conformances to agreed standards, plans and procedures 
  • Identifying, reporting and tracking any corrective or preventive action required on the project 
  • Identifying any corrective or preventive action required in the process asset library 
  • Supporting continuous quality improvement on the project

A Project Manager or Team Leader may assign other tasks, for example, to conduct quality reviews, however these delegated tasks are not part of this role.
