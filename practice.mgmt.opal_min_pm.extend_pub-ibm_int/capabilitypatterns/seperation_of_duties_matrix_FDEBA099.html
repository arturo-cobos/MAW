Work Product Descriptor (Artifact): Separation of Duties Matrix
This artifact describes responsibilities by role, and provides visibility to role/responsibility combinations that should belong to separate individuals according to accepted separation of duties criteria.
Based on Method Work Product: Separation of Duties Matrix

Main Description

In instances where a separation of duties conflict is unavoidable (for example, on a small project) this artifact is used to record the secondary controls that are put in place to manage the risk associated with the separation of duties conflict.
