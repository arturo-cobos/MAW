Main Description

Describes the solution and quantifies the business value acknowledged by the client for the IBM solution being proposed. Focused on building the client's commitment to implement the solution(s), this artifact highlights and differentiates how our solution addresses the client's stated problem and value drivers to achieve the desired business capabilities and benefit. Update this work product throughout the CVM phases to capture the client’s evolving acknowledgement and expectation of value from the emerging solution.

Key Considerations

A strong value proposition is needed to impact all client buying decision occasions but the Buying Behavior will influence the detail of the content included in of the value proposition.

The Justification template portrays the client’s investment and results over time; the Value Capture template portrays actual versus expected results.  Use the client’s financial measures to present the investment and results information in both the Business Justification and Value Capture sections.
