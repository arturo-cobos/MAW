Main Description

The Communications Management Plan defines the regular meetings, reporting, and other communications that take place during the project. It identifies the type of information, the medium for communicating, and the audience to whom it is directed.

Key Considerations

The content of the Communications Management Plan is selected by reviewing the key project objectives with focus on those areas where communication and information sharing will make a positive contribution to the project success.

A preliminary Communication Management Plan is built during project Defining and Planning activities. The plan is then expanded at the start of execution and maintained or adjusted throughout the life of the project.

The Communications Management Plan is developed with the involvement of appropriate stakeholders. It is typically approved by the project manager and validated by the sponsor and/or IBM management.
