Task Descriptor: Create financial plan and prepare business case (IMSP491)
This task will ensure that all project costs are accounted for and that the financial plan is updated and the benefits can be realized.

Main Description

This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern P4: Finalize sponsor agreement.

The key output of this task is the Financial Plan, which may be part of the Project Management System Summary.  Inputs may include the Human Resource Plan, which may also be part of the Project Management System Summary.

This task includes the following:

  • Consolidate costs and additional cost elements using all plans, such as:
    • Salaries and related costs
    • Contractor costs
    • Travel and subsistence expenses
    • Supplier stage payments
    • Supplier expenses
    • Hardware costs
    • Software costs
    • Training costs
    • Office space charges
    • Material costs
  • Document related assumptions using all plans
    • Identifies the planned revenue schedule including the amounts and milestones associated with payments from the client.
    • Identifies the planned expenditure schedule, including payments to suppliers, labor costs and other direct expenses.
    • Plans how the costs, identified during the generation of the cost estimate, will be spread over time.
  • When appropriate, work with the pricing or finance organizations to determine a project income schedule that will ensure project cost recovery over time, and will be submitted to the sponsor's agreement
  • Update Financial Plan, which may be part of the Project Management System Summary
  • When appropriate, analyze the business value of the solution from the key client stakeholders who either benefit or must adopt the solution.  Describe the business value in the Value Proposition.
