Key Considerations

The initial Conditions of Satisfaction should be collected from the sponsor during the up-front sales process.  At the beginning of the delivery phase you should check with the sales team to find out what expectations were set during the sales phase.

Use this list of expectations to revalidate and refine the sponsor's expectations and priorities during the beginning of the delivery phase.  This will enable you to capture any changes in their expectations and priorities and ensure that you understand what is important to your sponsor.

Routine checkpoints with the sponsor during delivery and at the close of the engagement will keep the project team on track to a successful project.

Development Approach

  • Collect/verify sponsor expectations
  • Establish actions to meet expectations
  • Periodically review progress agains expectations
  • Capture sponsor's input regarding expectations
