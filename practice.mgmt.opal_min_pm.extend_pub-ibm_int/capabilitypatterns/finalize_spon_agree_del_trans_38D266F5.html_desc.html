Activity: Finalize Sponsor Agreement and Prepare for Delivery Team Transition
This activity will consolidate the proposed plans and commitments, and review them with the client with the goal of obtaining their approval of the proposal.
Extends: Finalize Sponsor Agreement and Prepare for Delivery Team Transition
DescriptionWork Breakdown StructureTeam AllocationWork Product Usage
Purpose
To consolidate the proposed plans and documents into a single repository so that all relevant material can be easily distributed to, or accessed by, people who provide approvals, and to facilitate the understanding of and the approval of, the proposal by the client.
Relationships
Parent Activities
  • Planning
Description

This activity includes the following:

  • Package proposal components and review with client
    • Consolidate the plans and commitments into a single repository so that all relevant material can be easily distributed to, or accessed by, those who will provide approval
    • Conduct a proposal review by the client to ensure that all deliverables have been identified and the client understands what will be delivered upon project completion
  • Obtain client's agreement for proposed agreement and baseline
    • Negotiate with the client if approval is not received
    • Obtain client's agreement for the proposal
    • Advise appropriate parties of the decision
    • Baseline all project plans and other documents that are to be managed and controlled
