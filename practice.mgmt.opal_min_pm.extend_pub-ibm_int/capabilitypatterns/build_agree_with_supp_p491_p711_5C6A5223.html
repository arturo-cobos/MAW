Task Descriptor: Build agreement with supplier (IMSP491 and IMSP711)
This task will negotiate the commitments of a supplier for a project and formalize them in a supplier agreement

Main Description

The key output of this task is the supplier agreement.

This task includes the following:

  • Build agreement with supplier
    • Determine how to manage the supplier's performance and the key checkpoints to control progress and quality
    • Identify the internal (project) and external dependencies

    Note: An external dependency is a risk to the project and must be managed.

    • Identify the inspection and acceptance procedures
      • Review the statement of work, planned milestones, and obligations that constitute the agreement; identify and resolve conflicts. Ensure that any unique or client specific information security requirements are addressed in the supplier statement of work or purchase order
    • Obtain commitments from supplier
