Task Descriptor: Review the SOW/COS with the Client (IMSP391)
This task involves meeting with the client and understanding what is important to them, identifying their expectations across the life of an engagement.

Main Description

The key output of this task is the Conditions of Satisfaction.

This task includes the following:

  • Review the agreement (statement of work (SOW)) with the client
  • Identify the client's conditions of satisfaction (COS) through on-going dialogue with the client in order to meet/exceed their expectations
  • Update the conditions of satisfaction (COS), which are reviewed and revised at the beginning of the project to ensure their expectations are met during delivery
  • Document COS in the appropriate tool.  Reference IGSDF - Using GBS CRM on Engagements for links to your local GBS CRM Playbook for tools guidance for the appropriate COS tool for your community.
  • Communicate the conditions of satisfaction (COS) to the delivery team
