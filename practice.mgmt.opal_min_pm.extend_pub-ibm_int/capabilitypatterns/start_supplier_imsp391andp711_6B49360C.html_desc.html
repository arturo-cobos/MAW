Activity: Start supplier (IMSP391 and IMSP711)
This activity will prepare for suppliers to the project by developing and supplying orientation material, ensuring that needed resources are provided to the supplier, working with them regarding plans and procedures, and by ensuring the supplier assimilates into the project successfully.
Extends: Start supplier (IMSP391 and IMSP711)
DescriptionWork Breakdown StructureTeam AllocationWork Product Usage
Purpose
To provide a supplier with adequate orientation, material and support, to ensure consistency of its plans and procedures with the overall project, and to verify that the work on the supplier's sub-project has gotten off to a good start.
Relationships
Parent Activities
  • Starting
Description

This activity includes the following:

  • Prepare the orientation material for supplier
  • Ensure resources are provided to supplier
  • Work with supplier
    • Verify readiness of the supplier regarding performing efficient work, and meeting expected quality and schedule obligations
    • Obtain supplier acceptance on statement of work and procedures
    • Ensure scope of the supplier's configuration management activities are included in the baseline audit for the project
  • Ensure supplier assimilates into project successfully
