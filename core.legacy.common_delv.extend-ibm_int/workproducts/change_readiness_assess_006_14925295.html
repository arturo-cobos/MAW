Deliverable: Change Readiness Assessment Report Deliverable (DCD 006)
This report is based upon an initial scoping of the changes likely to occur with the project and their probable impact.
Purpose
The purpose of this deliverable is to document the attitudes and issues that may arise from an organizational standpoint due to the changes inherent in the project. It also is designed to uncover issues concerning the clientâ€™s IT infrastructure and processes which may impact the successful completion of the project.
Main Description
The Change Readiness Assessment report deliverable assesses the organization's commitment, readiness, and ability to accept and sustain the changes that will be required by an initiative. The report highlights issues that will impede change relative to:
  • Sponsorship of the change
  • Attitudes to change formed by past experiences
  • Culture of the organization
The Change Readiness Assessment report also concentrates on:
  • Executive Sponsors
  • Key Stakeholders
  • Change Management Issues
  • IT Readiness Issues
  • Change Management Strategy

Packaging Instructions

Draw findings and conclusions from all data sources. Prioritize the issues by severity. When writing the report, include quotes from interviews and focus groups. If an issue applies only to a particular segment of the population, ensure that this is communicated clearly in the report. Many excellent and diverse examples of change readiness assessment reports are available in ICM AssetWeb.

Develop recommended strategies and actions to enhance change enablers, eliminate or reduce barriers, ensure adequate sponsorship, build stakeholder commitment and capability to support change, and manage resistance. Include the time frame in which the actions should occur. As you develop recommendations, ground them in basic change management concepts - the change process, capacity to assimilate change, and critical success factors for change.
