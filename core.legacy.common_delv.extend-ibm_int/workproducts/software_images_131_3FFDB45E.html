Deliverable: Software Images (DCD 131)
Purpose
The IBM tested and client approved software images are used by the integration center to place an image on the equipment prior to deployment.
Main Description
A customized software image is a client specific equipment image that contains all relevant software and settings which are common to their situation. Depending on the situation, and the other technology employed by the client, images may contain one, two, or tier three applications. Multiple images may be required by the client to match department, local, or user group profiles. A software image is a "snapshot" of a piece of equipment and as such is hardware specific. Multiple images may be required by the client if more than one machine type or configuration is being deployed. The custom software image may be stored on CD medium, servers, or other storage medium.
