Deliverable: Change Management Plan (DCD 239)
The Change Management Plan deliverable packages the content from Organization Transition Plan work product into a deliverable for project sponsor sign-off.
Purpose

The purpose of this deliverable is to obtain client signoff of the Change Management Plan which will guide the remaining change management tasks on the project.
