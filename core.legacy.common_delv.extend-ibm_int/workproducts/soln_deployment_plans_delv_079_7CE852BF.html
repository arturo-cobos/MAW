Deliverable: Solution Deployment Plans Deliverable (DCD 079)
The Solution / Deployment Plans deliverable details the technical aspects of managing the deployment of a solution as well as supporting it once deployed and specifically addresses these in detail as applies to a given release of the solution.
Purpose

The purpose of this document is to finalize solution and deployment plans for the release. This includes – 

  • Completion of the systems management plan to provide the basis for selection:
  • of system management tools
  • of systems management processes including security processes
  • definition of job descriptions, based on identified roles, to support the IT system.
  • Revision of the software distribution plan to guide and control the roll-out of the release to all client locations.
  • Identifcation and implementation of security controls to harden the production environment in which the solution will reside.
  • Definition of the physical packaging of the executables and user support materials.
  • Identification of all relevant assets, which the maintenance team requires from the development team, as application turnover materials.
Main Description
This document covers some or all of the following topics as may apply to a solution and as specifically may apply to a solution release.
  • IT Services Plan
  • Software Distribution Plan
  • Deployment Plan
  • Production Environment Hardening Plan
  • Systems Management and Software Plans
  • Physical Packaging
  • Maintenance Turnover Materials
 
Upon its first drafting, this deliverable extends selected solution plans developed in the project design phases defining an overall strategy and description of the deployment approach to be used for a solution, in general, and detailing those that specifically pertain to a given solution release. Its content may be further detailed, as required, to include potential clarifications, revisions, or defect corrections identified during the Build Cycle phase solution construction and testing activities conducted for the initial as well as future releases defined for a solution.

Packaging InstructionsThe following work products contribute to the various deliverable topics or sections.
 
Upon its initial drafting, some of the work products may be completed at a high level as applied to the overall solution, but are detailed, as required, for the initial release of the solution. They may be iterated and more fully developed as applies to each future solution release in subsequent release phases (Micro Design, Build Cycle, and Deployment) of the engagement as the need for greater detail is identified.
 
The use and/or contents of the work products may vary from project to project. Also on short engagement utilizing a rapid development approach the Software Distribution Plan (ARC115) work product will not be included as part of the “IT Services Plan” topic.
