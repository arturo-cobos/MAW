Deliverable: Business Architecture Context (DCD 038)
Purpose
The content of this deliverable provide a clear foundation for the definition of an enterprise-level business and application architecture, which will support the desired business strategy, directions and capabilities.
 
Without this foundation, the findings and recommendations of the subsequent business and application architecture activities, will not have a sound business case against which the business can make appropriate future design and acquisition decisions. Neither will it have a sound basis for measuring the effectiveness and appropriateness of the architectures as they are implemented.
Main Description
The Business Architecture Context deliverable is normally produced as an output from phase 3 of the Business Architecture - Quickstart engagement model.
 
This content of this deliverable should include:
  • A reminder of the overall scope, objectives and deliverables of the engagement
  • A summary of trends about the marketplace, industry and technology, relevant to the client industry/business environment
  • A summary of specific information about the client, in terms of their market and competitive position, the key issues they are facing and other expectations and perceptions they may have relating to the above
  • An overview of the key capabilities and resources/enablers which will be needed to implement the desired business strategy and directions
