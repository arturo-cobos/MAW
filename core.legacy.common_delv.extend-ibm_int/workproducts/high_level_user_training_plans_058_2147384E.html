Deliverable: High-Level User Training Plans (DCD 058)
This deliverable defines the high level plans for user training and contains key training-related topics. It is a continuation and refinement of work completed and outlined earlier in the project. Further refinements and detail will be added as part of the detailed design efforts.
Purpose
The purpose of this deliverable is to present IBM’s recommendations for preparing the customer’s staff to move forward with the design and development of the solution, and to gain a shared understanding betweenthe customer and IBM of the training and education needed to meet the solutions requirements for support and operation.
Main Description
This document should contain the following:
  • The completed audit of specific employee capabilities required to carry out the activities required to operate and support the solution. The report will include a matrix linking employees to their skills and skill levels. A gap analysis will indicate top priorities to be addressed.
  • The recommended plan for developing the end user training and support materials that will be required for the Solution deployment. This document also will present the rollout plan for the End User training, including the training of the instructors, the pilot classes and the updating of the training material, if necessary.
