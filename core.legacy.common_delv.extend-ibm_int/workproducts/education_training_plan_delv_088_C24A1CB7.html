Deliverable: Education and Training Plan Deliverable (DCD 088)
The Education and Training Plan Deliverable identifies in detail all activities and responsibilities related to the development, delivery, and evaluation of client education and training for the planned implementation.
Purpose

The purpose of this document is to provide the basis for managing, monitoring, and evaluating training delivery - by articulating a specific, detailed plan for the development and delivery of all client training related to the implementation of the transformation project. The plan enables the execution of an education and training strategy.

The Education and Training Plan Deliverable may become part of the overall project plan for the entire engagement.

Main Description

The activities detailed in this deliverable include all training identified for all communities within the organization - including IT, process and organization. Once identified these activities are sequenced, prioritized, scoped, staffed, and resourced.

The output is a detailed project plan and materials specifying activities, roles, and responsibilities for IBM, client, and other resources.

Components of the education and training plan deliverable include the following:

  • High-level curriculum development
  • Participant education and training schedule
  • Training the trainers
  • IT infrastructure
  • Training instance
  • Delivery methods
  • Deliver training
  • Evaluate training
  • Post-Change support

Based on this design the Project Plan will also be updated and presented. The plan will then be used to control the build phase of the engagement.

Deliverable Properties
Deliverable Parts
  • Education and Training Plan
  • Education and Training Strategy
  • Project Definition
  • User Support Specifications
Packaging Instructions

The Detailed Education and Training Plan Deliverable content description assembles the defined work product outputs into a final presentation for client executives and key decision makers. The description provides a summary of key topics, outputs, findings and recommendations for the courseware. 

The format is a single document containing fifty to one hundred pages and a slide presentation. The document is either delivered as a printed and bound hardcopy (preferably printed in color) or in electronic files (.pdf, word processing, presentation slide formats) via CD-ROM, diskette and/or email.

The inputs used to develop the Education and Training Plan Deliverable include the following:

ENG 343 Project Definition is used to understand the engagement objectives and how they affect the training solution.

ORG 017 User Support Specifications is used for knowing the configuration of the standard package, including the package extensions that have been developed, the reports and interfaces that have been developed, and how a combination of these will be used to support the client's business processes. The specifications define the boundaries between the different units of user documentation, as well as content, audience, purpose, standards to be used, and the language(s).

ORG 312 Education and Training Strategy is used for identifying all staff affected by the implementing the new application, training requirements for each, sequence of training, delivery methods, and standards for success. The strategy is the basis for the plan; the plan supports the strategy.

ORG 311 Education and Training Plan materials serve as the basis for the content that make up this deliverable.
