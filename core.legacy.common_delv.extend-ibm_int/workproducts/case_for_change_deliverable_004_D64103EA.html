Deliverable: Case for Change Deliverable (DCD 004)
The Case for Change Deliverable should summarize at a high level the current situation of the client and make it clear to the client why they need to change, in what areas change should be considered, and what steps should be followed to pursue the change.
Purpose
The purpose of this deliverable is to communicate to the client the findings of the Assessment and Recommendation Phase and to justify to the client why they need the proposed solution. It is to show to the client the critical issues they currently face, the potential opportunities they stand to gain from the solution, and the recommended next steps.
Main Description
The scope of this deliverable includes the following:

  • An overall view that of the client environment that depicts the critical issues the client is currently facing and how the solution will address the issues.
  • One or more pages of specific, clearly stated, opportunities that are aligned with the clients critical issues.
  • A logical description explaining how each of the change opportunities relates to the conclusions that were formed by testing the hypotheses against the findings derived from the current business and environment.
  • A high-level estimation of the potential or expected benefits, investments and risks for each major opportunity, or group of opportunities.
  • Recommendations on what actions should be taken to investigate and validate each of the identified opportunities.
  • Tentative project plan for next phases.
