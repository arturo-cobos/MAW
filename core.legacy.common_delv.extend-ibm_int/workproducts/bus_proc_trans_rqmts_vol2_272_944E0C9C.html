Deliverable: Business Process Transformation Requirements (Volume 2) (DCD 272)
The Business Process Transformation Requirements (Volume 2) is a deliverable to the client documenting the requirements that will be used to redefine of the future state process and environment.
Purpose
The purpose of this document is to collect all the requirements that will be used to redesign the process and it’s supporting applications so that they meet the needs of the business.
Main Description
Sections of the Volume 2 include:
  • Process Objectives
  • Target Process Metrics
  • Root Causes
  • New Process Requirements
  • Critical Success Factors
