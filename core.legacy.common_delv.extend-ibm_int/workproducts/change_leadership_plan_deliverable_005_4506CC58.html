Deliverable: Change Leadership Plan Deliverable (DCD 005)
IBM’s approach to Sponsorship consists of a series of activities to identify and mobilize the sponsors who guide and support the implementation project. Analysis of data gathered in interviews and assessments provide key findings, concerns and recommendations that are incorporated into a Sponsorship Plan.
Purpose
IBM’s Change Leadership or Sponsorship Program combines a data collection methodology used to assess leadership commitment, readiness and willingness to lead the changes that will result from the implementation with tools to help strengthen their ability to initiate and sustain a successful implementation. The results of the data collection, alone or in conjunction with the Executive Visioning and/or Organization Readiness Assessment results, form the basis of a sponsorship plan and a transition management strategy and plan.  The data also serves as input into the transition management plan.
Deliverable Properties
Deliverable Parts
  • Change Leadership Plan
  • Change Readiness Assessment
Packaging InstructionsActivities:
 
1. Conduct Sponsorship Interviews
 
2. Analyze interview data
 
3. Mobilize sponsors
 
4. Develop Sponsorship Plan
 
5. Provide for sponsor assessment
 
6. Provide input to transition management
 
7. Support sponsors
 
Outputs
  1. Sponsorship Plan. The Sponsorship plan defines the framework, roles and activities necessary for consistent and committed leadership.
  2. Sponsorship Development Plan. The Sponsorship Development Plan documents activities and methods to strengthen and sponsorship capability gaps. It also defines feedback mechanisms that can be adopted.
 
The final document will be delivered to the customer in a reproducible format; use of proprietary file formats that may not be supported on the client’s platform should be avoided. The final report may also be delivered in hard copy format printed in a single, bound document.
