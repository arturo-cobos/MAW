Deliverable: Courseware Materials (DCD 066)
The courseware material deliverable content is developed using information garnered from the Education and Training Strategy.
Purpose
The purpose of this deliverable is to describe the scope of the training initiative. An outline of what will be developed and the method for development is included in the content. Curriculum covering the transactions mapped to roles and the delivery method are all reviewed and discussed. The deliverable is presented to establish a clear understanding of the customerâ€™s expectations for training and to indicate that the education and training plan will be supported.
Main Description
The courseware material deliverable content is developed using information garnered from the Education and Training Strategy. The document identifies the courseware that will be produced and the guidelines for producing and delivering the courseware to the client. The courseware deliverable includes the following elements:
  • Transactions Mapped to Roles and Courses
  • Delivery Method per Course
  • Published Curriculum
  • Document Change Control Process
  • Quick Reference Guides, Help Files and Job Aids
  • Courseware Materials
  • Education and Training Evaluation

Based on this courseware scope the Project Plan will also be updated and presented. The plan will then be used to control the deployment phase of the engagement.

Deliverable Properties
Deliverable Parts

  • User Support Specifications
Packaging Instructions

The Courseware Materials content description assembles the defined work product outputs into a final presentation for client executives and key decision makers. The description provides a summary of key topics, outputs, findings and recommendations for the courseware. 
The format is a single document containing fifty to one hundred pages and a slide presentation. The document is either delivered as a printed and bound hardcopy (preferably printed in color) or in electronic files (.pdf, word processing, presentation slide formats) via CD-ROM, diskette and/or email.

The inputs used to develop the Courseware Materials deliverable include the following:

ENG 343 Project Definition is used to understand the engagement objectives and how they affect the training solution.

ORG 153 User Support Specifications is used for knowing the configuration of the standard package, including the package extensions that have been developed, the reports and interfaces that have been developed, and how a combination of these will be used to support the client's business processes. The specifications define the boundaries between the different units of user documentation, as well as content, audience, purpose, standards to be used, and the language(s).

ORG 311 Education and Training Plan is used to identify activities and responsibilities related to the development, delivery, and evaluation of client education and training for the planned implementation.

ORG 312 Education and Training Strategy is used for identifying all staff affected by the implementing the new application, training requirements for each, sequence of training, delivery methods, and standards for success.

ORG 401 Learning Solution Materials provides the bulk of the materials that is then packaged to make up this deliverable.
