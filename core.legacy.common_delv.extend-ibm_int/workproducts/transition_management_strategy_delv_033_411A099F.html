Deliverable: Transition Management Strategy Deliverable (DCD 033)
The Transition Management Strategy summarizes needed changes and defines the overall approach that an organization will take to implement its new capabilities as well as its required direction.
Purpose

The purpose of this deliverable is to enable successful transformation by using a high-level strategy to:

  • Identify the changes that are needed, and recommend the most effective approach to implementing the desired business solutions, and
  • Reduce the depth and duration of disruption to the business by imposing consistency and direction.

The Transition Management Strategy facilitates acceptance by ensuring that the transition is well thought through by both the client team and the consulting team, and that critical success factors are in place.

Without a strategy for managing the transition, the likely results are:

  • Failure to achieve the business objectives on schedule and on budget.
  • Failure to attain the projected ROI.
  • Confusion about responsibilities, timing, and priorities for the initiatives.
  • Competitive disadvantage.
