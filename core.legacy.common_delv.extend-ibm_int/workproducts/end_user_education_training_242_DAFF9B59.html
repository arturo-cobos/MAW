Deliverable: End User Education and Training (DCD 242)
The End-User Education and Training deliverable packages the content from the end-user education and training work products into a formal deliverable for project sign-off.
Purpose
The purpose of this deliverable is to obtain client sign-off of the End-User Education and Training work products.
