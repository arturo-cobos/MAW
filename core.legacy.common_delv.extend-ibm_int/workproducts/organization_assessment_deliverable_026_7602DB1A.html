Deliverable: Organization Assessment Deliverable (DCD 026)
This deliverable is an assessment of the current organization within the scope of the project against the following design principles: alignment to business goals and human performance management. It also documents the potential impact of the weaknesses and strengths of the organization on the transformation project and identifies priorities for implementation.
Purpose
The purpose of developing an Organization Assessment Deliverable is to identify alignment opportunities for organizational structure and roles that are required to achieve the objectives of the project. This includes identifying organizational elements within the scope of this project and determining the level of organizational alignment to business goals. In addition, a risk/value analysis of the future organizational scope, which will document the magnitude of the organizational changes, may be developed, along with a recommended approach.
