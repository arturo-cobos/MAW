Deliverable: Business Impact Assessment (DCD 165)
This deliverable is a report that provides a high level assessment of the organization's readiness for change.
Purpose
The purpose of this deliverable is to provide an assessment of the customer organization’s relative ability to adjust to the changes that will be required to successfully build, deploy and support the new solution. The results of this assessment could indicate a need to adjust the intended scope of the solution.
Main Description
The Business Impact Assessment is a report that provides a high level assessment of the organization’s readiness for the changes that will occur in the customer’s organization as a result of implementing the proposed solution and identifies the primary issues and risks associated with the changes.
 
This deliverable contains the following content:
  • A description of the anticipated organizational and cultural impacts
  • A description of the potential business impacts
  • An itemization of the existing Training and Support process that will be affected, and the expected efforts needed to modify the existing support materials.
  • An assessment of the readiness of the organization to persevere though the anticipated level of change

The following work products are the inputs to the various report topics or sections. Some work products may be extensions and/or revisions to previously generated work products. Some work product may be separate instances of work product (e.g., Test Plan for Unit Test and Test Plan for Integration Test)

Deliverable Properties
External Description

The Business Impact Assessment is a report that provides a high level assessment of the organization’s readiness for the changes that will occur in the customer’s organization as a result of implementing the proposed solution and identifies the primary issues and risks associated with the changes.

The purpose of this deliverable is to provide an assessment of the customer organization’s relative ability to adjust to the changes that will be required to successfully build, deploy and support the new solution. The results of this assessment could indicate a need to adjust the intended scope of the solution.

The deliverable / report will contain the following content:

  • A description of the anticipated organizational and cultural impacts
  • A description of the potential business impacts
  • An itemization of the existing Training and Support process that will be affected and the expected efforts needed to modify the existing support materials
  • An assessment of the readiness of the organization to persevere though the anticipated level of change

The content in this deliverable will provide input to the Solution Strategy Report deliverable in this phase, and also influence the Solution Management Strategy and End User Training & Support deliverables in later phases.

The intent of this deliverable is to provide a high level assessment of the level of preparedness of the organization to manage the anticipated level of organizational change needed to develop, deploy and support the proposed solution. The Business Impact Assessment is not intended to provide the same level of detail that would typically accompany a full Change Management effort.
