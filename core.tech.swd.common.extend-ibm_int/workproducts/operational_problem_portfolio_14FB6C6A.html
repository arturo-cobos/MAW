Artifact: Operational Problem Portfolio (ART 0545)
The log is used to register and track all problems reported by users.
Domains: Maintenance
Work Product Kinds: Inventory
Purpose
The artifact ensures that there is a centralized view of the problems open, the status of each one, who is responsible for resolving it and the key dates related to the problemâ€™s lifecycle.
Description
Brief OutlineThis artifact contains a record for each problem instance that has been reported. It may be used as a consolidated log for an organization or as the log for a unique business area or application team.  It may contain open, in-progress and closed problems.  It helps in prioritizing and distributing the work within a team. It can ensure a common view of the application status between the Project Manager (PM) and the Client. It captures data which allows the PM to track his performance and ensure the service supplied is in line with the customer requirement.

Impact of not having

Consequences of not having this work product include:

  • Lack of visibility of the application problems.
  • Difficulties to adequately plan resources to resolve problems in a timely manner.
  • Lack of data to show that client requirements are being fulfilled.
  • Lack of history data relevant for the yearly maintenance plan review of the following years

The result is a lack of efficiency and productivity which impact customer satisfaction.
Reasons for not needingThis work product is generally replaced by a tool in areas where there is a large volume of problems reported or a medium to large team resolving the problems.
