Deliverable: Business Model (DEL 0538)
This deliverable captures the detailed business domains and their functions and processes relevant to the scope of the specific release of the solution.
Purpose

The purpose of this document is to:

  • Establish a common basis for understanding client approaches to industry challenges
  • Describe business domains and their functional areas relevant to the current solution
  • Provide sufficient detailed documentation on the business processes to complete the analysis and design of the specific solution
  • Identify the any detailed modifications to the business processes that will be needed as to implement the individual solution

Deliverable Properties
External Description

The Business Model is a report that documents the business environment relevant to the solution.

The purpose of the report is to provide the sufficient documentation of the business domains and their functions and processes relevant to the solution that are needed to support the determination of solution requirements. This includes business processes and rules that will be automated by the solution.

The report will include the following details relevant to this solution:

  • documentation of the existing operational business processes, with any detailed changes that are anticipated
  • documentation of the applicable business rules that would need to be incorporated into the release

Packaging Instructions

The final document will be delivered to the customer in a reproducible format.  The actual models may have originally been created using a diagramming tool, but should be included as a finished portion of the report for final delivery.  The final report may also be delivered in hard copy format printed in a single, bound document.

The artifact parts of this deliverable are the inputs to the various report topics or sections.  These artifacts may not all be fully elaborated (or completed in terms of both depth and breadth) at this time.  Some may be iterated and more fully developed in later phases of the engagement as the need for greater detail is identified. It is important that the deliverable accurately conveys the level of completeness for each artifact included.

The concept of completeness of an artifact instance depends largely on whether the artifact will be finished in this phase of the project, or iterated for additional work in later phases.  If the artifact is to be iterated later, it will only be partially complete at the end of this phase.  Regardless of whether each artifact instance is fully or partially completed at this time, the content of the artifacts should be fully included in the deliverable being produced in order to reflect the current level of known detail.
