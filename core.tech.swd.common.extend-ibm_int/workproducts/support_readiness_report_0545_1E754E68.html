Deliverable: Support Readiness Report (DEL 0545)
This deliverable provides plans and materials for supporting solution training and operations.
Purpose
The purpose of this deliverable is to provide customer management with an external assessment of whether the identified support organizations and procedures are ready for production cutover, and to make recommendations for actions that should be performed prior to cutover.

Main Description
The Support Readiness Report is an assessment of the general preparedness of the various support organizations and processes to support production operations of the solution.
 
The deliverable / report may contain the following content, as appropriate:
  • Education and Training Summary
  • User Support Materials Availability
  • Business Operations Support Readiness
  • Help Desk Readiness
  • Technical Performance and Availability Support
  • Geographic Locations readiness
  • Summary Recommendations
