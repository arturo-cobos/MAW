Task: Identify Security Processes
Disciplines: Security and Privacy
Purpose
The purpose of this task is to identify the security processes that will be necessary around the solution and obtain the buy-in of the people who will be implementing and supporting those processes.

      Main Description
      Although the project team will create and deliver a secure solution, any solution implemented will need maintenance over time. Day-to-day secure processes and procedures will be necessary in order to ensure the timely maintenance and support of the finished solution.

      During this task, the project team should identify the persons in operations who will have to support the solution. The team should gain an understanding of the way operations currently functions and devise the support processes with the involvement of the operations/delivery team. The team should both document identified processes and begin modeling the possible processes. Further iterations in Macro and Micro Design will detail the processes out.
