Concept: Method Adoption Workshop (MAW)
A Method Adoption Workshop (MAW) bridges the gap between the written method, and the specific proposal/project circumstances and project team requirements. A MAW creates the framework for the solution design and/or project team to adopt, or tailor, the Method to their project.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Brief Team on Methods
  • Tailor the Process
Main Description

The overall purpose of the Method Adoption Workshop is to tailor or adopt the Method to meet the solution design and delivery requirements of a specific proposal or project.　 The Method Adoption Workshop provides the framework, process and tools to enable the proposal/project teams to design and deliver projects based on the method.　 The Method Adoption Workshop is used to develop the content of a specific proposal or project, or project phase.　 The Method Adoption Workshop approach can also be used to educate the project team, and customers as appropriate, on the proposal/project scope and approach.
