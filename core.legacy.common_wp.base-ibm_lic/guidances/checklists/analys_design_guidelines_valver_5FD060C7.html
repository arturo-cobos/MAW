Check Items
All analysis and design work product are covered
That is, use the list of analysis and design work product descriptions as a checklist of headings under which to add guidelines.
Adequate guidance is provided for the use of tools, when appropriate
The guidelines provide for tight integration of analysis and design work products
For example, are the Interaction Diagrams automatically kept in synchronization with the Class Diagram and other models?
The overall approach to analysis and design is laid out and agreed upon
Clear definitions of what level of detail is expected at each point in the engagement should be established by mapping the elaboration points defined in the artifact to phases in the engagement.
Any assumptions about how certain design considerations will eventually be implemented are documented
