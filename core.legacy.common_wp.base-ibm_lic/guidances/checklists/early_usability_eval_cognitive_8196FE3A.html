Checklist: Early Usability Evaluation using Cognitive Walkthroughs

Check Items
The report describes participants in relation to the user profiles
The report contains a description of the number and types of participants who reflect the backgrounds of users identified in the user profiles documentation.
The evaluations were done with actual end users of the application
The evaluations were done with actual end users of the application, not business analysts, trainers, or others who may at one time have been actual users of the system.
Objective measures of usability are traceable back to quantifiable usability requirements
Objective measures of usability are traceable back to quantifiable usability requirements; that is, the selection and reporting of usability measures reflects measures identified in the usability requirements.
