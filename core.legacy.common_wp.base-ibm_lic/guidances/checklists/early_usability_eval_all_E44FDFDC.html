Checklist: Early Usability Evaluation (All)
Checklist for all types of early usability evaluations
Relationships
Related Elements

Check Items
Evaluation reflects activites in the usability test plan

If a usability test plan was written earlier in the solution effort, the contents of the Early Usability Evaluation will reflect the activities proposed in the usability test plan.

The report contains evaluation criteria and rationale for recommendations
The Early Usability Evaluation contains enough information for a reader to understand exactly how a design solution has been evaluated, including the rationale for understanding how conclusions and recommendations are reached.
The report describes how the evaluations were conducted
The report contains descriptions of methods and procedures describing how the usability and accessibility evaluations were conducted.
Recommendations for improving the design are provided
Based on the results of the study, recommendations for improving the design are provided. In addition, the report will indicate the future (near-term and long-term) steps necessary to refine the design.
