Main Description
Requirements are gathered through workshops and interviews with the client.  The IT organization must be interviewed to understand their IT requirements and their facility infrastructure requirements.  Also, the facility personnel at the facility must be interviewed to determine what must be contained in the new or upgraded facility and its related infrastructure.  Many of the IT requirements will have been developed by IT Architects working on the infrastructure design.  Review the requirements and add any items that might be facility specific.  The requirements are also based on any assessment that was done on the existing facility.  Requirements are developed to address problem and opportunity areas that were identified in the assessment.  In addition, some prioritization for the requirements may be required.
