Operational Support Assumptions

  1. The Lotus Domino Advanced Services server architecture contains single points of failure. There will be no multi-hosting, mirrored sites, disaster recovery, hot backup, or other advanced operational features.
  2. A Staging Server - intermediary, non-production server will not be provided. 
  3. Scheduled maintenance is normally performed weekly. The Hosting Organization reserves the right to interrupt access to the server to perform maintenance as needed.
  4. System and user access to the server and the information and data content may be dependent upon resources not under our control.
  5. The system will be monitored onsite from 8 am to 5pm 5 days a week (Monday â€“ Friday). Response time will be 1 hour for acknowledgement of call/ assessment of problem and problem resolution will be on a best effort basis. During non-business hours the system will not be monitored. 
  6. The Hosting Organization will use commercially reasonable efforts to assure that the servers are operational and available on the network.
  7. The Hosting Organization will provide access to web server log files for reporting and analysis during normal business hours. Web server logs will be sent via FTP to Customer on an agreed upon schedule.
