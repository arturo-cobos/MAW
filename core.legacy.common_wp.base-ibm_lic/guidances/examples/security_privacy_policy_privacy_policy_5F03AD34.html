  • Security and Privacy Policy
Main Description
  • We will ensure that all Personal Data is processed fairly and lawfully. 
  • We shall request and use only that personal information which is pertinent to the effective conduct of business. 
  • We shall consider personal information collected and maintained to be of a confidential nature, recognizing our responsibility to provide adequate safeguards to maintain that confidentiality. 
  • We shall refuse to make available without the knowledge and permission of the individual, personal information outside the Company or its subsidiaries, except to provide outline service or as required by law. 
  • We shall protect the information held on our employees. 
  • We shall make available to employees, upon proper request, any information we maintain on them, recognizing our obligation to protect the source of the information. 
  • We shall make available to policy owners and applicants, upon proper request, any information we maintain on them recognizing our obligation to protect the privacy of the source of the information and in the case of medical information, supplying that through the individual's designated physician.
