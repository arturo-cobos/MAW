Example: Benefit Impact Analysis Success Indicators
Examples of success indicators for the five strategic perspectives.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Benefit Impact Analysis
Main Description

Examples of Business Performance success indicators include:

  • Increase production of products or services.
  • Reduce errors.
  • Decrease down time.
  • Reduce turnover rate.
  • Reduce training costs.
  • Produce high ROI.

Examples of Job/Task Performance success indicators include:

  • Use standardized tasks and procedures.
  • Reduce time to complete task.
  • Increase number of outputs (e.g., closed sales, products produced).
  • Reduce number of errors.
  • Increase quality of products or services.
  • Increase job satisfaction.

Examples of Training and User Support success indicators include:

  • Reduce time to acquire job-related knowledge and skills.
  • Reduce time to job proficiency, from novice to expert.
  • Reduce on-the-job mentoring from other staff.
  • Increase on-the-job support with relevant just-in-time interventions.

Examples of Usability success indicators include:

  • Reduce time to access customer order information.
  • Enhance individual job/task performance with easy-to-use, easy-to-understand tools and navigation.
  • Accommodate novice to expert needs.

Examples of Technology and Implementation success indicators include:

  • Transition from current legacy mainframe system to object-oriented system with reusable objects.
  • Ensure fast system response time.
  • Work across delivery environments (e.g., Client/Server, Corporate Intranet, and World Wide Web).
  • Work across operating systems (e.g., Windows 3.1, Windows 95, O/S2, Mac O/S, and Unix).
  • Work across Web Browsers (e.g., Netscape 3.0, Internet Explorer 3.0, and America Online 3.0).
