customer facing and back office supervisors

Knowledge Worker
Information based researchers and planners; generally not customer facing;
data analysts, financial analysts, accounting division, product development, strategic planners, resource performance management

Admin Support
Secretarial support for managers.
secretaries

Service Support
Provide specialized, clerical services that support the bank and non-financial customer business.
building maintenance, computer operations, training, legal services, marketing services, help desks

Personal and small business customers
Customers and prospects with simple banking requirements, for whom the bank provides all processing and analysis functions.
existing private customers, small businesses, prospects

Non-personal customers
Customers and prospects with complex banking requirements, who typically use their own, or bank supplied systems, to analyze and generate financial transactions that are passed to bank systems electronically.
Corporate customers and partnerships, joint ventures, associates, prospects

3rd Party clients
Customers of the bank who use services provided by the bank.
Building societies

Introducers
Individuals or companies with an initial or ongoing customer relationship, who introduce potential new customers to the bank and its products.
Independent Financial Advisors, Mortgage Broker, Solicitors, Equity houses

Other financial institutions
Incoming and outgoing electronic transactions with other banks and financial networks.
Switch, BACS, Swift, Link

Other Banks in Group
Independent companies within the bank group who may or may not share processing services.
other banks in group

Outsourcing (electronic)
Electronic based services provided by external companies.
CCN, Credit, transaction processing

Outsourcing (resource based)
People based services provided by external companies.
PC maintenance, help desk

Suppliers
Suppliers of products used by the bank.
Stationery suppliers
