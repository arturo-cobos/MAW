Main Description

The first is a quadrant chart constructed from data obtained during a subjective walkthrough for a current solution evaluation of a cinema research tool. The aspects of task support that fall into the quadrant of high satisfaction and high importance (i.e., organization of data, links, and graphics) represent “must meet” targets for the new solution. Those that fall into the quadrant of low satisfaction and high importance (i.e., granularity of information, keyword search) represent the highest opportunity to improve upon the current solution.
