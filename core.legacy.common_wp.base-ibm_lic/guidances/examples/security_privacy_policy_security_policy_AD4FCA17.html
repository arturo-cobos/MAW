Main Description

Individual Use

Individual accountability is required on all company computer systems. Access to computer systems and networks must be provided through the use of individually assigned unique computer identifiers, known as user-IDs. All individuals are responsible for all activity performed under their user-ID. Each individual who uses company computer resources must only access resources to which they are authorized by means of their user-ID. Associated with each user-ID is a password, which must be used to authenticate the person accessing the system or network. Passwords must be treated as confidential information, and must not be disclosed.

Information Classification and Control

A Risk Assessment must be performed periodically to determine company’s most critical information assets. An Information Asset Inventory must be maintained to document each asset’s description, ownership, location, usage and security requirements. Based on its business value, company’s information must be classified, and all users and custodians must apply security controls to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of the information asset that is appropriate for the classification specified. Backup and recovery procedures must be implemented and tested for critical information and the critical systems that store or use information with high availability requirements to ensure timely recovery in the event of an extended outage.

Confidentiality/Integrity

All company information must be protected from unauthorized access to help ensure the information’s confidentiality and maintain its integrity. The information owner must classify and secure information within his/her jurisdiction based on the information’s value, requirements for confidentiality, risk of loss or compromise, and ease of recovery.

Availability

Information must be readily available for authorized use when the user in the normal performance of his/her duties needs it. Appropriate processes must be developed and implemented to ensure the reasonable and timely recovery of all company information, regardless of platform, should that information become corrupted, destroyed, or unavailable for an extended period.
