Brief Statement of Business Need:
ABC Bank has recently acquired several other banks nationwide and would like to take the opportunity to reduce overhead costs and increase the sales of bank services to customers. Their plan is to open a Call Center in each regional area and augment the current responsibilities of Call Center Agents of providing basic customer service information and/or troubleshooting with that of selling additional bank services.

PRIMARY USER GROUP(S)
Brief Description

Call Center Agent
There are currently 50 Call Center Agents at the headquarters site. Over the next 2 years, ABC Bank will establish 5 regional Call Centers with an average of 50 Agents per Call Center. There has historically been a high turnover rate among Call Center Agents.

Call Center Agent Team Leader
There are currently 5 Team Leaders with 10 Agents per team leader. Over the next 2 years, ABC Bank will establish 5 regional Call Centers with an average of 5 Team Leaders per Call Center. The Team Leaders are responsible for hiring, scheduling, and mentoring Call Center Agents as well as provide troubleshooting support and ongoing evaluation of Call Center Agent performance.

Knowledgebase Updating/Support
Product information and procedures need to be updated on a regular basis (some daily, some less frequently).

SUCCESS INDICATORS
Brief Description

Business Performance
Increase sales of products and services to customers by 20%.

Reduce Call Center Agent turnover rate by 30%.

Reduce training costs and training time for current and new Call Center Agents.

Achieve high ROI for the solution.



Job/Task Performance
Reduce time per customer call from 4.5 minutes per call to 3.0 minutes per call.

Increase the number of calls per agent.

Implement bank product and service sales function among current and new Call Center Agents.

Increase quality of calls with customers to meet and/or exceed customer expectations.

Increase effectiveness of customer troubleshooting by Call Center Agents.

Training and User Support
Reduce time to acquire needed knowledge and skills for Call Center Agents.

Reduce time for novice Call Center Agent to become proficient on-the-job.

Reduce the on-the-job mentoring required from other Call Center Agents and the Team Leader.

Usability
Accommodate novice to expert Call Center Agent needs on-the-job.

Reduce time to access needed product and services sales information.

Provide easy-to-understand and use software for accessing customer information and selling additional products and services.

Technology and Implementation
De-skill the knowledgebase content maintenance and updating process so it can be performed by data entry staff.

Reduce/eliminate the cost of paper-based production and distribution of products and services information.

Ensure that only the most current and up-to-date information is available to Call Center Agents.

Implement a client/server network to all regional Call Center sites to support Call Center Agent and customer activity.

Ensure an extensible environment for future Web-based customer service system.
