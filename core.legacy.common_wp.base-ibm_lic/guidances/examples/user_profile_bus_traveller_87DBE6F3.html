Main Description

Summary Definition: A guest traveling to conduct business.


Prior Knowledge and Experience

Business domain
Typically very skilled and experienced at booking hotel rooms, likely with years of experience, and with various hotel chains.

Computer skills
Likely skilled in at least basic computer operations, word processing, email, etc.

Other relevant skills (typing, ...)
Either a touch-typist or proficient "one-finger" typist

Education
Typically a college graduate, including about 30% with some college

Learning (approach, attitude, ...)
Willing to learn and adaptive to change with high demand on brevity and productivity

Physical Characteristics

Age/Gender
Age range from 25 - 55; gender ratio 55% male and 45% female

Sensory (visual impairment, ...)
The rate of physical impairments matches that of the general population (re: Dept. of Labor statistics ...)

Social and Physical Environment

Culture, language, accessibility, …
Most will be receptive to using a kiosk, as opposed to talking to a person, if it is efficient and reliable, allows them to use their native language, and does not violate cultural customs. Accessibility characteristics will conform to ADA specification … (re: an appropriate source or explicit declaration of support)

Loyalty to specific provider, brand, …
Typically loyal to specific airlines, hotels, etc. due to loyalty programs (e.g., miles, points, nights, …), and corporate travel policies.

Technology (attitude, acceptance, ...)
Typically receptive to new technologies that help them be successful with their business goals.

Physical (dusty, noisy, ...)
Will expect the environment to be clean with no excessive noise - typical of a hotel lobby or airport concourse. Will also expect sufficient "privacy space".

Current Job Characteristics

Key working relationships
A large percent are members of a hierarchical business organization, report to a higher level person, and may be required to obtain travel-related approvals; some are principals of their own business. In most cases they will be very concerned with overall travel costs.
