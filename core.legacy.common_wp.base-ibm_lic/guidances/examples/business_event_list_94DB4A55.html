Example: Business Event List
Sample list of internal and external business events.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Business Event List
Main Description

An example of internal business events include:

  • Inventory level drops below the reorder level
  • Management decide to withdraw a product from the marketplace
  • Management decide to offer an early retirement package to selected employees
  • Employee is removed from the system
  • Payment becomes 30 days overdue
  • End of the month
  • End of the year

These are examples of external events (processes / activities performed by external involved parties):

  • Person under 18 applies to open a savings account
  • New residential customer requests mortgage product / service information
  • Existing commercial customer requests business premises insurance quotation
  • Existing commercial customer accepts business premises insurance quotation
  • New customer places a vehicle order for a new model car
  • Residential customer submits a billing complaint
  • Small business customer returns an office furniture product
  • Residential policyholder makes a property subsidence claim
  • MBA student withdraws from a extra-curricula course
  • New customer gets enrolled to the on-line client system
  • Commercial customer registers a complaint
  • New business partner requests payment
  • Existing dealer provides sales report
  • A threat agent attempts to access the system
