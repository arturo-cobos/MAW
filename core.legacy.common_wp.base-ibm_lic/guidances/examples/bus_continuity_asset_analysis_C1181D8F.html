Main Description

"The largest replacement value was for Facilities/Building, which is estimated at $40,000,000. This constitutes 55.3% of the total value of all assets. The next highest values for replacement cost were for Computer Hardware and Communications Equipment. The values and percentages of the whole are, respectively, $29,290,306, at 40.5% and $2,129,397 at 2.9%.

Only tangible assets with a replacement value were provided for this report. Data on the hourly loss of some categories was not presented for evaluation. May wish to further investigate replacement value of sensitive resources such as customers, personnel, and vital records. Replacement of these assets could be greater than the replacement value when accounting for the loss of future opportunity and the extent of exposure.
