Artifact: Lessons Learned (ART 0624)
This artifact is the collection of team's assessment of what went well or what could be improved.
Domains: Project Management
Work Product Kinds: Inventory
Purpose

The purpose of this artifact is to document the lessons learned and to communicate this to improve future initatiatives and avoid the same mistakes.

Description
Brief Outline

Typically, this artifact should include the following for each category being reviewed:

  • Person submitting Lessons Learned
  • Successes (what went well)
  • Improvement Needed (what did not work well)
  • Recommendations
  • Comments

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without this artifact:

  • Important lessons learned could be lost and future projects and teams would not benefit from experiences
  • Improvements that could save time and money might not be implemented
Reasons for not needing

This artifact is not required if the same information is captured in another artifact or tool.
