Artifact: Technology Scan (BUS 328)
The Technology Scan work product offers benchmarking or comparative baseline views to identify new technologies and their details.
Domains: Business Strategy
Work Product Kinds: Assessment
Purpose

The purpose of the Technology Scan is to:

  • Establish the set of technologies required to support business scenarios, that is determining the technologies which should be deployed to enable "how" the business will work given a new set of capabilities
  • Understand new technologies that may affect competitive position and be ready to exercise the opportunity to use them and effective business change
  • Understand how competitors may be using technology for competitive advantage

  • Look for opportunities for technology to drive business strategy and position IBM to form closer bonds with business executives to drive additional work and solidify IBM as a primary client business partner

Description
Main Description

Both business value and technical risk are important factors in determining which technologies to implement. The organization's readiness to use each new technology should be evaluated in addition to assessing business value.

Identification of new technologies ready for use is derived from a variety of sources:

  • Prioritized Business Function
  • IBM Research
  • Outside Research
  • Technology Brainstorming
  • Client Technology Competencies
  • Marketplace Factors

Peer comparisons can be financial, application, technology, people/skill and/or IT process related.

Brief Outline

The Technology Scan work product offers is used to identify the following details:

  • New information technologies that offer value to the business and are ready for use.
  • How peers and competitors use technology.
  • The scope of the scan is generally driven by either required capabilities and/or business scenarios.

Tailoring
Impact of not havingWithout this work product, the organization may not identify a key force or driver that could change the dynamics of an industry and therefore lose the opportunity to drive the nature of competition. The organization may also needlessly pursue "bleeding edge" technology and burn up critical resources.
Reasons for not needingThis work product may not be needed if the sponsor does not believe technology can be a key driver in competitive positioning or if the sponsor has already made choices as to what technologies and technology-related initiatives to pursue and they have been effectively mapped to business direction, positioning, value creation and capability requirements.
Representation Options

This work product can take the following forms:

  • Technology Hunt (the old technology scan that will be updated in a formal project)
  • Competitor Use of IT
  • IT Peer Comparison
