Artifact: Application Information Questionnaire (ENG 365)
This work product is a document containing information about the application and its environment.
Domains: Environment
Work Product Kinds: Inventory
Purpose
The purpose of this work product is to document data and information about an application to help conduct a thorough technical and business-level analysis of the application.
Description
Main DescriptionThe application information questionnaire provides an insight into business, environmental, and technical aspects of the application. The data and information is collected at the application function and / or component level.

Key Considerations
  • Gather information at the function or component level, as this would be most helpful with the analysis work.
  • Ensure that all the questions are answered.
  • Have the completed questionnaire reviewed by people knowledgeable in various aspects of the application â€“ technical, business plans related, operational etc.
  • Avoid ambiguous answers.
    • When the answers are not clear, follow up with a telephone call or arrange a meeting with the application contacts.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without this document, the knowledge about the application cannot be easily referenced and this might lead to an inefficient and less effective application analysis.

There is a risk of selecting the components that might not be relevant to future business direction, or missing out on some components that may be technically sound but too complex to understand by the application maintenance team.

Reasons for not needing

The reasons for not needing this work product are:

  • It may not be necessary to create this document if the team performing the perfective maintenance has the required information available in some other form.
  • It is advisable to create this document as a vehicle for communication between the team members.
Representation OptionsThis work product is normally descriptive. Tables or forms can be used to collect the data.
