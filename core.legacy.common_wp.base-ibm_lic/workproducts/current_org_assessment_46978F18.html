Artifact: Current Organization Assessment (ORG 307)
The Current Organization Assessment work product is an analysis of the organization's current structure and behaviors.
Domains: Organization Design
Work Product Kinds: Assessment
Purpose

The purpose of this work product is to identify the strengths and weaknesses of the current organization in relation to the stated business strategy, vision, and goals in order to set key priorities for redesign.  In assessing the current organization, it identifies key gaps and areas of conflict to address in the future organization design and transition planning.

Description
Main Description

This artifact identifies how, and to what extent, the organization's structure, behaviors and enablers:

  • are aligned to the business strategy and desired capabilities
  • are efficient
  • enable effective human performance (ensuring a conducive atmosphere to bring out the best in both non-disabled and disabled personnel)
  • are internally consistent and reflect industry best practice

This artifact will often contain references to the following:

  • The stated strategy, vision, and goals of the business
  • The description of the current organization
  • Groups within the organization with respect to roles
  • External benchmarks
  • Nine-cell Organization Design matrix
Key Considerations

This work product is produced through a technically complex assessment process, which requires a level of expertise consistent with the level of complexity.  You should ensure your team has this expertise, and in its absence, seek help from the Organization Change Knowledge Network.

It is imperative that you use client resources to help gather your data.

Tailoring
Impact of not havingWithout a Current Organization Assessment there will be no basis for planning the redesign activity within the project, and the executive sponsors may not accept the need for redesign.
Reasons for not needing

Any project whose goals include major transformation will also involve organization change, and therefore organization analysis and design (OA&D). This work product is key to an effective OA&D delivery. It is not needed, however, if:

  • The engagement is not a major transformation.
  • The business is so new that an organization must be created from scratch.
  • There is already a clear consensus about the part(s) of the organization that is to be redesigned and why.

If the organization redesign is being done by the client and is therefore out of the engagement's scope, this work product is not needed
Representation Options

The Current Organization Assessment is documented in textual form illustrated by diagrams.  The document could be structured in two ways:

  • Using the nine-cell matrix as a structure for the document
  • According to level of impact and priorities
