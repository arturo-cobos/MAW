The major milestone or hand over point. This again will depend on the criticality of the project. Some projects require daily reporting, others only at specific milestones, such as moving to the next test level. Very small or low risk testing efforts may require only a single report at the end of testing. Suitable adjustments need to be applied to above estimates based on frequency of reporting, if tools are used to generate the reports, and the number of people who need to input information, etc.

The effort required producing effective presentation of Test Report data is often underestimated. The accurate reporting of numerical data in meaningful graphical formats is very time consuming.

Estimates will increase with size and complexity of the project, number of people and organizations involved.
