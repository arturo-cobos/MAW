Main Description
Estimates for this work product will vary, depending on when it is created and whether it is part of the billable engagement or not. If the estimate is part of the Statement of Work, then minimum work may be expended using most of the generic sections of the template. On the other hand, if this is part of a very big contract it is worthwhile investing the effort, even if it is not billable to the client.
 
The following estimates are realistic of actual effort required regardless of whether it is billable to the client or not. These estimates are for the Test Manager effort only. The client’s time and that of any other team member is additional.
  • If there are no standards at the client organization, it would take about 40 hours of effort to put together a generic testing strategy and an additional 8 to 10 hours to review with the key client team.
  • If other testing methods are in use, additional 20 hours of effort should be allowed to investigate, incorporate or reconcile the differences in standards between the client methods and IBM’s approach to testing.
 
The two test strategies included in the Key Consideration section will need to be estimated as one-offs, and normal rules of thumb, as stated here, do not apply.
