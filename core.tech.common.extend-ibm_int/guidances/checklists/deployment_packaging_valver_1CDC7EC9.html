Check Items
Everything required is in the package and works as intended
Put together a package and do a trial run.
Packaging has been tested by the intendend audience

Give it to someone who fits the profile of the intended audience (that is, a nontechnical user not a development team member), have him/her open the package and walk through what you expect a user to do with the package.
