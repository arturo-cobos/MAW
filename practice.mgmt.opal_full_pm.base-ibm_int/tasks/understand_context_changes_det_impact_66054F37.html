Task: Understand context changes and determine impact
Disciplines: Sponsor Agreement Management
Purpose
To understand changing business needs and the impact of such changes on the project.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Project Definition
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Project Definition
      Main Description
      Assess business needs, as follows:
      • Consider the changes that occurred during the cycle period and that affect the business needs covered by the project.
      • Changes that affect the already identified business needs.
      • New business needs.


      Update the business needs and priorities. Determine joint objectives with the sponsor. Determine the impact on strategy of the following:
      • Based upon updated business needs and the results already obtained by the project, update the overall approach and planning framework in the current Project Definition.
      • Obtain Project Charter(s) or create Project Definition(s) for new projects that will achieved the updated strategy.
      • Communicate new strategies to the appropriate project organizational units and launch actions to gain formal agreement on new business when applicable.
