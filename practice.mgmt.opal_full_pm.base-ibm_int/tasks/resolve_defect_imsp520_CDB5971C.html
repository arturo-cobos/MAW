Task: Resolve defect (IMSP520)
Resolve the defects in order of their importance update the defect document and notify of the defect resolution
Purpose
To resolve the defects and notify the stakeholders of the defect resolution.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Resolution Owner
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Defect Analysis Document
  • Defect Log
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Defect Analysis Document
    • Defect Log
      Main Description
      Resolve the defects in order of their importance based on the severity and prioritization.  As progress is made and corrective actions are taken update the appropriate defect documentation.  Once the defect has been correct notify the Project Manager, Stakeholder and other affected parties of the resolution.
      Steps
      Resolve defects in order of their importance
      Resolve defects in order of their importance, based on their allocated severity and project prioritization, within the turnaround time specified for the approved recommended action.

      Note: Priority values are defined by the project.

      Update the defect management documentation
      As progress is made and actions are completed, update the defect log, the findings log and/or the project management schedule, as appropriate. The defect log is updated with the following information:
      • For all defects: Current action status and actual date of when action was taken
      • When recording effort associated with defects: Actual effort of action taken
      • For defects where the actual action taken is different from the assigned action: Actual resolution
      • For high impact defects where a temporary bypass is required, before a permanent fix is developed: Date the bypass is available
      Communicate the defect resolution
      When complete, communicate the defect resolution to the Project Manager, the Stakeholder, and any other affected parties.
      Seek acceptance of the resolution
      Seek acceptance of the resolution from the Project Manager, the Stakeholder and other affected parties.

      If the resolution is not accepted, the Resolution Owner may need to implement additional actions to satisfactorily resolve the defect. Difficulty achieving consensus by all affected parties can be addressed through escalation, if necessary.
