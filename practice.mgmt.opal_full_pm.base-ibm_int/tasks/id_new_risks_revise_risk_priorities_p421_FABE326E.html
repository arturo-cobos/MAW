Task: Identify new risks and revise risk priorities (IMSP421)
Disciplines: Risk Management
Purpose
To search for and identify new risks, analyze them, and plan the risk response approach for addressing them.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Risk Log
  • Risk Management Plan
    Outputs
    • Project Status Report
    • Risk Definition
    • Risk Management Plan
      Main Description

      The following steps occur:

      • Review the Event Log, Issue Log, Change Log and related descriptions.
      • Review situational factors and risks indicators.
      • Identify those new risks coming from changes in the project context and lessons learned that need to be considered for the remaining of the project.
      • Evaluate impact level, probability of occurrence and time frame for the new risks.
      • Based on the risk indicators and other project data, update as necessary the evaluation of already identified risks.
      • Reorder the risk priorities based on risk evolution and new risks.
      • Update the Risk Definitions.
      • Determine and update the risk response approach.
      • If the risk response approach cannot be handled within the financial baseline, raise an issue.
