Task: Adjust assignments and estimate to complete (IMSP421)
Based on a comparison of the estimating basis used to initially estimate the workload with the trends of the actual workload spent on work completed or in progress, this task will make adjustments to assignments and to estimates to complete the work.
Purpose
To revise the estimates to complete the work and to take appropriate corrective actions, as needed.

        Main Description

        The key outputs of this task are the Operational Schedule; Staff Schedule; Root Cause Analysis Record; Action Control Document; Action Log; Change Request and Change Log.

        This task includes the following:

        • For situations in which there is a significant variance between actual productivity and the estimating basis, analyze the root cause
        • After root cause analysis, determine corrective actions to "get back on track" or to better prepare for the next period
        • Document the corrective actions
