Task: Clarify Deliverable Definitions and quality characteristics (IMSP491)
This task will identify the work products that each project organizational units (POU) plans to deliver, and will determine and document the quality characteristics and quality criteria that will contribute in meeting the work product's acceptance criteria.
Purpose
To ensure that the deliverables and their quality characteristics for each project organizational unit (POU) are identified and documented, so the project can be planned and started with a clear understanding of these items.

      Main Description

      This task includes the following:

      • Review project scope and identify the work products that each project organizational unit (POU) plans to deliver to the sponsor or another POU.
      • Clarify the acceptance or completion criteria for the work products, and determine the quality characteristics and quality criteria that will contribute in meeting the acceptance criteria.
        • Update the Deliverable Definition accordingly.

      The key outputs of this task are the Deliverable Definition and the Project Quality Plan.
