Task: Analyze and respond to sponsor findings (IMSP540)
Disciplines: Deliverables Management
Purpose
To analyze the sponsor's findings about deliverables and agree with the sponsor on their resolution.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Defect Log
  • Findings Log
  • Risk Management Plan
  • Test Log
  • Work Product Inspection Form
    		Optional:
    • Sponsor findings
      Outputs
      • Corrective actions
      • Correspondence with sponsor
      • Defect Log
      • Deliverable Definition
      • Findings Log
      • Issue Document
      • Issue Log
      • Operational Schedule
        Main Description
        Any findings made by the sponsor must be analyzed and responded to. The resolution for any findings may vary. Findings are not necessarily a result of a defect, and some corrections might be delayed to another release, etc.. If necessary, adjust the Operational Schedule and, potentially, project schedules in order to fix the findings as agreed with the sponsor or their representative. Update the Findings Log with replies and decisions.
