Task: Define regular meetings, reporting and other communications (P491)
This task will define the regular meetings, the reporting and other communications required for the project.
Purpose
To determine regular meetings, reporting, and other communications that take place during the project.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Project Definition
    		Optional:
    • Stakeholder Participation Management Plan
      Outputs
      • Communications Management Plan
        Main Description
        Define the regular meetings, reporting, and other communications that take place during the project and outline the communications management plan to understand its potential overhead on the work effort.

         Refer to the Communications Management Guideline.

          Refer to your organization's Communications Management Plan (Organizational) for P3 CTR to identify periodic management reviews that may be required.
