Task: Close risk (IMSP600)
Update the risk documentation with the reason for closing the risk and changing the status of the risk appropriately.
Purpose
To document and close the open risk.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
  • Contract Business Office (CBO)
InputsMandatory:
  • Risk Definition
  • Risk Management Plan
  • Risk Occurrence Document
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Risk Definition
    • Risk Management Plan
    • Risk Occurrence Document
      Main Description
      This task will close the risk.  All documentation pertaining to the risk will be updated and the status of risk with be set to the appropriate status.
      Steps
      Update risk management plan & risk definition with the rationale about the risk
      Update the appropriate risk management plan and risk definition with the rationale for the risk closure.
      Update risk management plan & risk definition with information about the risk

      Update the appropriate risk management plan and risk definition with any additional information about the risk.

      The additional information should focus on items that can be used later, either within the current project or by future projects. Examples of the type of information to be captured include:

      • Successful containment plans and reasons why they were successful
      • Failed containment plans and the reasons for their failure
      • Risk relationships and dependencies that were not obvious
      • Relevant data from various analyses, especially costs and benefits of mitigation plans
      Update the appropriate risk documentation with a risk status of "Closed"

      Update the appropriate risk management plan and risk definition with a risk status of "Closed".

      If a closed risk recurs, the appropriate risk definition can be reopened or a new risk can be created that references the old risk definition.
