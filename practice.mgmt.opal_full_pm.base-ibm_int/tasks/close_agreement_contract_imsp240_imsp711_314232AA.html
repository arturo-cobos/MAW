Task: Close agreement/contract (IMSP240 and IMSP711)
Describes the administrative closure of an agreement or contract towards the end of a project.
Purpose
This task is performed to ensure the closure of financial elements and other administrative obligations at the time of an agreement or contract closure. Such an agreement can be between a delivery organization and a sponsor or between a delivery organization and a supplier. 
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
  • Relationship Owner
  • Sponsor
InputsMandatory:
  • Agreement
    		Optional:
    • Subcontractor Status Report
      Outputs
      • Final acceptance for supplier
      • Final Acceptance from Sponsor
        Main Description
        This task outlines the activities to be performed at the time of closure of an agreement or a contract which includes:
        • Finalizing financial elements of the project such as
          • Process final expenses and labor claims
          • Review Financial Management Tool and resolve any outstanding issues
          • Review A/R and resolve any outstanding issues
          • Create and send final invoice to client according to terms in the SOW/MSA/CSA 
          • Closure of time recording and billing codes
          • Confirm with A/R that payment is applied correctly
          • Finalize financial reconciliation
          • Some projects: confirm with client that they have closed POs
        • Performing administrative closure of the sponsor agreement
        • Performing administrative closure of the supplier agreement

        The Partner, DPE or delegate closes the opportunity in the opportunity management system (Siebel status "Won & Completed"), which triggers the IBM Client Value Assessment-Transaction survey process (CVA-T). The Partner or DPE is responsible for confirming the client contact information and for ensuring its accuracy.   Once the data is verified the Partner should close the opportunity.  Client satisfaction feedback is gathered using the CVA-T survey which is conducted by a third party market research firm, not the Partner or DPE.  The survey feedback is used to measure the overall satisfaction of the client with all aspects of delivery and to provide insight into client satisfaction with IBM services.

        Note:  The Relationship Owner role is equivalent to the Partner or DPE.
