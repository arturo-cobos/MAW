Task: Conduct internal management review (IMSP391)
This task will conduct reviews of the agreement and plans with the internal team, after the plans have been reconciled and integrated.
Purpose

To ensure plans provide a complete, consistent, coherent, accurate, and acceptable approach, and to ensure agreement and commitment from stakeholders, reducing the risk of issues later in the project.

Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
  • Reviewer
InputsMandatory:
  • Agreement
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Agreement
    • Work Product Inspection Form
      Main Description

      The key outputs of this task are the Work Product Inspection Form and the Seven Keys Assessment.

      This task includes the following:

      • Conduct a seven keys assessment
      • Conduct internal reviews of the agreement(s) and all project plans by the delivery team and all other affected parties, including senior management
      • Ensure plans integrate and provide a complete, consistent, coherent, accurate, and acceptable approach
      • Review all commitments
      • Obtain approval and agreements from all affected parties
