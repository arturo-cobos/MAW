Task: Discuss and agree on corrective actions (IMSP421)
Disciplines: Quality Management
Purpose
To review findings and to agree on and launch corrective actions.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
  • Project Quality Analyst
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Corrective actions
  • Quality Review Documentation
    Outputs
    • Action Control Document
    • Action Log
    • Issue Document
    • Issue Log
    • Quality Review Documentation
      Main Description
      Review the audit or review report and accept, reject, or delay the corrective actions, or propose alternative actions and agree with auditor or reviewer.

      If recommended actions affect the sponsor Agreement, discuss these actions with the sponsor to determine acceptability of the actions.

      Review the proposed response with functional management, when required, and amend as needed. Launch corrective actions.

      Raise a Change Request if a corrective action impacts the milestone schedule in the sponsor Agreement or other elements in the project baseline.

      Review response with the audit or review team, and establish a plan for the review team to follow-up on agreed to corrective actions.
