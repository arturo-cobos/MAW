Task: Ensure resources are provided to supplier (IMSP391 and IMSP711)
This task will prepare for adding suppliers to the project by ensuring facilities, materials or other resources are available to them, in accordance with their Agreement.
Purpose
To prepare for suppliers to the project, facilitating their integration within the project team, and ensuring they have the resources to perform their work.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Agreement
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Asset Inventory
      Main Description

      The key output of this task is the Asset Inventory.

      This task includes the following:

      • Ensure that the facilities, information technology, communications material, security rights, and other resources are provided to the supplier in compliance with the supplier Agreement
      • Document any assets provided to the supplier in the Asset Inventory
