Task: Identify alternative solutions (IMSP445)
This task identifies alternative solution that will help the issue in this Key Decision process.
Purpose
To identify alternative solutions within the Key Decision process.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
  • Stakeholder
InputsMandatory:
  • Key Decision Record
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Key Decision Record
      Main Description

      Determine the strategy used to identify and explore alternative solutions that may address the matter. Execute the planned strategy to identify and explore a wide range of alternative solutions.

      Repeat this step in order to build up a sufficiently wide range of alternative solutions.

      Document each proposed alternative solution.

      Steps
      Determine the strategy

      Determine the strategy used to identify and explore alternative solutions that may address the matter.

      For example:

      • A brainstorming session can be used to stimulate a range of diverse and innovative alternatives through rapid interaction and feedback.  Other alternatives can then be added to the list of potential candidate solutions as the analysis proceeds and participants have had an opportunity to think about the matter more deeply and for a longer period of time.
      • A literature search may uncover what others have done both inside and outside the current organization.  It may provide participants with a deeper understanding of the matter, alternatives to consider, barriers to implementation, existing trade studies, and lessons learned from similar key decisions.
      • Interviews and working groups can be used effectively with stakeholders, or subject matter experts, or other relevant parties, or a combination of these to uncover alternatives.
      • Consider whether combining key attributes of any of the identified alternatives may generate additional, perhaps even stronger alternatives.
      Execute the planned strategy

      Execute the planned strategy to identify and explore a wide range of alternative solutions.

      Repeat this step in order to build up a sufficiently wide range of alternative solutions.

      Document each proposed alternative solution
      Document each proposed alternative solution.
