Task: Review plans with client (IMSP391)
This task will conduct reviews of the agreement and plans that need to be shared or agreed with the client, after the plans have been reconciled and internally reviewed. This will vary from project to project.
Purpose

To ensure agreement and commitment from the client, reducing the risk of issues later in the project.

Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager

    		Optional:

    • Agreement
    • Change Request
    • Work Product Inspection Form
      Main Description

      The key output of this task is the Work Product Inspection Form, and may also include an updated Agreement and Change Requests, as needed.

      This task includes the following:

      • Convert the project plans into a client presentation format, removing any organization specific confidential information
      • Review the plans and common procedures with the client, including:
        • Requirements
        • Scope
        • Milestone list
        • Project management schedule
        • Deliverable definition
      • Roles and responsibilities (delivery organization and the client)
      • Reporting between the project and client
      • Project decision structure
      • Client Data Security and Privacy plan
      • Common procedures to be used by the client and organization for the project, such as:
        • Change Management Procedure
        • Action Management Procedure
        • Issue Management Procedure
      • Record the results of the review, such as on a Work Product Inspection Form
      • As needed, adjust the project plans based on the client's feedback, provided these adjustments do not contradict the agreement
      • If any of the required modifications impact the schedule, cost or deliverables, raise a change request
      • Obtain approval and agreements from all affected parties
