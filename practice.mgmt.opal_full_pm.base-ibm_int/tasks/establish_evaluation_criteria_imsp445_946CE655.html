Task: Establish evaluation criteria (IMSP445)
Establish the evaluation criteria by confirming a common understanding for making Key Decision.
Purpose
To establish the evaluation criteria by confirming that all participants share a common understanding of the objectives and requirements.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
  • Stakeholder

  • Key Decision Record

      Main Description

      All participants must share a common understanding of the objectives and requirements of the formal valuation process for the Key Decision.

      Identify the criteria that could be used as the basis for evaluating alternative solutions to address the identified matter.

      Rank the evaluation criteria so that the highest ranked criteria will exert the most influence on the evaluation.
       
      Assess the evaluation criteria and their relative importance, and decides whether to include or exclude each criterion in the evaluation of alternative solutions.

      Document all identified evaluation criteria, their ranking, and the rationale for the selection or the rejection of each criterion.

      Categorize the evaluation criteria to allow for easier assessment and reporting.

      Reviews the evaluation criteria and determines whether to proceed to identifying alternative solutions.
      Steps
      Confirm all participants share common understanding of objectives & requirements
      Confirm that all participants share a common understanding of, and are committed to achieving the scope, objectives and requirements of the formal evaluation process for the matter requiring a key decision.
      Identify the criteria

      Identify the criteria that could be used as the basis for evaluating alternative solutions to address the identified matter.

      Criteria have to be traceable to requirements, scenarios, business case assumptions, business objectives, or other documented sources.

      Types of evaluation criteria to consider include the following: 

      • Technology limitations, such as the limitations of a client-specified technical environment within which new software must operate
      • Risks, such as the long term viability of companies providing licensed software
      • Cost, such as initial purchase costs, licensing fees, costs of ongoing support, and training costs
      Rank the evaluation criteria

      Rank the evaluation criteria so that the highest ranked criteria will exert the most influence on the evaluation.

      The ranking is also to reflect the needs, objectives and priorities of the relevant stakeholders.

      There are numerous ways to perform the ranking.  For example, both numeric and non-numeric criteria can be used.  Numeric criteria use weights to reflect the relative importance of the criteria.  Non-numeric criteria may use a more subjective ranking scale (such as, high, medium, low), or may be Boolean.

      Assess the evaluation criteria and their relative importance
      Assess the evaluation criteria and their relative importance, and decides whether to include or exclude each criterion in the evaluation of alternative solutions.
      Document all identified evaluation criteria

      Document all identified evaluation criteria, their ranking, and the rationale for the selection or the rejection of each criterion.

      Categorize the evaluation criteria to allow for easier assessment and reporting.  Examples of categories include, but are not limited to:

      • Cost
      • Support
      • Functional
      • Non-functional
      Review the evaluation criteria

      Reviews the evaluation criteria and determines whether to proceed to identifying alternative solutions.

      If a decision is made:

      • To proceed, continue at Task 03, "Identify alternative solutions"
      • Not to proceed, return to the beginning of this activity
      • That a Key Decision is no longer needed (for example, because there is only one possible solution, or because there is no possible solution), continue at Task 06, "Document and communicate final results"
