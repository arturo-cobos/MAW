Task: Perform a risk assessment (IMSP491)
This task will perform a risk assessment of the work environment established in the project.
Purpose
The purpose of this task is to perform a risk assessment of the work environment for the project.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Risk Definition
  • Risk Management Plan
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Risk Definition
    • Risk Management Plan
      Main Description

      Perform a risk assessment of the work environment established in the project, such as for the risk of adopting or not adopting the organization's common tools or other work environment standards (as defined in the organization's organizational reference document (ORD)) and documents in the technical environment plan.

      Follow the guidance in WW QA "Technical and Delivery Assessment (TDA)" and "Integrated Technical Review (ITR)" processes.

        Document these risks in the risk management plan and the initial risk definition, as necessary, in accordance with the Continuous Risk Management Procedure (IMSP600).

      Key Considerations

      Reference OPAL Procedures:

      • Project Planning Procedure (IMSP491)

      Reference WW QA "Technical and Delivery Assessment (TDA)" and "Integrated Technical Review (ITR)" guidance.
