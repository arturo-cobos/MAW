Task: Assess conformance to sponsor acceptance criteria (IMSP540)
Disciplines: Deliverables Management
Purpose
To review any relevant technical verification data and ensure that a deliverable is ready for release to the sponsor and formally authorize delivery.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Issue Log
  • Project Quality Plan
  • Risk Management Plan
  • Test Log
  • Test Strategy
  • Work Product Inspection Form
    		Optional:
    • Quality Review Documentation
      Outputs
      • Issue Document
      • Issue Log
      • Risk Management Plan
        Main Description
        Review the results and trends of the technical verifications of the deliverable or key component before releasing it to the sponsor to ensure the deliverable meets the requirements per the Agreement and that no open items remain and it is ready to be delivered. Prepare, conduct and document the review in the Quality Review Documentation. Formally authorize delivery.
