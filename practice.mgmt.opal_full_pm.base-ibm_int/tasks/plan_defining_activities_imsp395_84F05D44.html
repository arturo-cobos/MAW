Task: Plan the defining activities (IMSP395)
Disciplines: Project Definition
Purpose
To begin the process of planning further Defining and Planning activities and identify further activities needed to shape the project and thereby establish an Agreement.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Project Charter
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Operational Schedule
    • Project Control Book Initialized
      Main Description
      Determine the way to come to an agreement on the project being considered with the sponsor, and what further work must be accomplished in Defining and Planning activities to reach this agreement.

      Note that, at this point, the delivery organization may consider that the project being envisioned cannot be achieved with an acceptable level of risk, and decide not to proceed.

      Determine the technical skills that are appropriate to contribute to shaping the project, and document plans for undertaking the rest of the Defining and Planning activities.
