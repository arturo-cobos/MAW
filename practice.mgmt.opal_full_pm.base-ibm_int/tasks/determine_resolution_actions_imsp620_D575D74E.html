Task: Determine resolution actions (IMSP620)
Disciplines: Event Management
Purpose
To ensure that each pending issue has an appropriate action to resolve the issue.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Issue Document
  • Issue Log
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Compliance Incident Document
    • Issue Document
    • Issue Log
    • Risk Occurrence Document
      Main Description
      For each pending issue, determine resolution actions and identify any risk occurrences, compliance incidents, defects, or change requests that this issue may hide or reveal. Document appropriately these newly identified events which will be handled through their related specific processes.

      Determining resolution actions should consider potential alternative actions, or sets of actions, to solve the issue and avoid recurrence. Evaluate these alternatives, and their impact on the workload of their potential owners. When appropriate, update the Operational Schedule to help manage the workload of the owners of significant actions.
