Task: Track and manage outstanding defects to closure (IMSP520)

Review the defect documentation and log for outstanding defects and escalate to management if needed.
Purpose
To review the defect documentation and log for outstanding defects and escalate as needed.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Defect Analysis Document
  • Defect Log
  • Findings Log
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Defect Analysis Document
    • Defect Log
    • Findings Log
      Main Description
      Review the defect documentation and log for outstanding defects throughout the project's lifecycle and determine if the outstanding defect require escalation for resolution is needed.
      Steps
      Periodically review the defect management documentation or defect log
      Periodically review the defect log for outstanding defects throughout the project's lifecycle to ensure Resolution Owners are taking prompt and appropriate action to bring the defects to closure.
      Decide whether any outstanding defects require escalation
      Decide whether any outstanding defects require escalation for resolution before the project is impacted.
      Update the defect management documentation or defect log
      Update the defect log as necessary.
      Ensure that the findings log is updated
      Ensure that the findings log is updated for status of all defects found during Deliverables Release and Acceptance and reported in the findings log.
