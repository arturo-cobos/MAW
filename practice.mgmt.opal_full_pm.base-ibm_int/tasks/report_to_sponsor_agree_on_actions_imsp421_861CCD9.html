Task: Report to sponsor and agree on actions (IMSP421)
This task reports to the sponsor on project progress, status of sponsor's expectations and contractual obligations, and on the major events of which they should be made aware.
Purpose

To ensure that the sponsor is made aware of project progress, status of sponsor's expectations and contractual obligations, and major events of which they should be aware.

    • Issue Log

      Main Description

      The key outputs of this task are the Client Status Report, and the Action Control Document and Action Log, as needed.

      This task includes the following, done in accordance with the Communications Management Plan:

      • Review the report with the sponsor
      • Agree on actions, as appropriate
