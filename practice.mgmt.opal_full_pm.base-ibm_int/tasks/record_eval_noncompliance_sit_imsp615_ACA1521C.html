Task: Record and evaluate noncompliance situation (IMSP615)
Disciplines: Event Management
Purpose
To document the details associated with a compliance incident and notify the sponsor or supplier.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Issue Management Plan
  • Risk Management Plan
    		Optional:
    • Agreement
    • Compliance Incident Log
    • Event Log
      Outputs
      • Compliance Incident Document
      • Compliance Incident Log
        Main Description
        Check that the event is really a compliance incident and collect any evidence of its occurrence, breadth, and impacts. Estimate probable damages for the project in terms of cost, schedule shift, and health due to the incident. Formally notify the sponsor (or supplier, depending on which is involved) of the incident and its impacts on the project, and discuss ways to avoid recurrence.
