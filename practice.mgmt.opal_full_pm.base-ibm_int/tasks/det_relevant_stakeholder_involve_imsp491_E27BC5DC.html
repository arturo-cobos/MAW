Task: Determine relevant stakeholder involvement (IMSP491)
This task will determine how relevant stakeholders, including the response team, will be involved in establishing the project organizational unit (POU) plans and committing to it.
Purpose

To ensure the plans identify the relevant stakeholders and document their involvement, ensuring that all the components of the proposal will be properly defined and integrated.

      Main Description

      This task includes the following:

      • Using the planning framework in the project definition as input, determine how relevant stakeholders, including the response team, will be involved in establishing the Project Organizational Unit (POU) plan and committing to it.
      • Document their participation in the Communications Management Plan or the Stakeholder Participation Management Plan, or both.

      The Stakeholder Participation Management Plan should be used in addition to the Communications Management Plan to supplement information about stakeholders that is not captured in the Communications Management Plan for complex projects or projects without strong support for organizational change.

      In small organizations where control is centralized and there is strong support for organizational change, or the client is experienced with this type of change and has a defined strategy, the stakeholders' information, requirements, and their involvement can be documented in the Communications Management Plan.

      The key outputs of this task are the Communications Management Plan or the Stakeholder Participation Management Plan, or both, noting that both of these may be part of the Project Management System Summary.
