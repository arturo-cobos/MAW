Task: Acceptance or rejection and notify supplier (IMSP540 and IMSP711)
Disciplines: Deliverables Management
Purpose
To make a final decision on the acceptance or rejection of a deliverable, adjust plans if necessary, and formally notify the delivery organization of the acceptance or rejection.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:

      Outputs
      • Change Request

      • Event Log
      • Issue Document
      • Issue Log
        Main Description
        When the acceptance plan has been completed and any corrections verified, decide to accept or to reject the deliverable. If a decision to accept with limitations or to reject has been made, a new round of acceptance work may start. Adjust the Operational and Project Management Schedules, if necessary.
