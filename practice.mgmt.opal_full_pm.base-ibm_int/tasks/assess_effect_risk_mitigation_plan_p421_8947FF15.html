Task: Assess effectiveness of risk mitigation plan (IMSP421)
Disciplines: Risk Management
Purpose
To review risk data and assess the effectiveness of risk plans.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Risk Management Plan
  • Risk Occurrence Document
    		Optional:
    • Event Log
    • Project Status Report
    • Risk Definition
      Outputs
      • Action Control Document
      • Action Log
      • Issue Document
      • Issue Log
      • Project Status Report
        Main Description

        Acquire risk data from Risk Occurrence Documents, compile and analyze for trends and effectiveness of the Risk Management Plan.

        Assess existing risk evolution and mitigation plan effectiveness.

        For all the risks already identified, update risk impact and probability based upon all available records and tracking data, including issues, tests and incidents.

        Close the risks that are no longer a threat for the project.

        Key Considerations

        Reference OPAL Procedures:

        • Project Monitoring and Control Procedure (IMSP421)
        • Continuous Risk Management Procedure (IMSP600)
