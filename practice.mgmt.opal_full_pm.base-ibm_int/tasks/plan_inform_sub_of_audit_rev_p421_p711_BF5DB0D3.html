Task: Plan and inform subcontractor of audit or review (P421 and P711)
Disciplines: Supplier Management
Purpose
To prepare for an audit or review of a subcontractor.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Client Data Security and Privacy Plan
  • Project Management Schedule
    		Optional:
    • Project Management System Summary
    • Project Procedures Description
    • Project Quality Plan
      Outputs
      • Quality Review Documentation
        Main Description
        Prepare for subcontractor audit or review in accordance with the supplier Agreement.

        Determine the type of review needed (subcontractor progress review or subcontractor quality review) and gain agreement on the audit agenda with the subcontractor management.

        Decide if the audit will be performed by project team or by the delivery organization or by a third party appointed by the delivery organization.
        Key Considerations

        Reference OPAL Procedures:

        • Project Monitoring and Control Procedure (IMSP421)
        • Supplier Management Procedure (IMSP711)
