Task: Refine or prepare initial risk management plan (IMSP391)
This task will refine or prepare the initial risk management plan, establishing the plan to manage and document risks for the project.
Purpose

To reduce the impact of risk by identifying, analyzing, planning for, and managing them.

Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager

  • Risk Owner

      Outputs
      • Project Management System Summary

        Main Description

        This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern S1: Initiate phase.

        This task is part of activity that is the first to be launched when starting the execution of a project (or of a new major technical phase or sub-project), and needs a first set of project team members (at least the project manager) who will plan the activities necessary to get the project up and running.

        The key outputs of this task are the Risk Definition, and the Risk Management Plan which may be included in the Project Management System Summary.

        This task includes the following:

        • Prepare for risk management
          • Determine potential sources of risks
          • Determine the risk classifications to be used for grouping risks based on shared characteristics which aid in evaluating impact and probability, and in consolidating risks for mitigation and contingency planning
          • Define qualification criteria for the purpose of identifying and analyzing potential risks
          • Establish a risk management strategy
        • Identify and analyze risks
          • Receive or identify, review, and approve or reject risk
          • Assess and classify risk, and create risk definition
          • Assign risk owner and ensure analysis completed
          • Obtain risk reviews and agreements as needed
          • Prioritize risks
          • Update risk definitions
        • Plan risk response
          • Analyze risks
          • Allocate risk reserves
          • Identify mitigation countermeasures and define mitigation plan
          • Define contingency plan
          • Create Risk Management Plan, including reviews and updates as needed
