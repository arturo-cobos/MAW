Task: Prepare the orientation material for supplier (IMSP391 and IMSP711)
This task will prepare to welcome suppliers to the project by developing orientation material for them.
Purpose
To prepare for welcoming suppliers to the project, facilitating their integration within the project team, and ensuring they are brought up to speed with the work they have to perform.
