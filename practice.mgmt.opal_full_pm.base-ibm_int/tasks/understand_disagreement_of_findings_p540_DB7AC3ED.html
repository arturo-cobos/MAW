Task: Understand disagreement on the nature of findings (IMSP540)
Disciplines: Deliverables Management
Purpose
To understand better the nature of the disagreement by reviewing the findings and as a result be better prepared for negotiations with the sponsor.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Deliverable Definition
  • Findings Log
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Issue Document
      Main Description
      Review the findings received from the sponsor. Attempt to trace the findings back to the original requirements or specifications, and to understand why the sponsor claims these are defects.

      Determine the source of the ambiguity, the areas where non conformities should be recognized, and the areas that constitute a change.

      Determine the various solutions, including accepting changes at the sponsor expense, and their impact on the quality, coherence, and functionality of the deliverable.

      Determine optimal time scale options to implement the various solutions and estimate the related efforts, costs, and duration.

      Prepare for negotiating with the sponsor, in particular balancing the need to strictly enforce the terms in the Agreement as well as documented requirements or specifications with the need to achieve a high degree of sponsor satisfaction.
