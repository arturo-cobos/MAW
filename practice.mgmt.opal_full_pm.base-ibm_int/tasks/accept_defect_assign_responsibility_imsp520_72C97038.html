Task: Accept defect and assign responsibility (IMSP520)
Assign and investigate the defect.
Purpose
To assign the defect to an owner and perform an investigation into the defect.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Defect Log
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Defect Log
    • Findings Log
      Main Description

      Assign the defect to a Resolution Owner for further investigation and performs the following:

      • Assign the defect a planned resolution date
      • Assign the defect a severity code
      • Update the defect log and findings log as appropriate
      • Inform the resolution owner and other relevant stakeholders of the assignment

      For smaller teams the defect assignment can be delegated to team members, based on pre-defined responsibility areas.
