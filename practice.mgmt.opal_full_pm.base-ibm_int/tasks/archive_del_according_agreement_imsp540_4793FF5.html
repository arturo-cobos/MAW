Task: Archive deliverable and return sponsor components (IMSP540)
Disciplines: Deliverables Management
Purpose
To assemble and review deliverables and associated documentation and make sure that they are either returned to the sponsor or properly archived.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Agreement
  • Deliverable Definition
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Deliverable Definition
      Main Description
      Review the documentation and other materials associated with the deliverables to identify components that must be returned to the sponsor or that are suitable to be submitted as intellectual capital for future projects. Assemble and archive deliverable documentation. Launch maintenance or warranty activities for the deliverable, per terms in the project plans and sponsor Agreement.
