Task: Document and communicate final results (IMSP445)
This task will assess the potential risks of the proposed solution and communicate the final results to all interested parties.
Purpose

To document and communicate final results of the evaluation process and key decision.

Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Key Decision Record
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Key Decision Record
      Main Description

      Assess the potential risks associated with implementing the recommended solution.

      • Refer to the Continuous Risk Management Procedure (IMSP600) for information about how to assess and monitor risks.

      Document the results of the formal evaluation process, including the rationale for the selection of the recommended solution and the rejection of alternative solutions.

      Communicate the final results and distribute pertinent documentation to all relevant stakeholders.
       
      Document the cost to perform the evaluation and the estimated savings/value from performing this evaluation. Provide this information as lessons learned.
      Steps
      Assess the potential risks

      Assess the potential risks associated with implementing the recommended solution.

      • Refer to the Continuous Risk Management Procedure (IMSP600) for information about how to assess and monitor risks.
      Document the results
      Document the results of the formal evaluation process, including the rationale for the selection of the recommended solution and the rejection of alternative solutions.
      Obtain signoff

      Obtain signoff from all participants in the decision-making process to indicate agreement with the documented results of the formal evaluation process.

      Signoff by way of e-mail is acceptable.

      Communicate the final results and distributes documentation

      Communicate the final results and distribute pertinent documentation to all relevant stakeholders.

      Pertinent documentation may include:

      • Evaluation criteria
      • Selected methods
      • Alternative solutions
      • Rationale for the recommendation

      Document the cost to perform the evaluation and the estimated savings/value from performing this evaluation.  Provide this information as lessons learned.
