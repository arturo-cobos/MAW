Task: Consolidate costs and prepare budget and benefits case (IMSP491)
This task will ensure that all project costs are accounted for, the financial plan is updated and the benefits can be realized.
Purpose

To ensure that all project costs are accounted for and an appropriate financial plan is in place, such as having an appropriate income schedule or payment terms that provide for the recovery of costs over time.  Facilitate and confirm the client's and IBM's understanding of the complete business case.

        Main Description

        This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern P4: Finalize sponsor agreement.

        The key output of this task is the Financial Plan, which may be part of the Project Management System Summary and the Value Proposition. Inputs may include the Human Resource Plan, which may also be part of the Project Management System Summary.

        This task includes the following:

        • Consolidate costs and additional cost elements using all plans, such as:
          • Salaries and related costs
          • Contractor costs
          • Travel and subsistence expenses
          • Supplier stage payments
          • Supplier expenses
          • Hardware costs
          • Software costs
          • Training costs
          • Office space charges
          • Materiel costs
        • Document related assumptions using all plans
        • When appropriate, work with the pricing or finance organizations to determine a project income schedule that will ensure project cost recovery over time, and will be submitted to the sponsor's agreement
        • Update Financial Plan, which may be part of the Project Management System Summary
        • Analyze the business value of the solution from the key client stakeholders who either benefit or must adopt the solution.  Describe the business value in the Value Proposition.
