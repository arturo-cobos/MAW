Task: Assess supplier readiness to deliver (IMSP540 and IMSP711)
Disciplines: Deliverables Management
Purpose
To assess if a supplier or the client is ready to make a delivery according to the terms of the supplier or sponsor Agreement.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Deliverable Definition
  • Milestone List
    		Optional:
    • Agreement
    • Subcontractor Status Report
      Outputs
      • Issue Document
        Main Description

        Review delivery agreement terms for the supplier. Verify that the supplier or the sponsor is ready to deliver on the agreed date and with the expected level of quality.  

        If there is a possibility that the supplier will not be able to meet their commitments, raise a formal issue. \

        Refer to the Issue Management Procedure (IMSP620) for information about raising issues and associated actions.
         

        Key Considerations

        Reference OPAL Procedures:

        • Supplier Management Procedure (IMSP711)
