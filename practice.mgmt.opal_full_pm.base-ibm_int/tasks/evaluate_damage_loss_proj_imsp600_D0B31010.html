Task: Evaluate damage and loss to the project (IMSP600)
Disciplines: Risk Management
Purpose
To analyze the impact of a risk occurrence and understand the impact to the project.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
  • Contract Business Office (CBO)
InputsMandatory:
  • Issue Document
    		Optional:
    • Risk Occurrence Document
      Outputs
      • Action Control Document
      • Issue Document
      • Risk Occurrence Document
        Main Description
        Analyze the actual impact of the risk occurrence, review the project schedules, and evaluate damage and loss to the project. Raise Issue Documents and Action Control Documents as necessary.
