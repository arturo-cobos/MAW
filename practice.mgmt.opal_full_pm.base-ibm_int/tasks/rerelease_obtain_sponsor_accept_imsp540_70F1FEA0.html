Task: Re-release and obtain formal sponsor acceptance (IMSP540)
Disciplines: Deliverables Management
Purpose
To re-release a previously released deliverable that has been reworked because of sponsor findings and obtain formal acceptance of the deliverable.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Agreement
  • Deliverable Definition
    		Optional:
    • Delivery Control Documentation
      Outputs
      • Delivery Control Documentation
        Main Description

        When rework of a deliverable is complete, re-release all or part of the deliverable. Once findings have been dealt with, obtain the official document of sponsor acceptance for all (or those parts) of the deliverable that was (were) under validation as described in the sponsor Agreement.

        Initiate invoicing for delivery acceptance (if delivery is a payment milestone)
