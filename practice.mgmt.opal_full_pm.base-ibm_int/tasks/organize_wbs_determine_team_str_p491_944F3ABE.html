Task: Organize WBS and determine team structure (IMSP491)
This task determines and documents the resources that can undertake the project organizational activities, designs a team structure that best fits the activities to be performed and structures the activities so that responsibilities can be clearly assigned to components of the team structure.
Purpose
To organize the activities for the project organizational unit (POU), and understand and plan for the team structure needed to support them.
Relationships

      Main Description

      This task includes the following:

      • Determine the nature and number of resources that will be necessary to undertake the project organizational unit activities and, referring to relevant team structure models, design the team structure that best fits the activities to be performed.
      • Document in the Human Resource Plan and in the team structure of the Organizational Breakdown Structure (OBS) for the project organizational unit (POU).
      • For the activities that were identified in the initial project organizational unit Work Breakdown Structure (WBS), bundle into groups and structure so that responsibilities can be clearly assigned to components of the team structure.
      • Determine the activities and resources to manage the POU work as well as those for providing support when appropriate.
      • Document in the WBS for the POU being considered.
      • Conduct a self-assessment / peer review of the solution (in preparation for a Risk Management review).

      The key outputs of this task are the Work Breakdown Structure, Organizational Breakdown Structure; and the Human Resource Plan, which may be part of the Project Management System Summary.
