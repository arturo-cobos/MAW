Task: Identify situational factors (IMSP421)
Disciplines: Event Management
Purpose
To identify and evaluate the situational factors that might impact the success of the project.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:

        Main Description

        Review tracking records, meeting minutes, and any new information to identify trends and change in situational factors that may affect one or more dimensions of the project health framework.

        Consider the potential of these changing situational factors to hide other factors that might be a concern for the project's health.

        Determine healthy and unhealthy signs. Consequences may be opportunities that should be seized, as well as threats that should be eliminated.

        Utilize Seven Keys for Success to track the progress and understand the current health:

        • Scope is realistic and managed
        • Team is high performing
        • Work and schedule are accurately determined
        • Stakeholders are committed
        • Delivery organization's benefits are realized
        • Business benefits are realized
        • Risks are mitigated

        Analyze the potential consequences against current project health and discard those factors that have minimal consequences.
