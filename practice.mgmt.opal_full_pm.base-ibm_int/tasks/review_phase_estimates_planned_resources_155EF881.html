Task: Review phase estimates and planned resources
Disciplines: Work Plan Management
Purpose
To review and appraise the estimated costs and planned resources for a project organizational unit (POU).
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Estimation Report
  • Project Definition
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Estimation Report
    • Human Resource Plan
    • Organizational Breakdown Structure (OBS)
    • Project Management Schedule
    • Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)
      Main Description
      Determine the nature and amount of resources needed to undertake the project organizational unit (POU) activities and design the team structure that best fits the activities to be performed. Document in the Human Resource Plan and in the team structure of the Organizational Breakdown Structure (OBS) for the POU.

      At the same time, the activities that had been identified in the initial POU’s Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) are bundled into groups and structured so that responsibilities can be clearly assigned to members of the team structure.

      Lastly, determine the activities and resources to manage the POU work as well as those for providing support when appropriate. Document in the WBS for the POU being considered.

      Review the effort, duration, and amount of resources planned to perform the activities that constitute the critical path within the project organizational unit’s WBS, and adjust as necessary to optimize leads and lags. Document in the Project Management Schedule for the POU (it will be expanded when Starting the project).

      Appraise the costs related to internal human resources, contractors, travel and living expenses, office charges, etc., according to the expenditure categories that are required by the business unit. Document the POU estimates (to be consolidated later in the Financial Plan).
