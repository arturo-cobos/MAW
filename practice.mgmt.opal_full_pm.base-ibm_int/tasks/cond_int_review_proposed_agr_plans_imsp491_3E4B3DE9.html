Task: Conduct internal review of proposed agreements and plans (IMSP491)
This task will conduct internal reviews of the agreements and project plans by the response team and all other affected parties.
Purpose

To obtain commitment, agreement and approval on the agreements and project plans from those responsible for performing and supporting plan execution and other stakeholders.  This ensures that the plans provide a complete, consistent, coherent, accurate, and acceptable approach.

      Main Description

      This task is based on the WWPMM work pattern P4: Finalize sponsor agreement.

      The key outputs of this task are the Agreement, the appropriate plans which may be part of the Project Management System Summary, and the Work Product Inspection Form.

      This task includes the following:

      • Conduct internal reviews of the agreement and project plans with all stakeholders (the response team and all other affected parties), performing any needed revisions
      • Obtain commitments from the stakeholders responsible for performing and supporting plan execution, performing any needed revisions
      • Obtain approvals and agreements
