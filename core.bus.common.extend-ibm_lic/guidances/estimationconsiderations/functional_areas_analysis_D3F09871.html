Main Description

For each Domain that falls within the scope of the current project, estimate the following to document its functional areas.


Low High
Prepare for interview or work session 1 4
Interviews or facilitated work sessions 7 24
Documenting 2 8
Validation and final revisions 2 4
Total 12 40



The total duration and the amount of effort required to develop Functional Areas will vary depending on:

  • Number of in-scope business domains
  • Number of in-scope functional areas within each domain
  • Functional complexity of the domains and functional areas
  • Quality and completeness of the input work products (i.e. CBM can provide a significant amount of useful input, other business analysis activities may or may not provide that level of detail)
  • Experience of team (in technique and industry)
