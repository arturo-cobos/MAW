Artifact: Benefit Realization Plan (ART 0711)
This artifact outlines how the expected benefits of the project will be tracked and the activities that will be used to periodically measure whether or not the project is on track to deliver that business value.
Purpose
The purpose of this work product is to measure the business benefits realized from the project including the overall approach for tracking value realization.
Description
Main DescriptionThis work product will include both tangible (financial) and intangible (non-financial) measures.
Brief Outline

This artifact contains the following sections:

  • Expected Benefits - the benefits that will be measured and tracked are identified and valued (using appropriate discounted cash flow techniques)
  • Benefits Action Plans:
    • Key Performance Indicators - for each benefit with assigned executive owner
    • Data Collection Points - key intervals throughout the lifecycle for measuring benefits to ensure expectations continue to be met
    • Change Enablers - actions in the area of People, Process, Technology and Organization are included in order to define how to realize the benefit
  • Impact of Cost & Risk Management - describe how inputs from cost management and risk management will be included
Key Considerations

Do not shortcut this exercise – it's a simple sell to C level client executives. Where resistance is met – from either the IBM or Client project managers or team leads – the only route is to engage a client who believes in 'what gets measured gets done'.

To be developed in partnership with the client. A financial analyst from client is required to collect data and perform analysis, in sync with the client specific capital expenditure process. In addition, a number of key client staff who have knowledge of the client business and the potential of the ERP functionality should be involved in defining the benefits, KPIs and enablers.

Tailoring
Impact of not havingThe project may never deliver an appropriately acceptable Return on Investment – leading to the inability to secure initial funding and possibly not meeting expectations.
Reasons for not needingThis artifact may not be needed if the project was mandated and it is unnecessary to track benefits, or in some cases this work may not be in scope for the delivering organization.
