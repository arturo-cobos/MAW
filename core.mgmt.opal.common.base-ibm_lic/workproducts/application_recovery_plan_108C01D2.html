Artifact: Application Recovery Plan (OPS 414)
This artifact documents the recovery actions to be taken for a critical application if a business disaster or interruption to that application occurs.
Domains: Business Continuity
Work Product Kinds: Plan
Purpose
Its purpose is to provide an effective plan to ensure continuity or rapid restoration of a specific critical application following a business disaster or interruption to that application.

Brief Outline

This artifact typically contains:

  • Application details
  • Contact details
  • Application backup details, such as type, frequency, time and storage details
  • Highest disaster recovery classification
  • Highest security classification
  • Disaster recovery paper drill details
  • Disaster recovery test details
  • Recovery requirements
  • Recovery procedures

Key Considerations
This artifact is specific to an application and may be aligned with an overall disaster recovery plan for an organization.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not having this artifact are:

  • Restoration of an application in the event of a business disaster becomes very difficult
  • Identification of recovery timeframes and dependencies will be very difficult
  • Complete halt of business in the event of a disaster leading to possible heavy financial or business losses
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is not needed if there are no critical applications and disaster recovery is not supported.
