Outcome: Matter requiring key decision
This outcome indicates that a decision must be made on a matter and due to the nature of the decision, it requires a formal decision making process to be used.
Work Product Kinds: Completion
Purpose
Its purpose is to ensure that when a key decision is to be made, that the situation is recognized and the appropriate process is followed, so that all appropriate information is considered in order to make the best decision.
