Artifact: Process Assurance Review Report - Organization (ENG 442)
This artifact provides a list of items to be verified when performing reviews of an organization's compliance to its processes, and provides documentation of the review results.
Domains: Quality Management
Work Product Kinds: Assessment
Purpose
Its purpose is to provide the criteria for performing an organizational level compliance review, to assure that processes are being followed appropriately.

Impact of not having

Consequences of not using this work product are:

  • Inconsistency in verifying the process compliance of the organizational level procedures
  • Lack of objectivity and completeness while verifying process compliance
  • Evidence of conformance may not be recorded consistently
  • Overall compliance ratings may not be readily available leading to difficulty in reporting to senior management on the compliance status.
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is not required if process assurance reviews are not conducted.
