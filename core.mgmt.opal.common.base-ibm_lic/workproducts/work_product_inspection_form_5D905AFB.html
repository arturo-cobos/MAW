Artifact: Work Product Inspection Form (APP 413)
This artifact provides a record of the execution of a review of a work product that is performed during the project lifecycle, which includes issues and recommendations for improvements identified during the inspection.
Domains: Quality Management, Test
Work Product Kinds: Assessment, Record
Purpose
Its purpose is to record the details of a work product inspection including the defects found during that  inspection, any associated issues and risks, and added improvements to increase the quality of the work product.

Brief Outline

This artifact typically contains:

  • Project information
  • Meeting arrangements
  • Work product information
  • Participants
  • Defects identified, including location, description, severity and other classification information
  • Issues
  • Risks
  • Suggestions for added value
  • Exit decision

Key Considerations
This inspection is conducted in a formal meeting, however, it can be conducted individually using the artifact to document individual review comments about the work product.
Tailoring
Impact of not havingWithout this artifact the integrity and quality of the artifact my be impacted, depending on the severity of its defects. Not having defects documented in detail could be very costly if they are not fixed as early as possible in the project lifecycle.
Reasons for not needingIf the details of the defect and its resolution are negligible, then the information may be documented elsewhere.
