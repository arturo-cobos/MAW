Outcome: Policy, Standard, or Procedure Exception need
This outcome is used to identify the need for an exception to an established policy, standard or procedure that would normally apply to a project.
Work Product Kinds: Completion
Purpose
Its purpose is to identify the need for an exception, so it can be documented, reviewed and approved, as appropriate, for a project.

Tailoring
Impact of not havingWithout this outcome, an exception to a policy, standard or procedure may not be appropriately identified, reviewed and approved, and a project may be out of compliance with what would normally be done for the project, resulting in a failed review or audit.
Reasons for not needingThis outcome is not needed when an exception to a policy, standard, or procedure is not needed for a project.
