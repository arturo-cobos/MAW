Artifact: Key Decision Record (ENG 431)
This artifact provides a record of a key decision, and the criteria and evaluation information used to make the decision.
Domains: Project Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose

Its purpose is to help make a key decision and provide a record of the process to reach that decision.  This artifact is used when there is a  need to to analyze possible decisions using a formal evaluation process that considers more than one alternative solution, and at least one of the following is true:

  • The decision is likely to have a significant impact on the scope, schedule, cost or quality of the project
  • The decision is associated with a risk that has a Risk Rating of Medium or High

Description
Brief Outline

This artifact typically contains:

  • Project information
  • General information, such as matter requiring decision, application, scope of key decision
  • Evaluation criteria
  • Alternative solutions and evaluation results
  • Evaluation methods
  • Recommended solution and rationale for selection
  • Measurement for cost and effort
  • Participants signoff

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Lack of this artifact leads to:

  • Unclear responsibilities in decision for the key matters.
  • Lack of communication about the decisions that are made.
  • Contradictory or inadequate decisions that could hinder project progress.
  • Lack of information for justifying the decisions made.
Reasons for not needing

Following are reasons for not needing this artifact:

  • The decision is not related to a matter that involves a risk with a Risk Rating of Medium or High
  • The decision could not have a significant impact on the scope, schedule, cost or quality of the project
  • The costs of a formal evaluation process are unreasonable when compared to the decision's impact
