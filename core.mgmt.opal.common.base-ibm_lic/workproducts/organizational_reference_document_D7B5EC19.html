Artifact: Organizational Reference Document (ORG 417)
This artifact documents an organization's policies, standards and processes, and also documents the process asset library assets (such as project management procedures, guidance and work products) in use for that organization's projects.
Domains: Standards & Policies
Work Product Kinds: Standard
Purpose
Its purpose is to provide documentation for the organization and its staff on what information and assets are to be used for that organization, such as what procedures and work products to use on projects.

Brief Outline

This artifact typically contains:

  • Organizational Scope
  • Organizational Policies and Standards
  • Organizational Reference Document (ORD) Workbook, documenting the process asset library  assets, such as procedures, guidance, and work products in use by the organization
  • Organizational Reporting of Project Health and Status
  • Organizational Measurement Program
  • Organizational Training Program
  • Organizational Work Classification Criteria
  • Organizational Problem Management
  • Organization's Approved Lifecycle Models
  • Engineering Process Group Charter
  • PQA Group Structure
  • Configuration Management Board
  • Mandatory Approvals
  • Mandatory Reviews
  • Organizational Work Environment
  • Organizational Tools, Training and Guidance
  • Organizational Business Controls

Impact of not having

Lack of this artifact leads to:

  • Having no single point of reference on information for the organization
  • Absence of a standard way for the organization to store their local information
  • Inconsistency across projects that must comply with a standard for their organization and do not have access to this information
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is not needed if this information is documented and available to the organization elsewhere, such as in another work product.
