Artifact: Process and Technology Improvement Request and Evaluation Form (ENG 440)
This artifact documents a process or technology improvement request and its evaluation result.
Domains: Organization Change Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose

Its purpose is to document the required information related to a process or a technology improvement request, its evaluation result and the final disposition.

Description
Brief Outline

This artifact typically contains:

  • Process/Technology improvement identifier, such as request number
  • Requestor information, such as name and contact details
  • Process/Technology improvement request information, such as
    • Date submitted (and revision date, if needed)
    • Description of request, asset name and identifier for which this improvement is requested
    • Supporting attachments (if any)
    • Benefits
  • Process/Technology improvement proposal evaluation, such as
    • Team disposition
    • Rationale for Team Disposition
    • Process Area
    • Category
    • Priority
    • Related Quality and Process Performance Objective
    • Source
    • Impact of implementing the process/technology improvement request
    • Estimated effort
    • Impact
    • Method for evaluating effectiveness after Deployment
  • Additional evaluation to be performed for innovative improvements, such as
    • Cost analysis (qualitative or quantitative cost and benefit)
    • Benefit analysis
    • Potential barriers/risks in implementing the improvement
    • Estimated effort and duration of deployment
  • Improvement Validation Plan
    • Other tools evaluated
  • Disposition/Approval
Illustrations
Templates
  • Process and Technology Improvement Log
  • Process and Technology Improvement Request and Evaluation Form
Key Considerations
Multiple Process or Technology improvement requests may be collated in a log form for efficiently tracking the identified improvement requests.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not having this work product are:

  • No formally documented description of the improvement request
  • Poor decision making in prioritizing the improvement requests that need to be implemented
Reasons for not needingThis may not be required if there is a tool or another mechanism used for tracking this information.
