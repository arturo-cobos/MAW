Artifact: Organizational PQA Non-Compliance Log (ART 0576)
This artifact documents non-compliant items (NCI) found during Process Assurance reviews, including the details related to the resolution of those items.
Domains: Quality Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Extends: Action Log
Purpose

Its purpose is to ensure that non-compliance items from a process assurance review are managed and resolved.  It also provides documentation that can be analyzed to recognize trends in non-compliance, which may trigger a need for training or process improvements.

Brief Outline

This artifact typically contains:

  • Non-compliant Item (NCI) number or other identifier
  • Project identifier
  • Details of procedure, standard, or other related to NCI, including NCI description
  • Reviewer and review date when NCI found
  • NCI resolution information, such as action owner, related dates and other details

Key Considerations

Once determined, actions are recorded in the Action Log, unless simply listed in meeting minutes or status reports, on small projects.

The Action Log is then updated when actions change status.

It is reviewed periodically to monitor execution of actions, and also to analyze trends in actions.

The Action Log must be up-to-date and the information recorded for each action must be consistent with the corresponding Action Control Documents, if any were created.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without this artifact, there is:

  • No visibility into the open PQA non-compliance items and their status
  • No single source of PQA non-compliance data to use to identify trends that may lead to the identification of a need for training or process improvements
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is not needed if this information is documented and available to the organization elsewhere, such as in an Action Log or another work product, or if process assurance reviews are not conducted by the organization.
