Outcome: Orientation Material for Supplier
This outcome is used to introduce a supplier to a project, providing the supplier with information such as procedures and tools to be used on that project.
Work Product Kinds: Completion
Purpose
Its purpose is to provide the supplier with the information they need to participate as a team member on a project.
