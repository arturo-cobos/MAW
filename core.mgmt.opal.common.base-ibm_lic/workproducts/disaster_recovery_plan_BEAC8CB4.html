Artifact: Disaster Recovery Plan (OPS 415)
This artifact documents the response and recovery activities required in handling a business disaster or interruption that could make vital client support unavailable for any period of time.
Domains: Business Continuity
Work Product Kinds: Plan
Purpose
Its purpose is to document the planned actions required to restore business critical processes and applications in the event of a business disaster or interruption.

Tailoring
Impact of not havingWithout this artifact, there will be no documented plan to address the response and restoration of business continuity in the event of a disaster . Improper handling of business disasters may lead to business loss, financial loss and loss of goodwill.
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is not needed if disaster recovery is not supported or if this information is documented and available elsewhere, such as in another work product.
