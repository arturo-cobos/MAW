Outcome: Level of Project Management Defined
This outcome indicates the appropriate level of project management to apply to a project has been determined, based on organizational standards and on project characteristics such as work classification and other project attributes.
Work Product Kinds: Confirmation
Purpose
Its purpose is to ensure that the project is managed appropriately such as using the correct work breakdown structure, applying the correct standards and guidance, and applying the appropriate levels of granularity and formality, etc.

Tailoring
Impact of not havingWithout this outcome, projects may not be managed at the appropriate level, for example, a small project could be managed in accordance with the rules for a large project, which may negatively impact the project by imposing the creation of unneeded work products and the performance of unnecessary tasks.
Reasons for not needingThis outcome is not needed if all projects are managed the same way and there is no level to define.
