Artifact: Organization Level Measurement Program (ENG 439)
This artifact provides a document where an organization can record the details of its measurement program, including the project and organization metrics to be collected and reported on, along with information on the activities and infrastructure to support the program.
Domains: IT Management & Monitoring
Work Product Kinds: Procedure
Purpose

Its purpose is to:

  • Establish a measurement program and supporting infrastructure
  • Establish and maintain measurement program objectives including quality and process performance objectives, in line with the organization business objectives
  • Enable the organization to plan, perform, and manage the appropriate measurement activities
  • Enable projects to plan, perform, and manage the appropriate measurement activities in accordance with the organizations's established measurement program
  • Maximize the value of the organizations's measurement program

Brief Outline

This artifact typically includes:

  • Program objectives
  • Related business goals
  • Criteria for selecting processes or subprocesses
  • Training
  • Rules
  • Practices
  • Mandatory and recommended metrics, such as specific product, process, earned value (process) or other metrics
  • Traceability across business goals, objectives, processes, subprocesses, measures/metrics, associated statistical techniques and performance models
  • Metric attributes
  • Data gathering rules and processes
  • Targets and statistical control limits
  • Performance models
  • Early warning notifications
  • Statistical analysis
  • Repository location and related information
  • Validating, maintaining and submitting project data
  • Benchmarking
  • Roles and responsibilities
  • Reporting

Impact of not having

Consequences of not having this work product are:

  • No organizational level measurement focus
  • Definition of measures within projects may vary, making analysis of data difficult at the organization level
  • Absence of organization level targets leading to difficulty in project level benchmarking
Reasons for not needingThis artifact is not needed if this information is documented and available to the organization elsewhere, such as in another work product or if there is no measurement program supported.
