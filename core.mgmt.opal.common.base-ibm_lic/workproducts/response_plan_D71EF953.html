Artifact: Response Plan (ENG 343a)
This artifact documents the activities that must be performed in order to prepare a response to a service request or project charter.
Domains: Project Management
Work Product Kinds: Plan
Purpose
Its purpose is to effectively and efficiently conduct the solution design phase in response to the opportunity presented by a work request.  It covers the activities to develop the response package but is not the response itself.

Brief Outline

This artifact typically contains information on the following activities:

  • Define and plan the project
  • Develop the solution strategy
  • Prepare the schedule of tasks in the solution design phase
  • Record the resources, estimates, costs, budgets, and approvals
  • Specify the risk assessment
  • Perform quality assurance assessments
  • Package the response
  • Review and approve the response
  • Submit the response for acceptance
  • Close the response

Impact of not having

Lack of this work product could lead to:

  • Difficulty in tracking and monitoring the response preparation
  • Delivering an inadequate response
  • Failure in meeting the expectations of the sponsor
Reasons for not needingThis artifact may not be needed for projects that are low cost, low effort and minimal risk (with no risks that are critical or high).
