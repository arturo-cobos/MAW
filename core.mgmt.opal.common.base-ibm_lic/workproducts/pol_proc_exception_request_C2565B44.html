Artifact: Policy or Process Exception Request (BUS 445)
This artifact documents a request to deviate from organizational policies, standards or procedures, and documents the business need and approval.
Domains: Standards & Policies
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose
Its purpose is to document the exceptions requested to the organization policies, standards and procedures, and obtain approval for the exceptions from relevant authorities.

Brief Outline

This artifact typically contains:

  • Project identification information
  • Exception request information, such as Process Asset Library reference, description and justification of exception, and alternative process (if applicable)
  • Exception status, including approval information and reasons for decisions

Impact of not having

Consequences of not having this artifact (where a project identifies a business need for exceptions to be raised) are:

  • No formal documentation and record of exceptions requested, approved and rejected
  • No formal review and tracking of the exception request, which may result in serious violation of essential policies, standards and procedures.
