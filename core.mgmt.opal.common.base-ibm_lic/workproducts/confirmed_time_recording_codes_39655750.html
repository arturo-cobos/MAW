Outcome: Confirmed Time Recording Codes
This outcome ensures that the appropriate codes are available for resources to record the time spent on a work effort.
Work Product Kinds: Confirmation
Purpose
Its purpose is to provide resources with the information they need so they can record their effort time appropriately.
