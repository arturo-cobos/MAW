Template: Training Plan and Tracker
This excel worksheet template documents project specific training plan and it also records the result of training conducted such as revision to skill level and actual completion date.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Training Plan and Tracker
Main Description

This template captures information such as:

  • Skills or knowledge area required for project resources
  • Skill level required
  • Current skill level
  • Training required for addressing skill gaps if any
  • Mode of training
  • Method of assessing the skill level after the training
  • Status, Remarks to provide waiver details, if applicable
