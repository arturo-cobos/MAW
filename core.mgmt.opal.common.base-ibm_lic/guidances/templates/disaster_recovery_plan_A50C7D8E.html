Template: Disaster Recovery Plan
This MS Word template is used to capture the planning details of activities that are required to be executed in the event of a business disaster.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Disaster Recovery Plan
Main Description

The template typically contains the following sections:

  • Disaster resumption plan including the implementation details, contact details and recovery process 
  • Application recovery plan including the details of critical applications, backup type, schedule, recovery requirements and procedures 
  • Maintenance Strategy which includes the review trigger and testing activities 
  • Communication plan which includes the delivery audience, delivery method, delivery schedule and delivery information
