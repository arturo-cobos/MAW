Main Description

The purpose of the plan is to effectively and efficiently conduct the Solution Design phase in response to the opportunity presented by a work request. It covers the activities done in developing the response package, starting from planning and defining the response till closing the response. It typically contains following sections:

  • Customer contact
  • References
  • Definitions and acronyms
  • Solution strategy
  • Deliverables
  • Schedule
  • Quality assurance activities
  • Resources
  • Estimates, costs and budgets
  • Required approvals
  • Risk assessment
