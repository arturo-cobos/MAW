Guideline: Quality Management
This guideline elaborates upon the ways and means to quantitatively manage quality of deliverables within a project.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Develop the initial project quality plan (IMSP491)
  • Establish project management system (IMSP391)
  • Identify deliverables quality requirements (IMSP391 and IMSP230)
Main Description

This guidance provides details about how to establish measurable quality goals for a project, how to tailor goals for a project, how to develop a quality strategy to meet established goals, how a defect management environment is set up, how to use quality models and how to do quantitative monitoring and reporting to manage the project.
