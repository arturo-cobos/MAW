Role: Contract Business Office (CBO)
A Contract Business Office is the organizational unit responsible contract business management practices (normally through the implementation of a Project Management Methodology).
Role Sets: Business, Team
Relationships
Additionally Performs
  • Close risk (IMSP600)
  • Evaluate damage and loss to the project (IMSP600)
  • Identify contingency plan and assess workaround actions (P600)
  • Monitor progress and effectiveness of contingency plan (IMSP600)
  • Prepare initial risk management plan (IMSP491)
