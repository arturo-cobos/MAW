Artifact: Formative Measurement Review (ORG 404)
The Formative Measurement Review process is used to ensure that the learning intervention designed, developed and delivered is consistent with plans for human performance improvement.
Domains: Learning
Work Product Kinds: Assessment
Purpose
The Formative Measurement Review is important for maximizing the effectiveness of the learning intervention. It provides valuable input to the customer about the status of the instructional design and identifies problems with the proposed intervention that might inhibit the achievement of learning and business objectives. Often learning interventions deviate from the front-end analysis and related requirements, and consequently do not achieve the desired results - on-the-job behavior change. The use of the Formative Measurement Review should increase the likelihood that the learning interventions developed do support the organization’s achievement of positive business results.
Description
Main DescriptionThe Formative Measurement Review focuses on the completion of a checklist for instructional designers to use while designing, developing, and delivering learning solutions. The checklist is a comprehensive set of review items relevant maximizing the probability that learning interventions will enable behavior changes needed to achieve business impact. The Formative Measurement Review is designed to ensure successful business results are achieved.

Key Considerations

The following advice and guidance for using the Formative Measurement Review work product:

  • Make sure that all consultants working on the project are familiar with the Formative Measurement Review Checklist.
  • Ensure that the client is sophisticated enough to use the Formative Measurement Review Checklist.
  • Be aware that building the Formative Measurement Review work product into the Statement of Work might raise significant issues relating to the client organization’s approach to learning. Most organizations do not have the performance-based approach to intervention design that is exemplified by the checklist items.
  • The Formative Measurement Review work product is an excellent opportunity to expand the engagement beyond measurement and sell a Learning Strategy engagement.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without the Formative Measurement Review work product:

  • Learning interventions will tend to be weak and ineffective
  • Learning interventions are unlikely to have an impact on the client’s business
  • Learning interventions are unlikely to change behavior on-the-job
Reasons for not needing

The Formative Measurement Review work product will be of limited value, and should probably not be used, under the following circumstances:

  • When training is ad hoc
  • When there is little or no instructional design being done
  • When there is no desire to improve an intervention that is already designed
