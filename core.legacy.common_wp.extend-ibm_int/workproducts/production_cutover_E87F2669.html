Outcome: Production Cutover
The outcome covers gaining approval from the Client to initiate the final actions to prepare the ‘operationally ready’ package implementation.
Work Product Kinds: Approval
Main Description

This is the milestone necessary to agree to the commencement of all of the cut-over preparations on the technical, application and the organizational areas in order to finalize the package implementation for hand-over to the business for production operation.

When all preparations are complete, then it also gains the approval for accepting the package implementation as ‘live’.

This is a highly complex and technically demanding piece of work. It essential that all key Client personnel responsible for any parts of the subtasks involved attends walk through sessions for each of the individual plans.

Be focused on the fact that all of the subtasks contained related to this milestone must be performed on a fully configured production system, as specified and agreed by the Client representatives.
