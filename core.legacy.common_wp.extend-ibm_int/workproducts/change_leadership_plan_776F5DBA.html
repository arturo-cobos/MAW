Artifact: Change Leadership Plan (ORG 300)
This work product describes how to organize, develop, and support change leaders, and assists them in directing the organization toward a new business or organizational model.
Domains: Organization Change Management
Work Product Kinds: Plan
Purpose
The change will be successful only if it is effectively led from within the organization. The purpose of this work product is to identify key individuals who can act as role models for change and to support them in their activities.

Description
Main DescriptionChange leaders are individuals at all levels of an organization who have the responsibility or opportunity to encourage and guide change efforts. 

This artifact includes:

  • A set of assessment reports and plans
  • Selected education programs for change leaders
  • Materials to assist change leaders in performing their roles 
Key Considerations

Ensure that change leadership requirements are built into the normal performance measures and incentive plans of change leaders.  Experience shows that when change requirements are simply added to other measured accountabilities, the change activities receive less priority and execution suffers.

A specific review of leadership activities and results should be held with the project sponsor or steering body at regular intervals in order to maintain focus and gain sponsorship of corrective actions.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

If this work product is not used, implementing the changes will take longer and will be more difficult:

  • Key individuals may resent and oppose the change if they do not feel actively involved in the process.
  • Valuable insight into how best to implement the changes will be lost. 
  • Change leaders will not be able to sell the benefits of the changes effectively. 
  • Other managers may deploy resources and pursue goals that are inconsistent with the change project.
Reasons for not needingThis work product may not be required in organizations where change is embraced as an everyday activity, and where the capability for leading change efforts in addition to performing daily work is present.
