Artifact: Contract Templates (ENG 427)
Contract templates are documents that define and allow users to create contracts and agreements with suppliers.
Domains: Relationship Management
Work Product Kinds: Standard
Purpose

To give end users a standard contract template that allows for ease of development and renewal. The standard contracts also provides consistency across all areas of the client company and its suppliers. The contract templates are necessary to effectively leverage strategies, volumes, contracting efficiencies and scope.

The achieve the following:

  • Provide greater business opportunity for both parties
  • Efficiency with suppliers
  • Maintain long-term relationships with suppliers by using model agreements that do not expire and that provide for legal/business separation
Description
Main Description

The Contract Templates reflect the standardized structures the client wishes to enforce across all supplier agreements. A number of different template contracts may exist reflecting the client’s desired outcomes from supplier negotiation processes.

Contract templates are a textual, legal approved documents that contain a client’s standard contract templates.  The templates should contain key phrases, terms and conditions that can be copied and pasted into new contract templates.

Key Considerations
  • Convert as many existing contracts as possible to the new model agreement.
    • Develop timeline for conversion of other contracts
  • Engage legal department and/or resources from the start. Provide education for initiative and ensure they support the concept.
  • The document should contain legal terms and conditions, standard contract phrases that can be verified by reviewing the client’s existing supplier contracts.
  • Areas that can be easily standardized include: T's & C's; Performance of Obligations, Indemnification, Limitation of Liability.
Tailoring
Impact of not havingIt will be more difficult for team members to execute a commodity strategy. More time will be spent on each project re-creating contracts for individual suppliers.
Reasons for not needingThe contracts vary for each supplier and situation thereby eliminating the need for standard, reusable clauses.
