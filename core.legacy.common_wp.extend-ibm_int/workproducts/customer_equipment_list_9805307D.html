Artifact: Customer Equipment List (OPS 409)
The Customer Equipment List Report work product is a list of purchased hardware, organized and grouped by client location that includes the machine type, model and serial numbers.
Domains: Business Continuity
Work Product Kinds: Inventory
Purpose
It is produced to provide a client with a list of technology for warranty, insurance, asset management, maintenance, and for IBM Global Services buyback programs to register the equipment for IBM warranty.
Description
Main DescriptionThis work product is produced to provide a client with a list of technology for warranty, insurance, asset management, maintenance, and for IBM Global Services buyback programs to register the equipment for IBM warranty. It is created as output from a database or project spreadsheet.
Brief Outline

The following fields are common on a Customer Equipment List Report:

  • Client Name 
  • Street Address 
  •  City 
  •  State 
  •  Zip Code 
  •  Equipment Model/Type 
  •  Description of Equipment 
  •  Serial Number 
  •  Quantity

Key Considerations

The following things should be considered for customer equipment listing:

  • Once the list is created, make sure that the equipment is listed in the IBM warranty system via spot-checking. The client should be able to request repairs under warranty, even if the hardware is purchased from IBM Global Services. 
  •  Give the list to the client in hardcopy and softcopy.
Tailoring
Impact of not having
  • Clients will not be able to easily sign up for Maintenance.
  • Clients may not be able to get past warranty entitlement screening.
  • Clients may have to re-inventory newly installed technology to update asset management systems.
  • IBM Global Services will not be able to easily update IBM internal systems to reflect correct warranty entitlement.
Reasons for not needing
  • The deployed technology is not serialized (it does not have unique serial numbers, which is rare).
