Artifact: Vendor Selection Decision and Final Presentation (BUS 084)
The Vendor Selection Decision and Final Presentation presents the findings from the selection process to identify a final software vendor or product for client consideration.
Domains: Selection
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose

The purpose of the Vendor Selection Decision and Final Presentation work product is to provide the results from the selection process in a structured presentation to the client on the software vendor and product finalist recommended by the project selection team. This work product serves as a reference guide for the client to justify, to their organization, why the vendor finalist was selected. The Vendor Selection Decision and Final Presentation documents the information, process and the findings of selection completed by the project team.

The work product is used to help the client understand the approach that the IBM team uses to identify with the client, the preferred software vendor for their future business and environment needs. The structured presentation summarizing the key results and findings should also be presented to the client steering committee to gain acceptance of the process and the final deliverables of the project.

Description
Main Description

This work product provides the rationale for the selection and includes the details for the exclusion of the other short list vendors and products and then summarizes the results, findings and conclusions for the client executives. This work product focuses in on the key results for the selection process. This includes:

  • Selection of the Vendor Finalist
  • Summary of the High-Level Gaps for the Selected Software
  • Development of a High-Level Implementation Strategy
  • Potential Business Case Results
  • Next Steps Discussion
Brief Outline

The main sections of the work product may include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Cover Sheet
  • Table of Contents
  • Overview of the Final Selection Process
  • Vendor Identification Summary
  • Request for Proposal/Solution Response Summary
  • Demonstration Performance Summary
  • Reference Check Results
  • Initial Cost Estimates
  • High Level Gap or “Pros and Cons”
  • Technical Considerations
  • Elimination Criteria and Justification
  • Finalist Recommendation

Tailoring
Impact of not havingElimination of this work product may leave the client sponsor unclear of the selection results, justification for moving forward with implementation, and misdirected as to what actions they need to take to further the success of the entire project.
Reasons for not needingIf the client executives and client sponsor are heavily involved in the selection process and have seen all components that comprise this work product, a formal presentation may not be necessary. The delivery of the Project Book with the executive summary should provide the final results the client needs to bring closure to this phase of the engagement.
Representation Options

The Vendor Selection Decision and Final Presentation can take several different forms in addition to presentation format.  The information can be displayed in a text document, via spreadsheet tables, or as a presentation.  However, the main advantage of the work product is to take the client through the final selection recommendation process.

Again, the format of this work product can vary tremendously.  This work product should reflect the concerns and focus of the client.
