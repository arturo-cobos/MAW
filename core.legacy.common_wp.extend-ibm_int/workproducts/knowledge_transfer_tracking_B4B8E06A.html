Artifact: Knowledge Transfer Tracking Reports (ART 0684)
Knowledge Transfer Reports capture and track knowledge transfer requirements during the course of the ERP Program.
Domains: Knowledge Management
Work Product Kinds: Inventory
Purpose

Knowledge Transfer Reports are required in order to enable:

  • Focus: enabling the client and consultant team to focus on those areas which are crucial for knowledge transfer.
  • Self-sufficiency: enable the client to support the system and perform enhancement, upgrades, additional roll-outs.
  • Client Satisfaction: client are demanding that knowledge transfer be part of any consulting engagement, in order to build implementation skills in client organization.
  • Add-on-sales: additional skills development programs can be development to build deeper consulting, people, process and technology skills as an add-on to the existing project.
Description
Main DescriptionThese Reports can be leveraged to track aggregate knowledge transfer per major project deliverables, as well as the tracking of knowledge transfer per individual client project team member.
Key Considerations
