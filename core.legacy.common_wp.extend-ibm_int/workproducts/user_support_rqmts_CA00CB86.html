Artifact: User Support Requirements (ART 0688)
This is the post-implementation support for end-users with technical and functional questions related to the new system.
Domains: Deployment
Work Product Kinds: Requirement
Description
Main Description

This covers the support of the day-to-day maintenance of the system, and describes the extent of IBM's involvement. The output of is a proposal or service level agreement covering the support IBM offers.

This will specify the contents of the service level agreement. This proposal should cover aspects such as:

What type of support is needed:

  • Supporting/answering questions from the users.
  • Re-customizing the system.
  • Further development.
  • Response time and resolution time.
  • Stand-by agreement.
  • Ad hoc support.
  • Commercial aspects.
  • Prices.
  • Idle time.
  • Quality of support, and how this will be measured.

This should be coordinated with the team that will perform the maintain phase of the follow-on project. This is one of the key areas for hand-off and knowledge transfer to the team.
