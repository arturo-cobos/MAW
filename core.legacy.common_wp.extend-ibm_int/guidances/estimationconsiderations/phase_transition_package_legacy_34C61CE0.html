Main Description
Estimates will vary depending upon the number of work products associated with each phase. If the work sheet is updated as work products are completed, a half-day workshop with the team should be more than sufficient to assess work product suitability for inclusion in the Intellectual Capital Management database.
 
Depending upon the complexity of the next Phase, completion of the phase project plan and associated estimates may require 16 to 40 hours and the client workshop may require 2 to 8 hours.
 
The number of staffing changes between phases will determine the amount of time required to complete the resource-related activities. The Team Feedback work product estimates are useful to determine the amount of time per person to release someone. The Project Manager should take less than an hour to confirm all the staffing with the appropriate resource deployment manager, even if there are no immediate changes to be made.
