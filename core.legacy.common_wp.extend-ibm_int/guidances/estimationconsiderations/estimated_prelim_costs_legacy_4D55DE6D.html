Main Description
The development of the Estimated Preliminary Costs can vary widely depending on the scope and size of the project.
 
Normally the vendors are able to generate and present adequate estimates to fulfill the needed range of values. Typically the task would involve a small portion of a Consultant's time during the demonstration tasks of the project. The gathering and presentation of the information in a normalized fashion will take approximately one to two person-days. Extra hours may be required for unusual requirements such as gathering multiple estimates or finding additional resources to provide estimates.
 
The client should be prepared to assign a financial director/manager to work with the Project Manager/Functional Team Leaders to review and verify the estimates, since they will be the basis of estimates for the final project and budgets. It should be recognized that more accurate information would be available in 4-6 weeks at the Selection Phase conclusion.
