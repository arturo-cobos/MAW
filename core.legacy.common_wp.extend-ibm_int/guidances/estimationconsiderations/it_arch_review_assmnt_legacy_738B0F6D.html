Main Description
The time taken to complete this work product will depend on the scope of the assessment required. For example, it takes longer to complete an assessment of a full IT Architecture covering Applications, Data and Technical Architectures, than one for a Technical Architecture only. A review or assessment can normally be completed in a 2 - 4 week elapsed timeframe, with at least 2 consultants involved. We have also seen a review completed in a concentrated one-week timeframe with 6 - 8 consultants participating!
