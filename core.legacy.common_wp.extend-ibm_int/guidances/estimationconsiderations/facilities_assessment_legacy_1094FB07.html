Main Description

The following dependencies should be taken into consideration when estimating the time and resources required to complete this task and develop this work product:

  • Size of organization  
  • Number of locations
  • Geography
  • Number of on-site visits  
  • Quality of documentation
  • Awareness of what is in place throughout the organization 

Typically, this data could be captured within two to three days with one to two consultants per facility. The expectation is the customer will have received a questionnaire at least two to three weeks in advance. If the questionnaire must be developed an additional 40 hours should be budgeted to complete prior to its distribution.   

Actual time to perform the assessment and to write the report is 80 - 240 hours, depending upon the physical size of the facility and the documentation that is available.
