Main Description
The duration for producing this work product is dependent upon the size of the Editorial Style Guide to be created, the accessibility of the Editorial Style Guide, and the availability of needed client resources. As a rule of thumb, estimate at least 2-5 days for creation, proofreading and formatting of a typical Editorial Style Guide.
