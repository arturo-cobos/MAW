Main Description

This depends on the complexity of the existing business processes and the scope of the engagement. It also depends on the number of assessments being performed. Other factors include the level of analysis and completeness for the supporting work products. Sometimes it is decided that additional information is needed to complete the assessment. This will require additional time and the value must be considered.

The are three components to consider:

  • Preparation (3 to 5 days) depending on completeness of the prior work and the difficulty of scheduling the participants
  • Each Workshop (1 to 2 days) depending on the size of the groups and the number of groups/workshops
  • Documentation (3 to 5 days) depending on amount of information and availability of the validators
