Main Description

Range of effort:

  • Developing the program management framework will depend on the complexity of the transition program and the size of the team and the level of client support and understanding. This is likely to be a long and complex effort over at least 2 months involving 1-2 consultants
  • Defining and agreeing on the scorecard will take 2-3 days per measure, plus equivalent client time
  • Defining and developing measurement techniques and presentation of information depend on the complexity of design and the package selected
  • Developing targets will take 1-2 days per measure plus equivalent client time
  • Monitoring and reviewing will take 1-2 days per month
