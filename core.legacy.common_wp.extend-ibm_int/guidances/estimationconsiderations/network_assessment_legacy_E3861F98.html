Main Description

A typical Network Assessment will take from 2 to 6 weeks. As with all phases of a networking engagement, the main factors affecting the project estimate are:

  • Geographic reach of network (local, city, geo, country, world)
  • Amount of data (current and accurate) the client is providing to the team 
  • Number of interviews / location of interviews 
  • Topology of network to assess (campus LAN vs. worldwide voice/data/video WAN/LAN environment)
  • Number of network management processes being assessed (help desk, testing, capacity & performance, business continuity/recovery, problem/change, etc.) 

There will typically be a lead network consultant with 1-3 additional network consultants/architects/specialists plus some number of I&TM consultants if there is much process work being assessed. This is also a great time to involve junior or new members of the team as they can be very effective in the data gathering activities and gain a great deal of experience.
