Main Description
Effort to write a Network Node Design work product varies, depending on several factors:
  • Size and complexity of Client Network
  • Proficiency of Consultant/Architect in creating a Network Design (i.e., experience in protocols, vendors, products, etc.)
  • Availability of network sizing information (capacity and performance data)
  • Whether the client has a preferred network vendor or whether an RFP process needs to be performed for product selection
  • Availability and currency of pre-defined Network Strategy and Architecture.
In general, there are three phases of effort:
  1. Initial drafting of complete WP, 5 days to 20 days, depending on the factors above.
  2. Review (and review feedback) - by several reviewers - same as initial effort.
  3. Rework (possibly in several iterations), approximately the same as the initial effort.

Thus, for total effort to draft, review and finalize a small WP (5 pages, no diagram notation) by a proficient writer in a well-understood area, allow 15 days.

Total effort for a larger WP to be written by someone less proficient at WP authoring, 20 to 60 days.

Contingencies which can further extend the effort to achieve a satisfactory result are:
  • Lack of convergence between subject matter experts producing non-terminating debate
  • Change of author part way through
  • Too much research and invention (as contrasted with documenting current good practice)
