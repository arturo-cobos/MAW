Main Description

The amount of time required to create this work product will be dependent on the scope and number of tools being assessed. In most environments this assessment could generally be completed within 1-2 person weeks of effort.

Typically, it will take from 2 - 5 days to gather the data necessary to establish the current tools inventory and map those tools against the management processes and managed elements they support. It will typically then take approximately the same amount of effort to complete the assessment of the current, implemented and required level of tool capability.

Additional time and effort should be allowed if any of the following conditions exist:

  • The client has a very complex environment to manage with a significant number of unusual platforms or processes to support
  • The client makes extensive use of non-standard tools or tools developed in-house
  • There is a highly fragmented approach to tools therefore the possibility of considerable overlap or duplication of tools that must be analyzed
