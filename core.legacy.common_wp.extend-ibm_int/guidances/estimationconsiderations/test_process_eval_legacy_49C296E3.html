Main Description

Depending on the size of the project, the evaluation report can take from a few days to two weeks to produce.  Consider adding to this estimate if a detailed report is also required.  Interview and workshop guidelines may help refine the estimates; allot a minimum of three hours per interview and three hours prep and wrap-up for each hour of workshop time.
