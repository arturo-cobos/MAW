Main Description

The range, scope, and content of a cost and value engagement vary considerably. As such the breadth and depth of financial practices data required also varies making absolute estimation difficult. However as a general rule of thumb the overall time spent on financial practices model development needs to be placed into context with other client environment data collection and those engagement activities in performing subsequent data synthesis activities of assessment and conclusion development, solution approach development and recommendations, collation into developed initiatives and overall roadmap. The approximate relative ratios of overall time are shown as an indication below, however this should be used for initial planning purposes only as these will vary from engagement to engagement:

  • Overall planning and data collection (40%)
  • Financial practices work product data collection (within above overall data collection) (8%)
  • Assessment and conclusions (30%)
  • Solution approach and recommendations (15%)
  • Collation, initiative and roadmap development (15%)
