Main Description
The time to create a Business Event List primarily depends on the scope of the business area involved and the types and numbers of external interactions. Assuming the existence of a set of business context diagrams and classified terms, a Business Event List can be put together in 2 or 3 days. With no other input, it might take 1 or 2 weeks to gather and document these events from business subject matter experts.
