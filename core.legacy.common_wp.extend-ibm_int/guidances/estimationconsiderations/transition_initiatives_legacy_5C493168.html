Main Description

With a core team of knowledgeable participants from the appropriate business and IT communities, it is possible to produce a list of transition initiatives and their associated projects in two weeks elapsed time. This will vary depending upon whether initiatives are being defined at the strategic or more tactical level, as well as based upon the agreed to format and level of detail being used.   

For planning purposes allow two days for workshop preparation, three workshops of half a day each, three days to document, plus an initial review session with the core team.
