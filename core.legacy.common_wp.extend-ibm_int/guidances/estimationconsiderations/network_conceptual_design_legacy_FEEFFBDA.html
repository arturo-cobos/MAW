Main Description
Performing the Network Conceptual Design portion of an overall networking engagement will typically take from 1 to 2 weeks, depending on the breadth and complexity of the clientâ€™s business goals, objectives and requirements. For example, accommodating access to diverse services or a geographically dispersed enterprise will require more time than producing a conceptual design for relatively few client communities in a single location. If more than two weeks are required to prepare a conceptual design the design may be at a more detailed level than is called for. A conceptual design for a data network at a single location or campus should not take more than one week while a conceptual design for a converged enterprise network with multiple sites may take the entire two weeks.
