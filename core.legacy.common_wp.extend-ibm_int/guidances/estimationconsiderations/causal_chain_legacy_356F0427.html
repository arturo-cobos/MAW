Main Description

The effort required for Causal Chain development varies greatly depending on the issues involved, the scope of the engagement. Other estimating considerations are: the complexity of the subject, the availability of subject matter experts, whether the Causal Chain will be developed in person or remotely, and whether more than one consultant will be involved.   

Typically developing a Causal Chain requires between four and twelve hours of consultant effort.
