Main Description

The Quality Plan for Testing must be kept simple and the estimating considerations are:

  • The size and complexity of the project, and the number of development teams.
  • The location of development and testing teams.  The more dispersed the teams are, the greater the communication requirements and the formality of quality plans and documentation.
  • The technology is a major factor in the target level of quality. 
  • The application development maturity of the organization and the team.  The higher the maturity, the lower the cost.
