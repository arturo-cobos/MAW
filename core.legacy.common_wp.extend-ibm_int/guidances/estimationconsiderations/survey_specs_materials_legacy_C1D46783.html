Main Description

The level of effort associated with producing this work product is highly variable, as are the responsible parties. This work product could be completed through the delivery of a pre-compiled questionnaire by IBM consultants to workshop attendees, with the results aggregated and delivered the following day. Or cover a world-wide, multi-national respondent based administered by a third-party market research firm by mail, phone, or internet distribution, followed by several weeks of data analysis and preparation. The area of study, deliver approach and size of respondent audience, as well as the degree of pre-existing survey materials for re-use will drive the level of effort for individual engagements.

The time required to complete the initial planning will vary depending upon the scope of the study area, and the size and variety in the respondent audience. For engagements were the survey study area in already well defined as are the delivery media and resource requirements this planning could be very abbreviated. In cases where all specifications must be developed and agreed to during the scope of the engagement, this could be a significant activity requiring weeks to complete.

The production of the specification and materials vary depending upon the scope of the study area, and the size and variety in the respondent audience, and the delivery approach selected. For engagements were the survey materials are pre-existing, only tailoring and access activities may be required. In cases where all materials must be developed and agreed to during the scope of the engagement, this could be a significant activity requiring weeks to complete.
