Main Description

In conjunction with the running of the planning workshops, an Engagement Plan should be estimated as follows:

  • Prepare for the Planning Workshops - up to 4 hours
  • Run two Planning Workshops, 4 hours each
  • Collate the information gathered in the workshops and prepare the Engagement Plan draft - 2 days
  • Perform internal IBM review - up to 4 hours
  • Perform interim Client review - up to 4 hours
  • Prepare final copy - 2 hours
