Main Description

Competitor Analysis is often performed as an element of a larger strategy. In such cases, the analysis will occur at a high level and take 1-2 person weeks.

A thorough stand-alone competitor analysis can take 8 to 16 person weeks depending on the level of detail required and the method of data collection (customer surveys and focus groups may require more planning and elapsed times). The effort and duration required will depend on:

  • The level of experience the engagement team has within the given industry
  • The number of competitors to be reviewed and amount of information available for each competitor
  • The amount of assistance provided by the client
  • The level of depth desired by the client
