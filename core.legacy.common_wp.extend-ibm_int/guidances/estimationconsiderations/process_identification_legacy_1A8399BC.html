Main Description

The time to develop this work product is approximately 2-4 days, depending on process model availability and the level of detail required. It also depends on the breadth of the engagement, level of executives, and the maturity of the enterprise towards process understanding and management. I do agree for e.g.

  • If the engagement is a business transformation initiative for an entire enterprise or an organization that has historically done very minimal process definition work - identifying and grouping the processes would normally take about 4 days and would normally involve two business consultants
  • If the engagement is a business transformation initiative for the procurement process of an organization, identifying and grouping the sub-processes would take only 2 days and would normally involve two business consultants

The breakdown for the estimation is:

  • Selecting participants, agenda, workshop facility arrangements .25 to 0.5 days
  • Work session to identify/define processes 1.0 to 1.5 days
  • Preparing draft for client review 0.5 to 1.0 days
  • Holding client review session 0.5 to 1.0 days
  • Updating/distributing final work product about 0.5 days
