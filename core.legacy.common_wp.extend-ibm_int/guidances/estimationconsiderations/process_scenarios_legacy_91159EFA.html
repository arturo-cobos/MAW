Main Description
  • If the Client's members of the project team are capable of developing the scenarios: 25%-75% of a man day per scenario, mainly for coaching and reviews.
  • If the Client's members of the project team are not capable of developing the scenarios: 1-3 man days per scenario, mainly for design, writing and reviews.

Preparing and conducting organization requirements workshops:
  • 2-5 project team days

For Scenario Planning:
  • Approximately 4-6 weeks for preparation, work shop execution and work product development. This effort begins after the first draft of the Process Flow Definition has been released.

Completion of Process Scenarios requires different amounts of work depending on the number of different kinds of users and the complexity of the application.
