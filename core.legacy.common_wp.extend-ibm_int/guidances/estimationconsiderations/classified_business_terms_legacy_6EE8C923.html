Main Description
The effort required to produce Classified Business Terms is largely a factor of how much material is used as the source of the analysis. An analyst can realistically extract and organize the terms from two or three pages of text in an hour. The other estimation factor is the validation of the work with the client team, which can be done by exchanging and annotating documents, or in a meeting setting.
 
The effort needed to produce the Classified Business Terms work product is dependent on the amount of existing documentation that describes the business.
  • .5 - 2 hours per page (1 hour normal)
 
Also include a 10% rework effort between the first and second versions of this work product.
