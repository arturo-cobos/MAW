Main Description

Effort to develop standard contract phrases, clauses, terms and conditions is approx. 80-200 hrs depending upon the nature of existing contracts, stakeholders and legal department involvement.

  • The effort to develop contract templates for an organization that has a different, customized contract for each supplier will be closer to 200 hours
  • If the client organization has already standardized contracts to a high degree, then it may only take approx. 80 hours to organize the contracts into templates and document the standard phrases, terms and conditions that are acceptable to the client
