Main Description

The complexity of this work product will depend on the scope of the effort. Complicating factors include (but are not limited to) the amount of content, content stability, access to SMEs, and expected size of the learning solution. Time required to complete, review, and approve Content Gaps will depend on each of these factors.   

Range of effort:   

Plan on at least one day for analyzing existing content, and at least one day to fill in content gaps. This estimate may increase due to the size and complexity of a particular solution, as well as to the content that is available.
