Main Description
The range, scope, and content of accounting models vary considerably making absolute estimation difficult. However as a general rule of thumb the overall time spent on accounting model development needs to be split by the following key activities (as defined in the Advice and Guidance section) in approximate relative ratios of overall time:
  • Strategy (10%)
  • Scope and framework (The majority of which is attributed to cost model development) (60%)
  • Model - Service of offering costs (Customer agreement critical here) (15%)
  • Model -Customer pricing and billing (Customer agreement critical here) (15%)

Note that service definition is not included here. If services / cost pools are not well defined then a separate engagement will be required for this undertaking.
