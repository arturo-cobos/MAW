Main Description
  1. The kick off workshop would normally be scheduled to take a half-day, although a day might be more appropriate in a particularly complex environment.
  2. A team of a minimum of 4 people is suggested. The IBM Consultant, a Client Architect, and two Client Data professionals, one of whom should be a manager.
  3. Expect the report/conclusions to require at least three drafts. Allow a minimum of 2-3 days per draft elapsed time. This implies three further meetings/workshops of the team (say, 2 hours each) should be scheduled
