Main Description

Generally, actual time takes less than a week for 1-2 consultants but elapsed time for external drivers will take as long as the environmental and positioning activities take. Another period of 3-5 days would occur during capability modeling and again, elapsed for as long as that activity takes.

In an engagement of approximately six weeks duration, allow 2 person days per consultant (may require 1 week elapsed time) to define this WPD and the associated matrices.

In a longer engagement, allow 4 person days per consultant (2 weeks elapsed time) - dependant on the scope and content of any previous work products which have been defined:

  • Half day initial workshop
  • One day to revise the driver statements and document assumptions
  • Half day workshop for core team to review driver statements, draft matrix to business goals/CSF's
  • Half day workshop to produce driver impact matrix
  • Half day to finalise documentation and send out to business and IT management for review
  • Presentation and discussion session with business and IT management
