Main Description

Efforts to perform a Human Performance Improvement Strategy are dependent on client needs. The level of complexity, required consulting resources, and level of involvement on the part of the client are all factors in determining the size and scope. HPI strategies that focus on requirements gathering, analysis, and solution recommendation may require a 2-4 week effort for one or two consultants and larger scale engagements may go for far longer periods requiring a team of 2 or more consultants for several months. Adding in additional jobs will act as multiplier on the level effort required.
