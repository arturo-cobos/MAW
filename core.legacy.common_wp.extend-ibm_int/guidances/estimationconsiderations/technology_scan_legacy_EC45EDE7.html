Main Description

Estimate 1 person about 40 hours over project life for about 15 technologies for high level scans.

Assuming that you are examining 20 technologies that will be filtered down to a list of six to eight, conservatively estimate three person-weeks, which could take up to six weeks of elapsed time.

Note that the amount of time can vary greatly based on:

  • The number of technologies to be examined
  • The amount of information available at the start of the module
  • The amount of assistance provided by the client
  • The level of depth desired by the client
  • The level of experience the engagement team and the client have within the given industry and technologies
