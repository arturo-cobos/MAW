Main Description

Project size, determined by number of businesses/divisions and employees affected, will influence the breadth and effort required with communications. Communications effort will also vary based on the number of stakeholders affected by or interested in the initiative, as well as the degree of change that the initiative entails.   

Estimate taking 5-10 days to draft a communications plan plus revision time. The majority of time will be spent executing the plan, monitoring its effectiveness, and adjusting it for better results.
