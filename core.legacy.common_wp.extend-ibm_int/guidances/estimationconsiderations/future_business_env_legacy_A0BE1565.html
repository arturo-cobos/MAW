Main Description
The work to develop a Future Business Environment should take a team of 2-3 consultants approximately 3-4 weeks to complete. This is based on the assumption that there will be active participation by the client in a series of 1-2 day workshops over this period. It also assumes the use of existing intellectual capital (white papers, points of view, etc.) by the consulting team. If this IC and research material is not available, allow an additional 2 weeks to gather this information.
