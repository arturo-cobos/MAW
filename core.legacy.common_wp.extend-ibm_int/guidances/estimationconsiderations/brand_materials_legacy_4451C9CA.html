Main Description
Acquiring existing Brand Material from the client or a related company should be a relatively easy task once the source within the company is identified.
 
The development of new Brand Material is a complex and lengthy process and should be estimated on a case-by-case basis depending on the existing market position of the client and the estimated market value of the future Brand Concepts.
