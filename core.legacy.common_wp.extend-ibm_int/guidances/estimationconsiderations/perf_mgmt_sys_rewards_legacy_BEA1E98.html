Main Description

Time depends a good deal on the level of employee involvement in designing either rewards or performance management, the size of the organization, and the levels of redesign being undertaken. A rough estimate for the evaluation and redesign of performance management systems for an entire organization, assuming significant client-employee involvement, would be 1.5 to 2 consultants working full-time for about 8 weeks, and meeting with and incorporating the ideas and thoughts of employees. Again, much of this time will depend on the availability of key client resources and data.   

Where only job-level design is being done this would require less client-employee involvement and involve 1-2 consultants working full-time for about 4 weeks. Part of this time will include work with client contacts and will depend on the availability of key client resources and data.
