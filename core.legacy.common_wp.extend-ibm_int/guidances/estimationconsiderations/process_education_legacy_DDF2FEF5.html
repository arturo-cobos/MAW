Main Description

Overview Training:

7-10 days should be allocated to prepare a high level overview of the course modules and the application of business process enablers within each business process area. This includes scheduling and execution of the overview to the project executives and team members.

Training before future design:

10-20 man/days to prepare and carry out training of each Business Process Team. The amount of effort is dependent of the amount and complexity of functionality in the package needed to support the particular Business Process.

Training before Prototyping:

15-30 man/days to prepare and carry out a training class of people who shall participate in the configuration and maintain the solution after the project. 5-15 people can attend one class.

Tools training:

In most cases, tool training will be achieved by attending publicly available standard courses. It will seldom be possible to benefit from customized tool training, but remember to include the resource requirements in your project schedule and any dependencies for completing this training.
