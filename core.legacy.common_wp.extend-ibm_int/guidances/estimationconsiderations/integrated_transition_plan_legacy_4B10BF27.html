Main Description
The production of an Integrated Transition Plan for the client will depend on :
  • Number of separate projects and initiatives,
  • Level of information available on each project,
  • Existence of an agreed and committed program management framework and transition management strategy,
  • Client acceptance of an integrated approach to transition planning ,
  • Level of client program management resources to support this effort
An initial estimate of the work required, for a medium sized client provided that all of these elements were in place, would be between 3 and 6 weeks for 2 consultants to produce an initial plan which would become the basis of debate and discussion before agreement by the client. If these factors are not in place , which is more usually the situation, the plan will usually be developed over a longer period of several months before the client management is able to understand and accept it.
