Main Description

Specifically, to populate the Network Regulatory Profile usually requires a fair amount of research. Completion time is very dependent on how much detail is required. For a high level look at a technically advanced country, finding and absorbing the material might take eight hours for someone already familiar with the technologies. For multiple carriers, to gather all the information needed regarding the various service options could take a day per carrier   

In depth research can easily extend to three or more days per country for preparing detailed design and service provider recommendations, especially if the useful research resources have not been identified through other recently completed engagements of a similar type. This is especially true when dealing with non-English speaking countries/carriers and even more so when information is required for non-North American, non-European sites around the world.
