Process/Activity

Circuit Pack Demand & Supply Input


Compare and match of global demand with key supply constraints. Use a synchronized global demand plan to build to order with full level visibility to order or forecast.

  • Provide global and local on hand key items inventory position.
  • Provide local capacity constraints to the global supply chain.
  • Provide multiple views of key items demand.
  • Backward propagation of net key items demand requirements through the global supply chain.
  • Developing global, regional and local Master Production Schedules.
Source Demand to Supplier

Automated BCD Plans


Reliably and responsively commit to changes in demand. Respond to unplanned orders and change requests with less inventory: “what-ifs” for orders; prioritize “pull-ups” and “push-outs".

  • Perform what if scenarios for various alternative supply and demand plans.
  • Plan strategies based upon established business rules as input that allow for prioritizing different demands (orders and forecasts).

Collaborative Consensus Planning


Manage large number and varying types of locations: Distributors, contract manufacturers, value added resellers, etc.

  • Inclusion of material and capacity information from collaboration partners as part of the global supply chain.
  • Provide the best constraint based production and shipping schedule to each collaborating partner.
