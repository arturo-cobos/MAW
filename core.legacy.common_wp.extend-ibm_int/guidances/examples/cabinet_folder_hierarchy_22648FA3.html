Senate Documents Cabinet Hierarchy

Senate Documents

This cabinet holds all documents being worked on in the Senate. 

Bills
Contains all Senate Bills, Appropriations Bills, and Joint Resolutions.

SBnnnn or SJRx
Bill folder for a Senate Bill, Appropriations Bill, or Joint Resolution. There is one of these for every such document.

Meeting Notices
Contains all Senate generated meeting notices.

Meeting Notices Past
Archive of old meeting notices.

Messages
All Senate generated message documents not pertaining to legislation. 

Resolutions
All Senate generated resolutions.

SRnnnn or SCRnnnn
Bill folder for a Senate Resolution or Senate Concurrent Resolution. There is one of these for every such document.

House Documents Cabinet Hierarchy

House Documents

This cabinet holds all documents being worked on in the House.

Bills
Contains all House Bills, Appropriations Bills, and Joint Resolutions.

HBnnnn or HJRx
Bill folder for a House Bill, Appropriations Bill, or Joint Resolution. There is one of these for every such document.

Meeting Notices
Contains all House generated meeting notices.

Meeting Notices Past
Archive of old meeting notices.

Messages
All House generated message documents not pertaining to legislation.

Resolutions
All House generated resolutions.


HRnnnn or HCRnnnn
Bill folder for a House Resolution or House Concurrent Resolution. There is one of these for every such document.
