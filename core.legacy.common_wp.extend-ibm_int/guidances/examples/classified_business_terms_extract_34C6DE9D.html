Main Description

EDDIE BEAN operates some 30 HOME stores (consolidated stores with all 5 product lines in a single store) and some 400 stores that operate with one to many (but not all) product lines available.

Customers have the option of making their purchases through mail order catalogs or in any of the retail stores. Products are divided into five major product categories, Clothing and apparel, Sporting goods, outdoor equipment, Household furnishings and Furniture. EDDIE BEAN stores may not carry all product line categories.

EDDIE BEAN's current Gift Registry is viewed as a success by the customers that have utilized it. The registry process however is cumbersome, inaccurate and time consuming, and made available to customers only by request. Competition however has been quite successful in implementing and marketing their gift registry services.

The Catalog and Mail order program is a vital and core profit center. Customers order from the catalog via-telephone, and orders are shipped directly from one of our warehouses. Checks and Credit Cards are accepted for mail order purchases.
