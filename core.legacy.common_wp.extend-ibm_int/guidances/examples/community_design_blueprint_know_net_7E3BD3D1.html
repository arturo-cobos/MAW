Main Description
The design blueprints for these Knowledge Networks are well documented.  It is important to note, however, that the Knowledge Networks were developed around the use of the IBM Assetweb tool and represent a series of organizational guidelines geared towards the IBM Corporation.  Different companies using different technological approaches and management systems may require different design specifications.
