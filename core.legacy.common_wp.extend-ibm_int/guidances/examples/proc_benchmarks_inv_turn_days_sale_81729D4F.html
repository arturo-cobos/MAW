Example: Process Benchmarks for Inventory Turns, Days Sales and Payables
The following example shows how a company wanted to benchmark their “Inventory Turns”, “Days Sales Outstanding” and “Days Payables Outstanding” against the average of a wide range of companies.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Process Benchmarks
Main Description

First a list of comparable companies was created and a survey was done to collect the current state information for the three metrics from the identified companies. From this information the average value for each of the three metrics was calculated and compared to the client’s own measured performance.

Finally, based upon the comparison between its own current state and the average of the surveyed companies, the company defined future targets for its metrics on “Inventory Turns”, “Days Sales Outstanding” and “Days Payables Outstanding”.
