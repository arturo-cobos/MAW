Opportunity Name Multi-use Centers
Opportunity Owner Jane Smith
ID Code A-14

Opportunity Description
Transform the company’s 2000 financial centers into “multi-use, lifestyle centers”. Leverage the bricks and mortar employees, expertise, customer base and partner relationships in a financial center you can:
· Complete financial transactions
· Meet with other people
· Return e-commerce merchandise
· Drink Super coffee
· Meet with your personal concierge
· Get advice on starting your own business, taking it public, etc.
· Renew driver’s license, get technology advice’ drop off dry cleaning,
· Get elder care advice,
· Upgrade / maintenance of all-in-one devices

Capabilities
· Create and manage multiple partnership arrangements
· Provide quality, personalized financial advisory services
· Quickly upgrade technology to keep up with progress and meet customer needs
Implications / Issues / Dependencies / Other Comments
Re-purpose branch locations, re-train branch staff, alter branch staff to better serve customers

Assumptions
· Pervasive use of wireless and internet technology in day to day life
· Telecommuting and contract work becomes the norm
