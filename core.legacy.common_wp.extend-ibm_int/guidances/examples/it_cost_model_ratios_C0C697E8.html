Example: IT Cost Model for Ratios
Relationships
Related Elements
  • IT Cost Model
Main Description
  • Total Cost per Device and per GB
  • Total technology and personnel costs divided by total devices and divided by total installed gigabytes.
  • Hardware Cost per Device & per GB
  • CPU, DASD, Tape and Output hardware cost (lease/depreciation, maintenance, tax, consoles) divided by total devices and divided by total installed gigabytes.
  • Software Cost per Device & per GB
  • Operating system and sub-system software costs (lease/depreciation, maintenance, one time fees, support software) divided by total devices and divided by total installed gigabytes.
  • Business Resumption Cost per Device and per GB
  • Business Resumption costs (contingency site, consulting, off-site storage, testing expenses) divided by total devices and divided by total installed gigabytes.
  • Occupancy Cost per Device & per GB
  • Building occupancy and maintenance costs (raised floor, office space, utilities, environmentals, security system, maintenance) divided by total devices and divided by total installed gigabytes.
  • Operations Support Cost per Device & per GB
  • Operations Support personnel cost (salary, bonus, overtime, benefits, travel and training) divided by total devices and divided by total installed gigabytes.
  • Technical Support Cost per Device & per GB
  • Technical Services Support personnel cost (salary, bonus, overtime, benefits, travel and training) divided by total devices and divided by total installed gigabytes.
  • Technical Cost per Device & per GB
  • Total hardware, software, business resumption and occupancy cost divided by total devices and divided by total installed gigabytes.
  • Support Cost per Device & per GB
  • Total Operations and Technical Services Support cost divided by total devices and divided by total installed gigabytes.
  • Operations Support Cost per FTE
  • Operations Support personnel cost (salary, bonus, overtime, benefits, travel and training) divided by total operations full-time equivalents.
  • Technical Services Support Cost per FTE
  • Technical Services Support personnel cost (salary, bonus, overtime, benefits, travel and training) divided by total technical services full-time equivalents.
  • Total Cost per FTE
  • Total personnel costs (salary, bonus, overtime, benefits, travel and training) divided by total full-time equivalents.
  • Total Devices per FTE
  • Total devices divided by total full-time equivalents.
  • Total Gigabytes per FTE
  • Total installed gigabytes divided by total full-time equivalents.
  • Devices per Operator
  • Total number of devices divided by Operations Support and Tape Support FTEs
  • Devices per Systems Administrator
  • Total number of devices divided by Systems Support, Performance, Capacity Planning and Storage Management FTEs
  • Devices per Capacity Planner
  • Total number of devices divided by total number of Capacity Planning FTEs
  • Gigabytes per Storage Management FTE
  • Total on-line gigabytes divided by total number of Storage Management FTEs
  • Gigabytes per DBA FTE
  • Customer Allocated gigabytes divided by total number of Database Administration FTEs
  • Users per support staff
  • Total number of users divided by number of support staff
  • Users per Server
  • Total number of users divided by number of servers
  • Calls per user
  • Total number of calls to the Help Desk divided by the total number of users
  • Calls per Help Desk staff
  • Total number of calls to the Help Desk divided by the total number of Help Desk staff
  • Users per Help Desk staff
  • Total number of users divided by the total number of Help Desk staff
  • % of Revenue spend on IT
  • Total Company revenue divided by amount spent on IT
  • Users per Printer
  • Total number of users divided by total number of printers
  • Costs per client
  • Total costs (overall or by group) divided by number of hardware clients
