Purpose of Request for Proposal (RFP)

The client is soliciting solutions for the procurement of an enterprise-wide administrative system to manage the finance and accounting, asset management, human resources and payroll operations.  The primary goal of the project is to select a software solution that will provide the following features:

  • Comprehensive and fully integrated administrative management
  • Customized management reporting capabilities
  • Reliable and accurate financial and accounting functions:
    • General Ledger
    • Accounts Payable
    • Accounts Receivable
  • Reliable and accurate Asset Management functionality:
    • Fixed Assets
    • Purchasing
    • Inventory Management
  • Comprehensive and integrated Human Resources and Payroll functions:
    • Human Resources Management
    • Payroll
    • Time and Attendance
  • Open architecture to facilitate the integration with outside applications
