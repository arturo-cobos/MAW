Main Description

Charting the client’s position against key competitors or substitutors is arguably the most effective way to capture the attention of busy executives and senior managers.  When executives see a chart positioning them against their competitors, they invariably want to understand the details behind the chart – e.g. why are they lagging and how they can move ahead. Charting methods include: opportunity charts, wheat charts, Harvey ball charts and radar/spider charts.
