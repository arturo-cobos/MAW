Main Description

The relevant criteria for validation and verification of this work product focus on the completeness and consistency.  Reviewers need to determine whether and how well this work product spans all impacted systems, interfaces and areas of concern.  Primary areas include system, data, interfaces, and dependencies.

If possible validation of this work product should be undertaken by review with other project members, with other application technical specialists, and in more complex situations, with the key architectural members.  The final verification of the Emergency Fix work product occurs during the final review, when it is considered alongside other documentation, and its correctness assured by the completion of the Release Assurance Verification form.
