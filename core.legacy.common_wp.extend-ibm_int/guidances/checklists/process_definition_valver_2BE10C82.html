Checklist: Process Definition Validation and Verification
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Process Definition
Check Items
All relevant external interactions and flows have been articulated
All relevant paths have been modeled
Many times, the consequence of a decision may have more than 2 outcomes.
Parallel activities are modeled as such and not as sequential
If this is a redesigned process, verify that all roles are still valid
In some cases, one role may need to be split into several due to separation of duty or labor relation restrictions.
For package-enabled processes, gaps are documented
Gaps between future process flow definition and package are documented and consequences understood by the client's process owners and by the sponsor.
The client and IBM agree on the scope and boundaries of the process
The client's process team understands the design of future processes
This includes the organizational impact and how the future process design fulfills required capabilities and performance.
Processes begin with triggering events, and work their way through to significant business results
All tasks or activities are assigned to roles that are meaningful to people in the business
Be sure you have captured all relevant roles, which may sometimes be outside of the client’s company.
Participants can read and verify the models/maps
  • Knowledgeable business people, technology implementers, process participants can read the models/maps and verify that they depict the actual or desired flow of business activity.
The flow is logically correct
Synchronization points (wait states) must be resolved as a result of a revert or branch in the process.
Future process defintions cover current proccess and future design points
  • Future process definitions cover aspects of current processes that are performing well, as well as achieving the future design points, compare the “future” process definition with:
    • The “current” process definition
    • The target business capabilities
    • The process design principles and objectives  (where do you define these?)  Good question?
    • The information or data model
    • The business event list
