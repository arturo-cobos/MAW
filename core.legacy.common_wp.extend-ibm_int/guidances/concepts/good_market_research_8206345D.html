Concept: Characteristics of Good Market Research
Kotler's seven characteristics of good market research.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Market Research
Main Description
  1. Scientific methodology – The principals of scientific methodology are careful observation, formulation of hypotheses, prediction and testing and these are applied to market research.
  2. Research creativity – At it’s best, market research is a means to develop new ways to understand customers.
  3. Multiple methods – Good market researchers do not over-rely on any one method of data collection but select the appropriate method based on the research objectives. 
  4. Interdependence of models and data – Good market researchers recognize that data are interpreted from models, which guide the data collection.
  5. Value and cost of information – The decision to conduct research should be weighed against the value of the research. 
  6. Healthy skepticism – Effective researchers will show a healthy skepticism to assertions made by managers.
  7. Ethical marketing – While research can benefit the client and its customers, poor research techniques can annoy or harm customers.
