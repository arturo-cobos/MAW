Main Description

The FOAD Toolkit and white paper have all of the following tools and methodologies:

  • Impact Assessment Framework
  • Strategic Alignment Matrix
  • Structure, Enablers, & Behaviors Summary
  • Financial Resource Allocation to Business Goals
  • Measurements Alignment to Business Goals & Strategies
  • Organization Design Consistency Checklist
  • Efficiency Measures Checklist
  • Human Resources/Assets to Accomplish Business Goals
  • Human Resources/Assets to Provide Accessible Solutions
  • Learning Process Checklist (including the requirements to provide training to users who have disabilities)
  • Human Performance Measurement Checklist
  • IBM Accessibility Checklists
