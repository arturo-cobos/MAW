Tool Mentor: GBS Standardized Process Modeling and Analysis with WBM
Describes best practices and guidance for process modeling, the application of operational data into a process flow to analyze a process, with Websphere Business Modeler (WBM). Provides standards for performing process analysis activities, explains the different types of analysis, and provides guidance for using analyses effectively.
Tool: Websphere Business Modeler
Main Description

1 Brief Description

Process modelling and analysis activities support a broad range of methodologies and engagement approaches. These are technical and precise activities which must be utilised carefully to obtain accurate results. The steps described in this technique paper were developed as a result of experience from a large number of modeling and analysis engagements. This paper provides standards for completing process analysis activities within a GBS engagement using WBM. It gives detailed explanations of the different types of analysis and guidance on where these can be used effectively within engagements. It will help to ensure that you are more productive and your team can easily produce standardised and accurate analysis results. Following the standard approach outlined as recommended will yield accurate and time efficient results.

2 Usage Context

This technique paper is for IBM GBS practitioners engaged in process modeling and analysis activities using WebSphere Business Modeler (WBM) Version 6.0, irrespective of their industry sector or service line, and independent of any engagement methodology. It is assumed that readers have experience in the application of process mapping with WBM in accordance with the GBS Standards documented in GBS Standardized Process Mapping with WBM technique paper.
Other potential users of this guide include project managers who need to appreciate the rigours of process modelling and effort and outcomes of process analysis.

4 Key Considerations

It is assumed that you have a solid understanding of the principles and GBS standards for mapping processes in WBM. All processes which are being modelled and analysed in accordance with this technique paper must comply with the GBS standards for process mapping as specified in the “GBS Standardised Process Mapping with WBM” technique paper.

It is also assumed that you have a solid understanding in the use of WebSphere Business Modeler version 6.0 including familiarity with process constructs and process simulation.
