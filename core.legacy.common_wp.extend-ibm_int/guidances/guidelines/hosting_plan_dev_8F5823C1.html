Main Description
Many approaches can be taken to develop a Hosting Plan.  The requirements analysis is key to developing the correct Hosting Plan.  By focusing on the most critical elements associated with hosting the customerâ€™s application, a Hosting Plan can be developed.  Some of the key elements to consider are: application performance (hits per minute, bandwidth served, and more), data synchronization, fault tolerance, content and application staging, and security to ensure hardened systems, resilience against Denial of Service and penetration and vulnerability checking within a production configuration..  Hosting Plans for new customers represent a best estimate of predicted customer hit rate and data transfer volume.  These plans have to be designed with adequate provisions for significantly higher or lower throughput.  Conducting a series of performance, stress and penetration tests best specifies the customer-hosting environment. Also ensure the staging environments are securely zoned from production systems.
