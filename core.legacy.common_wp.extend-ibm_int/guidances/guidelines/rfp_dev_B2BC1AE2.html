Guideline: Developing Request for Proposal

Main Description

The IBM Selection team develops the Request for Proposal.  The IBM Selection Team members use information gathered on the client, include appropriate best practices and complete the RFP for delivery to the vendors.

Prepare the Request for Proposal according to the following steps.  These steps will be the same for a Request for Solution, exceptions are noted.

  • Utilize the various models and work product components produced during the pre-selection phases to identify the business areas to be evaluated in the RFP as well as specific problems or issues the client is seeking to eradicate with the software solution.
  • Develop the purpose description for the RFP, which will establish to the vendors the desired result of the software selection.  Emphasize the focus of the software selection in the purpose to ensure a clear understanding of the clientâ€™s goals.
  • Determine the schedule for the selection process to highlight key dates to the vendor.
  • Establish the response content and format desired from the vendors.
  • Identify the key points of contact for the vendor with regards to the RFP.
  • Gather and include the terms and conditions required by the client as follows: (not required for RFS)
    • Contract type
    • Rules for modification
    • Terms
    • Settlement of disputes
    • Confidentiality
    • Termination criteria
    • Penalty and Bonus clauses
    • Payment Schedule
  • Include a statement of confidentiality for the vendor to sign and return with the RFP  response.
  • Summarize the client, include the following:
    • Project Background
    • Current Technical Environment
    • Desired Future Business Processes
    • Scope for the software solution
  • Develop the vendor questionnaire specific to the client needs.  Include the following:
    • Company Background
    • Financial Performance
    • Warranty Policies
    • References
    • Technology Overview
    • Initial Cost Estimates
  • Solicit initial cost estimates for software, maintenance, training, support, hardware and implementation services for the proposed solution offered by the vendor.
  • Establish a change policy for the RFP and the time line to be guaranteed by the vendor for all solution costs presented by the vendor for software, maintenance, training and support.
