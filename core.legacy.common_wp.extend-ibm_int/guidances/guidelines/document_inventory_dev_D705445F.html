Main Description

Early on in an engagement, or even prior to project proposal, this work product would just consist of a catalog of documents and some basic information such as volume and average size.

During the initial phases of the project, the entire collection of the Document Inventory examples should be collected up front from the client.  Within a given document type, there may be significant differences in the document during its life-cycle which could drive requirements.  Examples of the different permutations of a single document type should also be documented and collected.

The Document Inventory will be used for developing the project scope of the new system.
