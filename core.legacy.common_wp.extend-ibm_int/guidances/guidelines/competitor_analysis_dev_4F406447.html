Main Description

The approach to competitor analysis:

  1. Identify the clients key competitors or substitutors.
  2. Select a small number of competitors to focus on.
  3. Identify the focal points for the competitor analysis.
  4. Prepare the research strategy, data collection guide and interview guides.
  5. Assess the competitor/substitutor market positioning (market segments, market share, profitability).
  6. Assess the competitor’s internal capabilities as well as their competitive strengths and weaknesses.  Use graphical representations and competitor benchmarking wherever possible.
  7. Identify how competitors are exploiting strategic relationships or alliances to achieve competitive advantage. Use a competitor snapshot to summarize alliance data for each competitor.
  8. Use the competitive intelligence gathered to evaluate the client’s competitive options in formulating strategy.
  9. Identify specific initiatives to address competitive threats and opportunities.
