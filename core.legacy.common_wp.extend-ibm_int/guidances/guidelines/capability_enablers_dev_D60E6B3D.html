Main Description

Start with the list of future capabilities needed to provide value to the client.  Then identify the things required to enable each future capability e.g. process, organizations / people, measurement / management systems, policies / practices, information or information technology, skills / resources, etc.  

Other inputs to developing the list of future enablers may include:

  • enablers that have been assessed as being weak in the current environment
  • best practices/leading practices that may be adopted by the client

Next, a determination has to be made of which processes / sub-processes need to be changed / are impacted by the future enablers.

Finally, the measurable targets for each sub-process affected also need to be identified.

The recommended approach is a workshop or series of workshops.  In addition to the consulting team, the participants should include:

  • functional managers and key staff in the major areas that will be impacted
  • technology/package/application specialists

The structure and content of the workshop(s) will depend on the type of engagement.
