Guideline: Developing Strategic Market Position
Migrated development approach from exisiting IBM Global Services Method work product.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Strategic Market Position
Main Description
  1. Understand the client's segmentation of the market place/ space on the dimensions of customers, products/services and access channels.
  2. Understand the segment characteristics and make trade-offs to select a segment on which to focus.
  3. Understand the customer's winning needs in the selected segments.
  4. Determine how the firm will differentiate itself from its competitors in the focus segments.
