Main Description
To develop the assessment, the existing facility documentation is reviewed against best practices and how the facility is meeting the client's needs.  Through interviews and working sessions, information is gathered from the client's facilities personnel on areas where the business needs are not being met in order to understand the problems.  Discussions are held with the architects on the IBM team to determine potential solution options and what type of facility may be needed to support the options.  These options are reviewed against the facility's current or planned infrastructure and performance to determine its potential to accommodate a future environment.  In addition, best practices are applied to determine areas where the facility may not be performing or where opportunities exist for improvement.
