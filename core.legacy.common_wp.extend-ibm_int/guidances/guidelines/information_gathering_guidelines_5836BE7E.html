Main Description

The Information Gathering Guidelines technique provides generic guidance for the gathering of information during the early stages of any client engagement. Information gathering is to be looked at, resourced and conducted as a single activity, with the various 'threads' of information about the current client business and IT environment being routed through to specific work products, as appropriate.
