Main Description

The following development approach is proven:

1. Work with the information assets defined in a business information model as a starting point (if available).
2. Interviews with IS/IT and key user management to:

- identify critical information assets
- understand what the impact on the business would be if these critical assets are compromised
- understand incumbent risks

3. Produce a comprehensive review of the organizationâ€™s information assets, their relevance to their key business issues, security threats and requirements, ownership and use.
4. Document in an inventory of information assets - including a profile for each asset (1 per page) containing its description, location, owner, users and usage, availability impacts, confidentiality impacts, integrity and privacy impacts.
