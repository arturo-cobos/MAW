Main Description
This Principles Definition technique paper provides detailed guidance on how to define a set of business and IT principles for the selection, deployment and implementation of business and IT assets - it should be used in conjunction with the Principles, Policies and Guidelines work product. The technique can be used at many different levels and by many different roles in a client engagement. For example:
  • By Business Consultants to define Business Principles
  • By Technical Consultants to define overall IT Principles
  • By A Content Strategist to define Content specific Principles
  • By a Security Consultant to define Security and Privacy Principles
 

In practice, principles are defined and agreed by small teams of people who may perform combinations of roles.
