Main Description
The Security and Privacy Policy work product is produced based on interviews with business and IT and security managers using the IT Security Consulting methodology and associated Intellectual Capital. An example of the tasks used to develop a privacy work product include:
  • Identify policies and standards that will be used to define privacy practices. Gather information from interviews with key business managers to assess the organization’s privacy strategy, related requirements and current privacy concerns.
  • Map privacy policy and standards best practices requirements as defined by relevant industry and/or government bodies (e.g. EU Privacy Directive, the Better Business Bureau, etc.) to the organization’s privacy policies and standards and provide recommendations for improvements to comply with best practices.
  • Based on information gathered from interviews, recommend a corporate privacy policy and a custom web site privacy statement that will be clear, easy to read and that will meet the organization’s privacy strategies and information handling practices.

The Security Policy work product development tasks include:

  • A review of the organization’s business strategy and related security requirements.
  • A review of the organization’s IT strategy, current security concerns, and future security requirements.
  • A review and analysis of the organization’s current security policy and standards against the needs of the organization’s business and IT strategies.
  • A customized security policy document which will prescribe management’s direction to guide the organization in meeting the corporation’s security objectives according to its business needs.
