Main Description

One option to perform an engagement that includes cost baselining is using the TCO approach.

Objective of a TCO - Total Cost of Ownership - engagement is to analyze all IT costs and to identify areas for optimization.

Definition of TCO

TCO for IT components is defined by the

  • Lifetime costs of an IT component including the initial hardware and software purchase price, as well as installation, management, service, support, administration, upgrades, training, downtime and disposal costs, i.e. all costs related to the owning, management and support and usage of an IT component.

(Intel, 1998)

In this way the TCO is driven by processes, people and technology / tools and comprises all costs in the lifecycle of an IT component (see picture).
