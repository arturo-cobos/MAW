Main Description

Today's business environment demands improved service and quality. Business managers and professionals continually assess how they deal with their customers, and question how they can improve such relationships. At the same time, businesses need a way to help document, evaluate, and often change business processes to meet the demands of customers for quality and service. Equally important, and requiring continuous focus, is employee morale and internal efficiency.

While Computer Aided Software Engineering (CASE) tools and methologies are available for systems design, they are predominantly designed for Information Systems (IS) professionals who need tools and techniques to consistently develop data and process models used in application development. These models and techniques represent a more abstract view of a business and are not easily understood or used by business professionals and managers.

This document provides you with a methodology, which is especially suitable for business process re-engineering (BPR) and business process management (BPM). LOVEM is a people-oriented methodology and is supported by a number of modeling tools( VISIO, ProModeler, etc.).  LOVEM uses an integrated set of graphics to document, evaluate, and redesign the business process flows. The family of line of visibility charts (LOVCs) is at the core of LOVEM. An LOVC highlights every time an organization, department, or individual has contact with its customer. LOVCs help you to:

  • Identify the service encounters with your customers or clients 
  • Understand how your customers and clients see your company's or department's processes 
  • Focus on the redesign of those areas in need of:
     — Improvement in customer service
     — Improvement in employee morale
     — Cycle time reduction
     — Better use of resources
     — Cost reduction.
