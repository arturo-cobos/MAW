Guideline: Developing Principles - Policies and Guidelines
Migrated development approach from existing IBM Global Services Method work product
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Principles - Policies and Guidelines
Main Description

Policies:

  • Review existing client business and IT strategy documentation.
  • Review existing practices and policies against future requirements and directions.
  • Customize IBM IC/sample policy documents (these exist within specific disciplines e.g. Security):
    • Finalize and agree policy statements, objectives and support for business drivers and needs
    • Agree general and specific roles and responsibilities
    • Agree education and planning requirements

Principles:

  • Review business and IT strategy documents and key architecture drivers (if they have been defined).
  • Develop 'strawman' set of principles (using IBM IC).
  • Prepare for and conduct principles workshop(s)
    • Refine principle statements
    • Develop motivation and implication statements
    • Develop linkages from principles back to key architecture drivers, business drivers and strategic enablers
  • Develop communications plan.
