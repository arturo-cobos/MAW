Main Description

The development of the External Project Kickoff Presentation is the joint responsibility between the IBM and Client Project Managers with the assistance of the IBM Engagement Team, as well as senior Client team members. Input from the client management should be used to reflect strategic objective and reuse of method work products. The high level tasks to complete this work product include:

  • Prepare Kickoff Materials
  • Hold Kickoff Meeting

Information to develop the presentation will be gathered from the following sources:

  • The Methodology Adoption Workshop (MAW). The output of this activity will be a list of the required work products necessary to complete the engagement. Once this activity has been completed, the scope of the project can be defined for the project team.
  • The Statement of Work (SOW). This document should be researched to collect information regarding the overall scope and objectives of the project, the key issues and risks that have been identified as the project was being defined and a high level project approach. This information should be refined based on current information and expanded during the presentation.

  • If the Scope and Approach document is under construction, the information gathered to date could also be incorporated into the kick-off if appropriate.
