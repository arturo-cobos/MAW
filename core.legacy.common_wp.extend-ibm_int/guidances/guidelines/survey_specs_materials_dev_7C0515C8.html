Guideline: Developing Survey Specifications and Materials
Migrated development approach from existing IBM Global Services Method work product.

Main Description
  • This work product assumes hypothesis have already been developed to support the survey area of study, if this work has not been completed, it now needs to be done.
  • To begin development of this work product, the targeted area of study and targeted respondents of each hypothesis required must be known.
  • For each hypothesis, develop a set of survey questions for obtaining respondents views.
  • Develop a set of survey analytics to assess the responses and generate observations.
  • Determine the most appropriate delivery method for obtaining survey responses.
  • Based upon the scope of study, analysis requirements, and delivery method, determine the timeframes necessary for survey design, construction, dissemination, collection, and analysis and identify the supporting requirements.
  • Plan the responsibilities and staff, and the acceptance criteria and and contingency procedures.
  • Document results.
  • Obtain client approval.
