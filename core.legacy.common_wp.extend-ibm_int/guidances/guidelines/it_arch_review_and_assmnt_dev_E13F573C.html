Main Description

The development approach outlined here is specifically geared towards a management level review and assessment. This approach can be broken down as follows:

  • Develop assessment data package â€“ during this step the content and meaning of the different assessment criteria will be defined and agreed with client staff
  • Gather additional data
  • Assess current enterprise-wide IT Architecture
    • Review and agree the assessment criteria (both "Basic criteria" and "Specific criteria") with the client.
    • Assess the architecture against the 'Basic criteria'
    • Assess the architecture against the 'Specific criteria'
  • Define Desired State
  • Determine gaps and business imperatives
    • Determine architecture gaps - understanding and documenting deficiencies and gaps
    • Understand business imperatives - understanding the business requirements, drivers and prioritisation
  • Develop Recommendations
  • Gain Client Agreement
  • Present Results
  • Finalize the client report - Make any final presentations to the sponsor and the client participants.
