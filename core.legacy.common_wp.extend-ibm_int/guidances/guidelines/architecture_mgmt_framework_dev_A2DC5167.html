Main Description

The overall recommended approach is outlined briefly here:

  • Document roles and responsibilities - relating to the maintenance and management of the architecture:
    • Understand Current IT Management and Business User Roles and Responsibilities
    • Define new roles and responsibilities - Develop new roles and responsibilities matrix
    • based on your understanding of the current organisation and roles, develop a new matrix which includes the roles and responsibilities necessary to maintain and implement the architecture.
  • Define Management Processes
    • Develop a ‘strawman’ set of processes
    • Validate and refine processes
    • Document the new processes
    • Agree new processes, roles, responsibilities and transition initiatives
    • Agree new processes, roles and responsibilities
    • Identify and involve Architecture Implementation Manager
  • Define transition and implementation initiatives and recommendations
