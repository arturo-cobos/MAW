Main Description

The Physical Security Assessment work products are developed using various steps depending upon the scope of the engagement.  These include:

  • Review of the client’s IT site security processes and related documentation in the following areas: physical security, emergency planning, incident management, contract management, and information protection.
  • Analysis of the information from interviews with key managers and process owners against the corporation’s standards, IBM’s own internal standards and a standards-based model of “best practices” (BS7799). Social engineering infiltration testing, in addition to interview based data gathering, can also be included.
  • Report detailing observations and recommendations made during the review, and a management presentation outlining identified strengths and weaknesses relating to site security processes and procedural compliance.
