Task: Adapt Service Litmus Test and Exposure Scope
Tailor service litmus tests and service exposure scopes to support client/project needs and environment.
Disciplines: Technical Architecture
Purpose

Service litmus tests are gating criteria for filtering services to build and expose. SOMA has defined default service litmus tests. But there could be need to adapt the service litmus tests to support client/project needs.

Designation of service exposure is not adequate to specify who can use the service. In addition we need to specify at what level of scope the service is being designed to be exposed. Service exposure can occur at multiple scopes: Project, Domain, Line of Business, Enterprise, Eco-system. Designation of service exposure is not adequate to specify which parties can use the service. In addition we need to specify at what level of scope the service is being designed to be exposed. There could be need to adapt the service exposure scope to support client/project environment.

      Main Description
      Adapt the service litmus tests and service exposure scopes to support client/project needs and environment. The universal service litmus tests defined in SOMA may not be sufficient to meet a client’s needs, so additional project/client specific SLTs are defined and added. The questions asked to evaluate each service litmus test may also need to be adapted to meet the client’s environment. The client may not be accustomed to service exposure scopes pre-defined in SOMA, hence exposure scope may need to be adapted for the client’s environment.
      Steps
      Adapt the questions and scoring table for universal SLTs
      Update the SLT questions for the universal SLTs to meet client environment and terminology. Update the scoring table and guidance around scoring level to meet client’s environment.
      Add additional project/client specific SLT
      Add project/client specific SLT, define the corresponding questions, define the scoring table for newly added SLTs, and add guidance around scoring level for those questions in the scoring table.
      Adapt the service exposure scope to client’s environment
      Update the service exposure scope pre-defined in SOMA and associated definition to fit into client’s environment.
