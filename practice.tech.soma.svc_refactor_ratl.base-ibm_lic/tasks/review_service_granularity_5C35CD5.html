Task: Review Service Granularity
Review the service model to ensure that the services are at the right level of granularity.
Disciplines: Technical Architecture
Purpose
To ensure that the service is at the right level of granularity
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • SOA Architect
Additional Performers:
  • Business Analyst
InputsMandatory:
  • Service Model
    		Optional:
    • Goal Service Model
      Outputs
      • Service Model
        Main Description

        Review the service granularity of services in the service model to ensure that they are at the right level of granularity. The right or best granularity for a service can only be specified in the context of its function and foreseeable usage in processes and use cases (usage scenarios / service consumer view).

        Steps
        Use Goal-Service Model to define the correct granularity
        Use the service usage scenarios
        To ensure service consumers will generally find the service to be at the right level of granularity
        Use non-functional requirements to help drive the granularity
        Finer grained services will tend to create more network chatter. Courser grained services will tend to perform better.
