Task: Determine Service Exposure Scope
Determine exposure scope for the services. Service exposure can occur at multiple scopes: Project, Department/Division, Line of Business, Enterprise, Ecosystem.
Disciplines: Technical Architecture
Purpose
To capture exposure scope of the services since scope impact non functional requirements of the service.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • SOA Architect
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • Architectural Decisions
  • Service Model
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Service Portfolio
      Main Description
      Designation of service exposure is not adequate to specify which parties can use the service. In addition we need to specify at what level of scope the service is being designed to be exposed. Capture the exposure scope of the exposed services. Use the exposure scopes adapted for the client or else use the pre-defined exposure scopes such as Project, Department/Division, Line of Business, Enterprise, Ecosystem.
