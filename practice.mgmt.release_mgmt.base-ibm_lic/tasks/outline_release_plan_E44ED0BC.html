Task: Outline the Release Plan
Identify initial rough scope of releases
Disciplines: Technical Management
Purpose
The Release Plan sets the scope of the project and divides the work up into manageable units. This initial sketch provides the customer and developers with an understanding of what types of function and architecture may be delivered during each release.

      Main Description
      The key use cases should be grouped and ranked according to criteria of interest to the customer and project management. Some considerations for this ranking are:
      • effective reduction of program risk such as use cases that are high risk, complex, or rely on new technologies.
      • exposure and test of assumptions concerning the project's underlying technical structure.
      • significant impact on the architecture, such as use cases that involve development of large portions of particular architectural layers.
      • demonstrate to the customer that progress is being made.
      • provide the customer with useful capability.

      The Release Plan is developed by the project manager and the sponsors.
