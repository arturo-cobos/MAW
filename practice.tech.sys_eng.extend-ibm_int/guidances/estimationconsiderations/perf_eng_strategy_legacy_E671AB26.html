Estimating Considerations

Estimates for this work product will vary, depending on whether it is a part of the Statement of Work or not, and whether it is part of the billable engagement or not.  If it is part of the Statement of Work, then minimum work may be expended using most of the generic sections of the template.  On the other hand, if this is part of a very big contract it is worthwhile investing the effort, even if it is not billable to the client. 

The following estimates are realistic of actual effort required regardless of whether it is billable to the client or not. These estimates are for the Performance Engineer effort only. The client’s time and that of any other team member is additional.

  • If there are no standards at the client organization, it would take <hh> hours (say about 40-60) of effort to put together a generic PE strategy and an additional 8 to 10 hours to review with the key client team. 
  • If other PE methods are in use, additional <hh> hours (about 20-40) of effort should be allowed to investigate, incorporate or reconcile the differences in standards between the client methods and IBM’s method, related to Performance Engineering.
