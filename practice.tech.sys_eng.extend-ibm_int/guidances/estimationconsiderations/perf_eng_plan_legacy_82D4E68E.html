Estimating Considerations

The effort for putting together a PE Plan work product should not be underestimated.  For big and complex projects (of size greater than 50000 hours) and where the business and technical risks are high, it could take as much as 0.3% of total project hours for an experienced performance engineer to organize a PE planning meeting, to run it, and to document the outcome and a PE Plan. 

For a medium/small project (of about 10000), it could take anywhere from 0.5% to 1% of total project hours. The reasons may be various like there may be some optional activities/tasks or inputs/outputs, you just need to spend less time on each of these, especially if the project involves many different applications and stakeholder groups.
