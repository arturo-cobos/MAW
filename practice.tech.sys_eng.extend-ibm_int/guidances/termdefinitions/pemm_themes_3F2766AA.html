Term Definition: PEMM Themes
Main Description

Categories of performance engineering and management activity which often cut across project phases:

  • Early Requirements and Design – PE activities during requirements and early design phases of a project
  • Estimation and Modeling – Activities associated with predicting future    performance behavior or capacity requirements of a system
  • Volumetrics – Quantitative information which has a bearing on the performance or capacity of a given solution
  • Technology Research – Investigation required to make estimation and modeling more accurate and fact-based
  • Design, Development and Tracking – PE activities in support of solution development
  • Performance Testing and Validation – PE activities in support of performance testing;
  • Live Monitoring and Capacity Management – Performance engineering and management activities in support of deployment and run
  • Risk and Performance Management – Management of performance and related risks of the solution under development
