  • IBM Global Campus Courses
  • PEDSP00B - Performance Engineering: Delivering Successful Projects This distance learning course introduces students to the basic concepts of IBM’s Performance Engineering & Management Method (PEMM) and shows how it would be applied in the context of the GS Method project. It contains both an overview path for managers and a detailed path for practitioners.
  • S9C21 (US) – Architecting for Performance This two-day classroom course builds on the material introduced in PEDSP00B (above) with a combination of lecture and team exercises. It is ideal for students who are preparing to assume performance architecture duties or who will be responsible for performance engineering duties on a major project. The course is also offered in other IBM geographies under different course numbers.
