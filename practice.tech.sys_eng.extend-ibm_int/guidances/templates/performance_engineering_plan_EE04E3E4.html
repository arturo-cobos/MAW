Main Description

The PE plan work product information includes the following:

  • Introduction: An introduction to the PE plan work product.  It describes the document, its purpose and, if appropriate, provides project background.
  • PE Strategy Overview
  • PE Overall Plan
    • Roles and Responsibilities
      During the master PE Plan development activity, all stakeholder representatives come to a consensus on, among other things, areas of organizational responsibility. This is done at a high level to identify which business or functional areas need to get involved in order to move the overall development and test planning into detailed levels of PE planning. At the master PE Plan level, it is a good practice to identify primary as well as secondary responsibility for the major PE activities.
    • Schedule of PE Activities
    • Major PE milestones
    • Resource requirements
    • Contingencies
      Standard contingencies as well as any specific ones suggested by the project and test domain risk assessments should be identified here.
  • PE Management and Reporting: The need for a structured approach to performance engineering management and reporting is emphasized here.  This will help increase awareness on the part of client management to the importance of performance engineering, so that they will be better prepared to commit their resources full time, and to participate in PE Activity
    • Reporting Procedure: The specifics of when to report and to whom belong here.
