Main Description
The PE plan should be reviewed with representatives of those enterprises as well as business or application architecture who have a role and/or stake in the delivery of a well-performing solution (e.g. client or sponsor, performance engineering, business, architecture, development, test and operations).  If a PE project definition workshop has already been held or the PE strategy document has already been reviewed, then much of the material contained in the PE plan document should already be familiar to them.  The review should be used to confirm both the understanding of and agreement to the PE plan by the organizations represented.
