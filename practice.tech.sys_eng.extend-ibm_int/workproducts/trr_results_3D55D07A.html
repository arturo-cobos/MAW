Artifact: Test Readiness Review Results (ENG 425)
The Test Readiness Review Results work product documents the results of the Test Readiness Review (TRR).
Domains: Quality Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose

The TRR objectives include:

  • Establish a Test Baseline,
  • Verify that test entrance criteria has been met for each application to be tested,
  • Verify test environment readiness,
  • Verify test data readiness,
  • Verify Test Team readiness,
    • Test Strategy, Master/Detailed Test Plan, 
    • Test Cases mapped to requirements (test traceability matrix), 
    • Test Cases, 
    • Test tools, 
    • Defect and change management, 
    • Test exit criteria, 
  • Verify that the Quality Model in the Quality Management Plan has valid defect estimates and that actual vs estimated defects are being tracked and analyzed,
  • Obtain customer and Solution Project Manager (SPM) approval to start test execution.
      
    The work product identifies the follow-up items, including the owner of defects, issues, actions or changes. The requirements to address the exceptions will vary depending upon the nature of the exceptions. Minor ones can be addressed moving forward. Significant issues may require re-planning and the re-work of key work products.   

A review scorecard is produced to quantitatively assess the review content and process.   

The purpose of the review documentation is:

  • To create the conditions that allows the review to have effective results to improve the project course,
  • To provide all the involved persons with the decisions that come out from the review,
  • To provide the evidence that the review has been conducted and that the recommendations have been taken into account.
