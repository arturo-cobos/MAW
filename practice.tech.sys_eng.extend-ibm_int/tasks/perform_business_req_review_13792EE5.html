Task: Perform the Business Requirements Review
This task describes how to review the business requirements.
Disciplines: System Engineering
Purpose
This task defines the process of performing a Business Requirements Review (BRR).
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Systems Engineering Professional
Additional Performers:
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Business Requirements Specification
    Outputs
    • Business Requirements Review Results
      Main Description
      The Business Requirements Review objectives are:
      • Review the Business Scope and Objectives.
      • Review the 'As Is' Business Process Flows (if available.
      • Review the Business Requirements (functional and nonfunctional).
      • Review the success criteria for the business.

      The review materials may be presented by either the client or the Systems Engineer. See the ‘Conducting a Business Requirements Review’ techniques paper for more information.
