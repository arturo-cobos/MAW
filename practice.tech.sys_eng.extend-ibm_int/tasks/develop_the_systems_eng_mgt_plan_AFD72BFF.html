Task: Develop the Systems Engineering Management Plan
The System Engineering Management Plan (SEMP) documents the process that the Systems Engineering team will follow for the project. It documents how the System Engineering team will tailor the process for the specific project.
Disciplines: System Engineering
Purpose
The SEMP captures the plans, processes, and procedures for the System Engineering & Architecture (SE&A) team to create and maintain the SE&A related project documents for its definition, development, test, integration, acceptance, and technology refreshment .
