    Main Description

    Business requirements describe the business' need in a tangible and measurable manner. It is a statement of what the system must do in order to successfully achieve the objective (mission) from a business point of view. It is not how the system will be implemented to perform this task.

    The business requirements are part of the customer baseline and are reviewed during the Business Requirements Review. The business requirements should include functional, nonfunctional, usability, and interface requirements.

    See the Business Requirements Specification template for assembly details.
