Task: Establish Service Component Baseline
The services component baseline describes the component model for the service components that realize exposed services. This task describes why a service component baseline must be established, and the steps to establish a service component baseline.
Disciplines: System Engineering
Purpose

Service components provide the implementation of exposed services. A service component baseline consists of the service component-level architecture, the component requirements and associated test architecture artifacts. The purpose behind baselining the service components is to achieve an enterprise service component model that can serve as an asset base for deploying services across an enterprise and facilitate the creation of service component assets.

      Main Description

      The traditional component baseline defines how the system will meet its requirements and what each of the components must do in order to aggregately meet the system requirements. In SOA, the service component baseline defines how a service and its operations are mapped to specific elements in a service component model.

      These steps are typically completed iteratively along with the application of standard component modeling techniques. When the traces are validated and the services are complete in terms of their specification in the service components, the baseline activity may start. The Systems Engineer secures component design-level agreement on the structure and composition of the service components, and baselines the components, the component requirements, the data attributes, the test architecture and the traceability aspect in the component model. Thus, the service component baseline is established.

      Steps
      Clarify Service Operations and rationalize with relevant external interface requirements
      Map Services and Services operations to service components and/or interfaces
      Define the service messages corresponding to the service operations
      Trace through the key processes or system use cases to the service operations and components, validate the trace for each aspect of the component architecture
      Review, validate and trace decomposed Non Functionals on process goals and service operations
