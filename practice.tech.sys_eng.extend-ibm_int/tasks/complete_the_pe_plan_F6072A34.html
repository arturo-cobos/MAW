Task: Complete the PE Plan
The PE plan is a document of understanding on performance engineering between various stakeholders of a project. It documents the 'what' (scope & objectives), the 'why' (purpose), the 'when' (key milestones), and the 'who' (roles & responsibilities) for performance engineering across project phases.
Disciplines: System Engineering
