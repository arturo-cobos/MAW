Task: Assemble Business Model
This task outlines the steps to establish a business model baseline. This baseline is typically established at the start of an IT transformation or implementation engagement.
Disciplines: System Engineering
Purpose

This task describes how to establish the basis for aligning the processes and resources in an enterprise, to define the scope of an IT engagement, to assist in gaining client executive agreement to the scope, and to define the future IT or system environment for the transformation.

    Main Description

    Assemble Business Model Baseline aims to collect, validate and baseline the business model of an enterprise. The constituent tasks are completed initially i.e. develop and document the business processes, business roles, rules and supporting artifacts. Once the deliverable is assembled, it is reviewed with the various stakeholders. Any gaps, conflicts or special situations are resolved and documented. This method continues iteratively until the stakeholders agree upon the final model. At this point the model is baselined along with its supporting artifacts. Thus, the business model baseline is completed.
