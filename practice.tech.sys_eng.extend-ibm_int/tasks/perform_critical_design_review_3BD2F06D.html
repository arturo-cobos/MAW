Task: Perform the Critical Design Review
This task describes how to conduct a critical design review.
Disciplines: System Engineering
Purpose
The goal of the CDR is to verify that the component designs meet the component requirements, to verify the component and e2e test plans, and to verify the necessary infrastructure needs.

      Main Description

      See the 'Conducting a Critical Design Review' technique paper for more specific details.

      Steps
      Establish component design baseline.
      Review the components' design for completeness.
      Review the components' test plans.
      Verify that the end-to-end test strategy validates system requirements.
      Verify production infrastructure capacity supports the system requirements.
      Establish traceability from component requirements to component design.
      Identify/review technical risks and mitigation plans.
