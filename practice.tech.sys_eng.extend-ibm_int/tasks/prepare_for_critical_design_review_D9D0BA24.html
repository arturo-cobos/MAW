Task: Prepare for Critical Design Review
This task describes how to formulate the guidance for Critical Design Review including setting the agenda, scheduling so that the appropriate attendees are available and developing the CDR.
Disciplines: System Engineering
Purpose
The purpose of this task is to prepare the CDR template (i.e., review materials) for the CDR (Critical Design Review).  The Critical Design Review is used to establish the design baseline.
