Task: Associate Business Rules and Policies with Service Operations
For each service specify associate documented business rules and policies are to be adhered to.
Disciplines: Technical Architecture
Purpose
To capture the business rules service operations need to implement/invoke.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Software Designer
Additional Performers:
  • Application Developer
  • Business Analyst
  • SOA Architect
InputsMandatory:
  • Service Model
  • [Business Design]
    • Current Organization Description
    • Process Definition
Optional:
  • None
Outputs
  • Service Specification
    Main Description

    For each service specify what business rules and policies are to be adhered to. Refer to the business rule artifact and associate one or more business rules with the service operations that have been started to be identified. If the business rules artifact does not exist, then do a deep dive into the functional requirements document (look into the step by step description of the main flow and each of the alternate flows) and extract the business rules and associate them to the service operations as identified.
