    Main Description

    The progression and refinement of the business notions of domains to sub-domains and functional areas was done in domain modeling and decomposition. When we enter subsystem analysis, we make a conscious decision to map a functional area to, usually, one subsystem. This mapping is the bridge from the business domain to the IT domain. Here, we identify the coarser-grained service components that will realize a subsystem. The ramification of this mapping is that the service component chosen will have the responsibility of implementing the functionality of the services and delivering the QoS of the services it will realize.
