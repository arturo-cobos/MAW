Guideline: Sample Realization Decisions By Architecture Layers
Documents realization decisions by architecture layers of the reference architecture.
Main Description

IBM SOA reference architecture has 9 layers - 5 horizontal layers and 4 cross cutting (veritical layers)

The 4 cross cutting layers represent technical and non-functional capabilities required to build a solution.


Sample architectural and realization decisions to be taken in different layers are:

Integration Layer

  • How to take a service call and messages to the end point i.e. to enable a service consumer to connect/interact with service providers?
  • How to handle service request and service response?
  • How to support communication through variety of protocols?
  • How to support variety of messaging styles such one way, pub-sub, request-response?
  • How to route messages to correct service provider?
  • How to transform protocol formats, e.g., from SOAP/HTTP to SOAP/Message Queue or SOAP/JMS?
  • How to link a variety of systems that do not directly support service-style interactions so that a variety of services can be offered in a heterogeneous environment.
  • How to store and forward messages using message queuing?
  • How to transform data formats, e.g., from proprietary to standard format or industry standards and vice versa.
  • How to aggregate (including messages and data) from different services and service providers?
  • How to propagate the events from producers to consumers?
  • How to handle transactions from the other layers, especially when a statically composed service invokes a service chain?
  • How to handle exceptions raised in the process of service invocation and message passing?
  • How to authenticate/authorize for service invocation and message routing?
  • How to capture and record message routing and service invocation history?
  • How to track and monitor the message routing and service invocation activities?

QoS Layer

  • How to assure that access to resources has been given to the right identities, at the right time, for the right purpose?
  • How to monitor and audit access to resources for unauthorized or unacceptable use?
  • How to protect unstructured and structured data from unauthorized access and data loss, according to the nature and business value of information?
  • How to monitor and audit access to information?
  • How to maintain the security status through proactive changes to the system, reacting to identified vulnerabilities and new threats, and to respond to detected incidents and reported problems
  • How to authenticate  based on proper roles?
  • How to authorize based on proper roles?
  • How to secure and encrypt service messages?

Information Layer

  • What mechanism will be used to access data and provide integration and/or replication services? Example: Information-as-a-Services, Wrapper/adapter pattern, information integration
  • How to expose data as services, to add/remove/manipulate data entries in different services or service components, to disable some data from outside access?
  • How to interface with the information layer in multiple ways such as message based, service call, batch interface?
  • How to handling representing data from various data sources in a unified data format? How to transform and map data from one format to another and align data from different resources.?
  • How to manage lifecycle of business entities?
  • How to manage hierarchy and relationship among data?
  • How to validate records against defined business rules?
  • How to notify and trigger actions based upon events detected within the data?
  • How to validate and enforce data quality rules?
  • Which pattern will be used for information integration? Example: Consolidation, Federation, Message Integration
  • How to perform extract, transform, load (ETL capabilities) data from one source to other. How to extract relevant information from sources, transforms the information into the appropriate integrated form, and loads the information into the target repository?
  • How to perform Enterprise Information Integration capabilities such as access to federated query to structure and unstructured data?
  • How to virtualize data representing actual data from the actual data repositories of various types, such as a DB2 database in the Operational System layer, or an excel file.?
  • How to handle data transformation (including transformation of data types and contents) and to aggregate data from multiple data sources.?
  • How to perform data standardization and perform data reconciliation including semantic reconciliation?
  • How to cleanse and match inbound records to existing data?
  • How to cache data in support of the data virtualization/ information services capability?
  • How to manage and maintain meta-data in common metadata repository for the enterprise?
  • How to manage to capture, aggregate, and manage unstructured content in a variety of formats such as images, text documents, web pages, spreadsheets, presentations, graphics, e-mail, video, and other multimedia?
  • How to author, configure, manage, customize and extend meta-data?
  • How to store operational and re-shaped information (structured and unstructured) that adds business value including common model of data. Used to share canonical forms (common data models) between SOA integration layer elements and also other SOA Layer elements. Typically invoked by other components (including information virtualization capabilities)?
  • How to store instance and definition of master data and historical data that records changes to master data.?
  • How to store analytical data?
  • How to handle access privileges of various participants to data?
  • How to control access on individual data items?
  • How to monitor and manage data usages using a log-like facility. Typical traceHow log includes: who has accessed the data, when, and what part of the data has been accessed?
  • How to analyze data access history and providing optimization algorithms and business intelligence for data optimization?
  • How to query and search capabilities for enterprise information?
  • How to visualize interactively the results from business analytics and data analysis?
  • How to interface with the integration layer and obtains event from the integration layer. How to analyze this event information, both in realtime/near-realtime, as well as stored (warehoused) events?
  • How to review and assess inbound service activity in the form of event information and determines responses or issues alerts/ notification?

Governance Layer

  • How to advertize and discover available services?
  • How to store and access governed artifacts and best practices for solutions based on SOA principles?
  • How to define, manage and maintain policies?
  • How to enforce policies and evaluate compliance of policies and identifying violations of policies?
  • How to measure, gather, and evaluate metrics on SOA services, governed processes and governing processes?
  • How to indicate the status of the governing and governed processes and their metrics?
  • How to capture the results of governing processes and their effectiveness?
