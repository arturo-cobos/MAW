Guideline: Life Sciences MAW
This provides a high level guidance to a Life Sciences Organization project in identifying the Method work products required to satisfy the design and delivery requirements of the specific project.
Relationships
Related Elements

Main Description

This will help enable the project team to adopt, or tailor, the Method to specific requirements and circumstances of their Life Sciences client.

The guideline provides guidance to identify the method work products, within the AS Family Engagement Model, required to satisfy and support a Life Sciences Organization project. This is used while conducting a Method Adoption Workshop for a Life Sciences Organization project. In addition to this guidance, requirements for each individual client should be considered during the MAW.

The following steps may be considered while identifying the method work products, within the AMS Family Engagement Model, required to satisfy and support a Life Sciences Organization project:

  • Use the Life Sciences MAW Guidance Spreadsheet provided in the references section to identify work products within the AS Family Engagement Models that are required for Life Sciences Organization projects
  • The listing of work products identified as required must be included, but they may be customized for specific accounts
  • Work products not included in a specific AS Family Engagement Model as identified in the Method Catalog, are not required for that model
  • Additional Method work products from engagement models other than the AMS Family Engagement Models may also be required. These will be determined with a Regulatory Compliance Subject Matter Expert (SME) and a Method Exponent (ME) during a MAW

Key Considerations

In addition to this technique paper, the following may also be used while conducting a Method Adoption Workshop to identify work products required to support a Life Sciences Organization Project:
  • OnDemand Process Asset Library (OPAL) Guidance, including:
    • Life Sciences Organization Compliance Standard
    • Life Sciences Organization Compliance Guideline
    • Life Sciences Organization Compliance Mapping
  • Additional guidance, as needed, from a Regulatory Compliance Subject Matter Expert (SME) and a Method Exponent (ME)
