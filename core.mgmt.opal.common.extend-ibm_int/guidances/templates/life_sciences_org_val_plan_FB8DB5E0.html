Template: Life Sciences Organization Validation Plan
This MS Word template is used to document the validation activities required during the system life cycle of the project as required by Life Sciences organizations, in order to comply with the industry standard and government regulations (laws and guidelines).

Main Description
This template is intended to serve as the master-planning document for the process validation required by Life Sciences organizations. It details the processes and documentation necessary to maintain a highly reliable and qualified system and its infrastructure for all relevant business purposes related to the delivered services which are the responsibility of IBM for the specified client.
