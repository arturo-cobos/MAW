Template: Activity Hand-over Form
This template is used to document project-related responsibilities to be transferred from one team member to another and is available in A4 and US formats.

Main Description

The Activity Hand-over Form is to be used while the project-related responsibilities are handed over to another team member. This Form is to be used during resignations, long term assignments, project manager responsibilities handover, etc.
The Activity Hand-over Form has four important sections. Some of the key information captured is listed below:

Part A

  • Responsibilities Assigned
  • Activities Completed (last week / month)
  • Activities Initiated but not completed
  • Activities Planned till date but not started
  • Customer Contact point
  • User Id & Passwords
  • Personal Business Commitments(PBC) Reviewed & Status
  • IDP (Reviewed & Status)

Part B

Location of important documents such as:

  • Contract Document (DOU, SOW, review & approval records)
  • All Plans - Documents, Review & approval records, Change logs (if any)
  • Project Related Documents: Weekly Status Report, Monthly Status Report, Schedule
  • Process Review / Audit Reports, LAM & RCA Reports
  • Billing (Time Sheets), Metrics Data (If required), Project specific documents/manuals, etc.
  • Customer Communication
  • Risks & Issues
  • Databases being used for Project
  • Communication with Support Groups

Part C

  • Proposal, Contract , SOW
  • Account Management
  • Billing Status

Part D

Hardware, Software and Backup Tapes related to:

  • TD Assets
  • Customer Funded
  • Customer Loaned
