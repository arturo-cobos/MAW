This template is used to plan how an organization will implement the Process, Performance and People Critical Thread Reviews.

Main Description

This MS Word template is established at the organization level, and that organizational entity may be a delivery organization, a region, a service line, a program. In all cases, the intent is to support the project manager defining the project-level communications management plan by describing how a project reports up the organization management chain. If the project is subject to matrix organization sponsorship, it may have to assess several organization's communications management plan (organizational) for P3 CTR, to determine the total extent of its reporting obligations.
