Artifact: Separation of Duties Matrix (ART 0564)
This artifact describes responsibilities by role, and provides visibility to role/responsibility combinations that should belong to separate individuals according to accepted separation of duties criteria.
Domains: Security & Privacy
Work Product Kinds: Specification
Purpose

It is used to ensure that no one staff member has responsibilities or access that could result in misuse or diversion of assets.

Description
Main Description

In instances where a separation of duties conflict is unavoidable (for example, on a small project) this artifact is used to record the secondary controls that are put in place to manage the risk associated with the separation of duties conflict.

Brief Outline

This artifact contains:

  • Instructions for use of the artifact
  • Role assignments for the team by role title, organization, and resource name
  • Potential conflict matrix by project life cycle
  • Description of secondary control detail and associated actions

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without this artifact, staff members may have the ability to misuse or divert assets without restriction or management oversight.

Reasons for not needing

If the organization has no application development, creation of this work product will not be needed.  To support the business organization reviews, the organization will need to document that this work product is not required.
