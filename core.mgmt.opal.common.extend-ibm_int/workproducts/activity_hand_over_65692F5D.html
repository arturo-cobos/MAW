Artifact: Activity Hand-over Form (ART 0704)
This artifact documents project-related responsibilities to be transferred from one team member to another team member.
Domains: Project Management
Work Product Kinds: Record
Purpose

Its purpose is to capture activities and responsibilities to be transferred to another team member in the event of a transfer, resignation or other change in a project's organizational structure.

Description
Brief Outline
This artifact typically contains:
Part A: Team Member Responsibilities and related information
  • Responsibilities
  • Activities
  • Customer Contacts
  • User IDs & Passwords
  • Personal Business Commitments (PBC) status information
  • IDP status information

Part B: Location of important documents such as
  • Contract Documents
  • Plans
  • Project Related Documents, including status reports and schedules
  • Reviews and Audit Reports
  • Billing (Timesheets) and Metrics Data (if required)
  • Project specific documents and manuals
  • Customer Communications
  • Risks & Issues
  • Project Database
  • Support Group Communications

Part C Contract or organizational level information
  • Proposal, Contract , Statement of Work (SOW)
  • Account Management
  • Billing Status

Part D Hardware, software and other assets
  • Hardware
  • Software
  • Backup Tapes
Illustrations
Templates
  • Activity Hand-over Form
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Without this artifact, there is a possibility that some detail may be missed when responsibilities are transferred from one resource to another, such as the location of an important asset. This ensures completeness of the handover.

Reasons for not needingThis artifact is not needed when the activities are turned over to a team member who is performing the same role.
