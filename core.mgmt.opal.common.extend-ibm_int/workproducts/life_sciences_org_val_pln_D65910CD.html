Artifact: Life Sciences Organization Validation Plan (ENG 432)
This artifact describes the validation activities required during the project lifecycle for a Life Sciences Organization client. It is the master planning document for validation required to comply with industry standard government regulations for a Life Sciences Organization project.
Domains: Standards & Policies
Work Product Kinds: Plan
Purpose

Its purpose is to detail the processes and documentation necessary to validate and maintain a highly reliable and qualified system (including its infrastructure) for all relevant business purposes related to the delivered services, which are the responsibility of IBM for a Life Sciences client.

Key Considerations
This artifact is reviewed with a regulatory compliance subject matter expert to ensure all compliance requirements are addressed adequately in the document. This artifact must be approved prior to the initiation of the validation activities and must contain or reference all of the relevant information that may be requested by a regulatory auditor.
Tailoring
Impact of not having

Consequences of not having this work product are:

  • Regulatory requirements governing a life sciences organization may not be addressed
  • The validation activities may be inadequate or obsolete resulting in an unreliable or less qualified system delivered to a life sciences client
Reasons for not needing

This artifact is not needed when the project is not a Life Sciences organization project.
