Task: Map Business Rules to Business Entity State Transition
Refine business rules in the context of business entity state changes
Disciplines: business_modeling.bela
Purpose
To make business rules more complete.

      Main Description

      Analyze state transitions of the key business entities and map and refine business rules as guard conditions on those state transitions. These will primarily capture rules around data consistency i.e for the Business Entity to be in some (target) state, implies that certain conditions around its data content are true. In the simplest form the rule could just be checking that certain fields are not-null in the given state. For example, for the Purchase Order to be in the Rejected state, the PurchaseOrder.rejectionReason attribute cannot be null. As the process model and the state charts are synchronized, the process perspective of this mapping can also easily be derived. The rules become pre-conditions on the processing paths (input/output port) of these entities through the process tasks.
