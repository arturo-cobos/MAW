Task: Update Conceptual Data Model based on BELA
Update the Logical Data Model by tracing the artifact through the refined process and validating info added and referred is accounted for.
Disciplines: business_modeling.bela
Purpose

To enhance logical data model with refined relationship.

        Steps
        Traverse the process model and note down other business entities
        Traverse the process model and note down other business entities that will be produced or used by the key business entity at every task.
        Add the produced business entities to the information model
        Add the produced business entities to the information model with a “contained” relationship to the key business entity or to a children in the hierarchy, creating a data graph of business entities rooted at the key business entity
        Add the used business entities as a “reference” relationship to the data graph
        Add 'current state' attribute to key business entities
