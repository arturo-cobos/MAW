Deliverable: Business Impact Assessment (DCD 113)
This deliverable provides a high level assessment of the organization’s readiness for the changes that will occur in the target organization as a result of implementing a new solution and identifies the primary issues and risks associated with the changes.
Purpose

The purpose of this document is to provide an assessment of the organization’s relative ability to adjust to the changes that will be required to successfully build and deploy the new solution. This deliverable gives the ability to:

  • Ensure the expected organization change is planned for and addressed in the later phases of the project

  • Verify the feasibility of the engagement (IBM "due diligence")
Main Description

This content of this deliverable should include:

  • A high-level assessment of the organization’s readiness to accept the changes that will be brought about by the solution. This assessment covers those organizational units that will use the solution
  • A high-level assessment of the I/T organization’s readiness to develop, deploy, and support the solution and its enabling technologies. This will address the IT organization’s capabilities to install and administer the solution
Deliverable Properties
External Description

The objective of this activity is to conduct a high level assessment of the organization’s readiness to accept the new solution and to identify organizational and cultural issues related to deploying the new solution.  The IBM team will perform the following

  • Conduct a high level assessment of the impacts the solution is expected to have on the customer’s organization and identify the relevant issues and potential barriers to change that may impact and/or shape the recommended solution.
  • Conduct a high level assessment of the customer’s IT organization’s readiness to deploy and support the recommended solution and its enabling technologies. 
  • Conduct a high level assessment of the likely effects of client requirements on the existing IT applications, infrastructure and supporting documentation.

Completion Criteria:
 
This activity will be complete when the Business Impact Assessment has been delivered to the customer or IBM has met the criteria defined in Section x.x "Completion Criteria" of this Statement of Work. 

Deliverable Parts
  • Change Readiness Assessment
  • Current Organization Assessment
  • Customer Wants and Needs
  • Process Definition

Key Considerations

This deliverable does not encompass a full change management effort; however, it should identify:

  • Organizational and cultural impacts and issues
  • The relative ability of the client to persevere through the changes required to successfully build and deploy the new system
