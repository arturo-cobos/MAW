Work Product Descriptor (Artifact): Current Organization Assessment
The Current Organization Assessment work product is an analysis of the organization's current structure and behaviors.
Based on Method Work Product: Current Organization Assessment

Main Description

This artifact identifies how, and to what extent, the organization's structure, behaviors and enablers:

  • are aligned to the business strategy and desired capabilities
  • are efficient
  • enable effective human performance (ensuring a conducive atmosphere to bring out the best in both non-disabled and disabled personnel)
  • are internally consistent and reflect industry best practice

This artifact will often contain references to the following:

  • The stated strategy, vision, and goals of the business
  • The description of the current organization
  • Groups within the organization with respect to roles
  • External benchmarks
  • Nine-cell Organization Design matrix

Key Considerations

This work product is produced through a technically complex assessment process, which requires a level of expertise consistent with the level of complexity.  You should ensure your team has this expertise, and in its absence, seek help from the Organization Change Knowledge Network.

It is imperative that you use client resources to help gather your data.
