Task: Develop IT Readiness Assessment
Determine whether the IT organization will be able to operate and maintain the solution as currently envisioned
Disciplines: Due Diligence
Purpose
Developing the IT Readiness Assessment determines the readiness of the client's IT department to deploy and support new business processes and their enabling technologies.

      Main Description
      The following goals must be met when preparing the IT Readiness Assessment Report,:
      • Describe required I/T function(s) and the skills necessary to deploy it.
      • Describe the expected roles the function will play within the organization and in other departments.
      • Inventory the personnel skills in affected departments and their understanding of the new I/T function.
      • Conduct a behavioral assessment of affected employees to anticipate reactions to changes in I/T function.
      • Perform a high level skills gap analysis to determine training needs.
