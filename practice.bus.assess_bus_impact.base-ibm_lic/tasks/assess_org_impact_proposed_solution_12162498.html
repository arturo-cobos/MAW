Task: Assess Organizational Impact of Proposed Solution
Determine what impact the envisioned solution will have on the client's organization structure and dependencies
Disciplines: Due Diligence
Purpose
Development of the Current Organization Assessment highlights the current state of the client's organization, allowing a further assessment of the impact of the proposed project on the client's organization.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Business Analyst
Additional Performers:
  • Strategy Consultant
InputsMandatory:
  • Current Organization Description
    		Optional:
      • [Project Definition and Scope]
        • Project Definition
        • Work Product List
      Outputs
      • Current Organization Assessment
        Main Description
        For those parts of the client organization that will be affected by the proposed project, assess them against the following principles:
        • How closely the organization's current goals align with the business goals and overall goals of the project
        • How efficiently the current organization can support the defined goals of the project.
        • How well the management of human performance supports the achievement of the goals of the project.
        If this assessment discovers substantial differences between the goals documented in the defined project goals and the current business goals, a business transformation project should be considered.
