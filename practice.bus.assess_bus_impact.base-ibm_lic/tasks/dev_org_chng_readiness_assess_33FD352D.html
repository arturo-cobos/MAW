Task: Develop Organization Change Readiness Assessment
Determine whether the business users involved in the solution will be able to utilize the technology and processes needed to realize the client goals
Disciplines: Due Diligence
Purpose
Developing the Organization Change Readiness Assessment determines the client's IT organization's commitment and ability to accept and sustain the changes required as a result of the proposed project.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Business Analyst
Additional Performers:
  • Strategy Consultant
InputsMandatory:
  • None
Optional:
  • Change Readiness Assessment
  • [Project Definition and Scope]
    • Project Definition
    • Work Product List
Outputs
  • Change Readiness Assessment
    Main Description
    To perform this assessment, the following need to be performed:
    • Assess the level of sponsorship.
    • Test the sponsorship durability.
    • Engage client project management.
    • Identify and engage other key stakeholders.
    • Identify and engage process team member peers.
    • Establish issues management.
    • Identify relevant issues to change: barriers and attitudes to change [rooted in norms of behavior], values and mind-sets [formed due to experiences with past implementation] and/or organization culture.
    • Review the scope of change by area in the organization.
