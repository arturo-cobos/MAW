Work Product Descriptor (Outcome): Help Desk Established
This covers the establishment of a help desk or similar infrastructure to provide on-going assistance for supporting users of a system after it has gone live.
Based on Method Work Product: Help Desk Established
Relationships
RolesResponsible: Modified By:
  • Application Developer
Output From
  • Implement Help Desk Support
Main Description
The objectives are to:
  • Provide the users with a single point of contact for all their system queries
  • Offer advice to the users to solve their problems in a timely, coordinated manner
  • Communicate with the user community the solutions to common queries
  • Direct system faults and problems to the development and upgrade teams for resolution
In most instances, the help desk will be staffed by the target ogranization.
